# Round 07 — Political

> "Starting next quarter, deploy frequency is a KPI for all engineering teams. I want to see at least 10 deploys per team per week. Teams that ship more, ship better."
— VP Amy Nakamura, all-hands announcement

What happened next:

> "Team by team, PRs started getting split. What used to be one PR with three changes became three PRs with one change each. One team literally deployed a README update to pad their numbers. Deploy frequency tripled in two weeks. Failure rate also tripled."
— Marcus Chen, platform metrics review
