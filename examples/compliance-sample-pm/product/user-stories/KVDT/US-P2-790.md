### User Story

As a **System**, the system must detect a "Card für Privatversicherte" immediately after reading and prevent its data from entering KVDT billing routines,
so that privately insured patient data does not contaminate the GKV billing process.

### Compliance Context

PKV cards must be detected and excluded from GKV billing. If PKV data enters KVDT processing, it causes invalid billing records and potential claim rejections. PKV cards are identifiable by zero-filled VKNR/IK (KVK) or specific Version_XML content in EF.StatusVD (eGK).

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Immediate PKV notice after card read**
Given a "Card für Privatversicherte" is read
When the card data is processed
Then a system-side notice immediately indicates that private insurance is present

**Scenario 2: Exclude PKV data from KVDT billing**
Given a PKV card has been read
When billing processing occurs
Then the read data from the PKV card does not flow into KVDT billing processing routines
