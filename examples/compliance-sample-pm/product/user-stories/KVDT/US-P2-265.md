### User Story

As the **System**, the system must **detect when a cost unit is not permitted in the practice's KV-Bereich and block billing and form issuance with an error message**,
so that **no claims are submitted to cost units restricted by regional contracts**.

### Compliance Context

Regional contracts between cost units and Kassenaerztliche Vereinigungen restrict certain cost units to specific KV regions. The KT-Stammdatei lists disallowed KV-Geltungsbereiche. Billing against a cost unit not permitted in the practice's region will be rejected.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Cost unit not permitted in KV-Bereich detected**
Given IK_v is present in the KT-Stammdatei
And the practice's KV-Bereich is listed under `/kostentraeger/unz_kv_geltungsbereich_liste/unz_kv_geltungsbereich/@V`
When the system processes the cost unit
Then a system-generated error message is issued that billing with this cost unit is not permitted in the practice's KV-Bereich

**Scenario 2: Billing and form issuance blocked**
Given a disallowed KV-Bereich has been detected
When the user attempts further processing
Then the software does not support direct further processing for ADT billing at the expense of this cost unit
And the software does not support the issuance of contractual physician forms or BFB forms at the expense of this cost unit
