### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche** (billing manager), I want patient master data in the inpatient and outpatient areas to be processed separately,
so that billing uses the correct IK for each domain -- KV billing uses the IK from the insurance card, while inpatient billing uses the Hauptkassen-IK.

### Compliance Context

Inpatient billing requires the Hauptkassen-IK (main insurance IK) for direct billing with the health insurer, while outpatient KV billing uses the IK interpreted from the insurance card per the Mappingtabelle KVK. If these domains are not separated, the wrong IK could be used, causing billing to be routed to the wrong entity and resulting in rejected claims or incorrect payments.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Separate processing of patient data** [derived]
Given a patient is treated in both inpatient and outpatient areas
When the insurance card is read in each area
Then the patient master data is processed separately for inpatient and outpatient areas

**Scenario 2: Re-read insurance card for outpatient after inpatient** [derived]
Given a patient was treated in the inpatient area and their card was read there
When the same patient is later treated in the outpatient area
Then the insurance card must be re-read and the data processed separately for outpatient billing
