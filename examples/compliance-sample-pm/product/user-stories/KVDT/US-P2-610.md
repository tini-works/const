### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want to assign justification texts to fee schedule numbers and have material costs handled with correct Pseudo-GNRs,
so that billing justifications are properly structured and KV-specific rules for material cost transmission are met.

### Compliance Context

Justification texts must be assignable to any GNR and transmitted under the correct field identifier. Material costs (FK 5012) require specific Pseudo-GNR handling per KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei rules. Non-compliance leads to billing rejections for unjustified services or incorrectly formatted material costs.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Assign justification texts to GNRs**
Given the user documents a service
When justification is needed for a fee schedule number
Then one or more justification texts can be assigned to any GNR
AND texts are transmitted under the respectively prescribed field identifier
AND the system allows the user to differentiate between justification types
AND all justification texts are stored "behind" the corresponding GNR

**Scenario 2: Pseudo-GNR for material costs without GNR**
Given a treatment day (FK 5000) contains no GNR (FK 5001) but has material costs (FK 5012)
When the billing file is generated
Then FK 5012 is preceded by the Pseudo-GNR "88999" (FK 5001)
AND if a deviating Pseudo-GNR is defined under field identifiers 9410/9411 of the KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei, that value is used instead

**Scenario 3: KV-specific Pseudo-GNR rule not followed — warning or auto-insert**
Given the KV-specific rule from the KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei regarding Pseudo-GNR for material costs is not observed
When the user submits the billing
Then either a warning is issued using data from field identifiers 9410/9411
OR the Pseudo-GNR is automatically transmitted (if a sole Pseudo-GNR is stored under field identifier 9410)
AND this does not apply when FK 5012 is preceded by a GNR matching one in the EBM-Stammdatei under `../gnr_zusatzangaben/gnr_zusatzangaben_liste/gnr_zusatzangabe/@V="5012"`
