### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want to display the meaning of DMP-Kennzeichen values for FK 4132,
so that I can correctly identify DMPs in which an insured person may be enrolled.

### Compliance Context

DMP-Kennzeichen values encode specific disease management programs. Without access to their meanings, users risk selecting incorrect values, leading to billing errors and potentially misattributing patient enrollment status. All permitted values and meanings are published under [S_KBV_DMP].

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Display DMP-Kennzeichen meaning**
Given the user is viewing or editing the DMP-Kennzeichen (FK 4132)
When the user requests to display the meaning of a DMP-Kennzeichen value
Then the software displays the corresponding meaning as published under [S_KBV_DMP]
