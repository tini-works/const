### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **to record the start and end dates of an interruption of an ongoing approved psychotherapy, including the presence of a justification to the health insurance fund**,
so that **the interruption is properly documented per the Psychotherapy Agreement and downstream reminder functions work correctly**.

### Compliance Context

Section 10 para. 6 and 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement (Annex 1 BMV-Ae) govern the documentation of therapy interruptions. Without proper interruption recording, the system cannot correctly suppress or trigger termination notification reminders, leading to incorrect billing workflows.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Recording the start date of a psychotherapy interruption**
Given an ongoing approved psychotherapy exists
When the user initiates recording of an interruption
Then the software must offer the ability to record the start date of the interruption
And the ability to record the presence of an informal justification per section 17 para. 6 Psychotherapy Agreement
And store the information in the system

**Scenario 2: Recording the end date of a psychotherapy interruption**
Given an ongoing approved psychotherapy has a recorded interruption
When the user records the end of the interruption
Then the software must offer the ability to record the end date of the interruption
And store the information in the system
