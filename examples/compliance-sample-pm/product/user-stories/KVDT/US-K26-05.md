### User Story

As a **System**, the system must use exclusively the KIM (Kommunikation im Medizinwesen) service of the Telematikinfrastruktur for sending and receiving digital Form 6,
so that electronic referrals are transmitted only through the approved secure channel per BMV-Ae Annex 2b.

### Compliance Context

The BMV-Ae Annex 2b section 3 number 1 restricts digital form transmission to channels fulfilling specific security requirements. KIM is the only approved secure transmission channel for digital Form 6 in doctor-to-doctor communication. Using any other channel would violate the regulatory framework and compromise patient data security.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Use KIM for sending and receiving Form 6**
Given the software sends or receives digital Form 6
When the transmission channel is selected
Then the system uses the specialized service KIM of the Telematikinfrastruktur
AND the form is transmitted in PDF/A standard
