### User Story

As a **System**, the system must automatically manage statutorily insured patients under 18 years of age as "gebuehrenfrei" (fee-exempt), except for patient transport (Muster 4),
so that children and adolescents are not incorrectly charged co-payments in compliance with their legal exemption.

### Compliance Context

Children and adolescents under 18 have no co-payment obligation under statutory health insurance, except for transportation costs. The automatic exemption must end at the beginning of the quarter in which the patient turns 18. For Muster 4 (patient transport), the user must explicitly indicate exemption status. The system must inform the user when a patient transitions into co-payment obligation.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Automatic fee exemption for patients under 18**
Given a patient is under 18 years of age and statutorily insured (VKNR 3rd-5th digit < 800)
When the patient is managed in the PVS
Then the patient is automatically managed as "gebuehrenfrei"
AND the automatic exemption ends at the beginning of the quarter in which the patient completes their 18th year of life

**Scenario 2: Inform user of transition to co-payment obligation**
Given a patient is transitioning from under-18 to co-payment-obligated status
When the patient enters co-payment obligation
Then the user is informed by the PVS about the change

**Scenario 3: Explicit exemption indication for Muster 4**
Given a Muster 4 (patient transport) prescription is being issued
When the user prepares the form
Then the user must explicitly indicate whether a co-payment exemption exists or does not exist for the patient
