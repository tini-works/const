### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt** (contract physician), I want to partially deviate from the standard formulations on patient receipts by editing explanatory texts and headings,
so that I can tailor the patient receipt content to my practice needs while remaining compliant.

### Compliance Context

The Patientenquittung (patient receipt) has standard formulations, but physicians are permitted to partially deviate from them if the PVS provides editing functionality. This flexibility allows physicians to adapt receipts to their specific practice context without violating the regulatory framework.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Edit explanatory texts and headings on patient receipt** [derived]
Given the physician is preparing a patient receipt
When the physician wants to modify standard formulations
Then the PVS provides functionality to edit explanatory texts and headings

**Scenario 2: Preserve compliance while allowing deviation** [derived]
Given the physician has modified standard formulations
When the patient receipt is generated
Then the modified content is used in place of the standard formulations
