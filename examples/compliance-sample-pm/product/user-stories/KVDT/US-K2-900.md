### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt** (contract physician), I want the system to support billing of simultaneous procedures by suggesting the highest-valued service and calculating time surcharges from the GSNZ,
so that simultaneous procedures are billed correctly per EBM specifications.

### Compliance Context

EBM specifications require that for simultaneous procedures, only the highest-valued service is billed, with additional procedures accounted for through GSNZ (Total Suture Time) and time surcharges. Both pieces of information -- GSNZ (FK 5037) and surcharge services -- must be transmitted. Incorrect billing of simultaneous procedures risks reimbursement clawbacks.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Suggest highest-valued service** [derived]
Given multiple simultaneous procedures are documented
When the physician prepares the billing
Then the PVS offers the highest-valued service for billing as support

**Scenario 2: Calculate time surcharges from GSNZ** [derived]
Given the highest-valued service has been selected for billing
When time surcharges are calculated
Then the surcharges are derived from the difference between the GSNZ and the actual SNZ of the main procedure

**Scenario 3: Transmit both GSNZ and surcharge services** [derived]
Given simultaneous procedures are being billed
When the billing data is transmitted
Then both the GSNZ (FK 5037) and the surcharge service(s) are included in the transmission
