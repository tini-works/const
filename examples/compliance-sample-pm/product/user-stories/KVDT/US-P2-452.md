### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want to correctly assign Bundeswehr treatment cases to the appropriate cost unit and capture the required additional data,
so that billing for Bundeswehr patients complies with the Vertrag Bundeswehr/KBV per § 75 Abs. 3 SGB V.

### Compliance Context

Bundeswehr patient billing requires precise assignment to one of two cost units depending on the referral form type, plus capture of the Personenkennziffer in a defined format. Incorrect assignment or missing data leads to rejected claims under the Bundeswehr/KBV contract.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Manual cost unit assignment by treatment form type**
Given a Bundeswehr patient presents with a referral form
When the user assigns the cost unit
Then for Überweisungsschein für Überweisungsaufträge der Bundeswehr (Vordruck San/Bw/0217), the cost unit VKNR 79868 (Kurzname: BUNDESWEHR) is used
AND for Überweisungsschein zur Feststellung der Wehrdienstfähigkeit (Vordruck San/Bw/0117), the cost unit VKNR 79869 (Kurzname: BUNDESWEHR MUSTERG) is used

**Scenario 2: Capture Personenkennziffer**
Given a Bundeswehr patient billing case is created
When the user enters the Personenkennziffer
Then it is captured as SKT-Zusatzangabe under FK 4124 per KV-Spezifika (kvx3) settings
AND the format conforms to "TTMMJJannnnn"

**Scenario 3: Deviating Gültigkeitsdauer**
Given the KV-Spezifika (kvx3) settings require a deviating Gültigkeitsdauer
When the user captures the Bundeswehr case
Then a deviating Gültigkeitsdauer can be captured and transmitted under FK 4125
