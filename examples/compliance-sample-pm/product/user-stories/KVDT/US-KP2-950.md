### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want **to record and transmit Pseudo treatment cases with GOP 88194 for selective contract / mine-physician treatment cases within ADT billing**,
so that **the prerequisite for billing GOPs 03060, 03062, and 03063 (NäPA services) is fulfilled per EBM Preamble No. 11 section 3.1**.

### Compliance Context

Billing of GOPs 03060, 03062, and 03063 requires verification of all selective contract/mine-physician treatment cases using GOP 88194. Without the Pseudo treatment case, the practice cannot meet the prerequisite for billing these NäPA-related services.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Pseudo treatment case recording and transmission**
Given a selective contract / mine-physician treatment case is billed directly with the cost carrier
When the user creates the ADT billing for the current quarter
Then the software enables recording and transfer of an additional "Pseudo treatment case" as a further data record 010x within the ADT billing
