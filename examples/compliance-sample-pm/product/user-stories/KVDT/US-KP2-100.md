### User Story

As a **Praxisadministrator** (practice administrator), I want all terminals to be connectable to any billing system via at least one standard interface (RS232, LAN, USB),
so that card readers can be integrated with the PVS regardless of the specific billing system in use.

### Compliance Context

Interoperability between card terminals and billing systems is essential for the contract physician workflow. Terminals must support at least one standard interface to ensure they can be connected to any PVS. This requirement applies to systems with Arzt-Patienten-Kontakt (APK). Without this, practices could face hardware lock-in and inability to read insurance cards.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Terminal connectivity via standard interface** [derived]
Given a mobile or stationary terminal is deployed in the practice
When the terminal is connected to the billing system
Then it is connectable via at least one interface: RS232, LAN, or USB
