### User Story

As a **System**, The system must restrict ADT billing to only the pseudo subgroups and corresponding billing areas defined in record type "kvx2",
so that only valid pseudo subgroup and billing area combinations are used in the billing file.

### Compliance Context

Pseudo subgroups and their associated billing areas are strictly defined by the KV-Specifika master data. Using unauthorized combinations would cause validation errors in the KVDT file, leading to billing rejections at the KV level.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Only permitted pseudo subgroups used** [derived]
Given an ADT billing is being generated
When pseudo subgroups are assigned
Then only the pseudo subgroups listed under field identifier 4239 of record type "kvx2" are used

**Scenario 2: Billing areas match pseudo subgroups** [derived]
Given a pseudo subgroup has been assigned
When billing areas are associated
Then only the billing areas listed under field identifier 4122 of record type "kvx2" are used with the corresponding pseudo subgroup
