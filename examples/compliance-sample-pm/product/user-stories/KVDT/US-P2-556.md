### User Story

As a **System**, the system must automatically create a new "010x" data record when a patient requires an additional Abrechnungsgebiet marking within the current quarter,
so that billed services are correctly grouped for separate remuneration per contractual or legal requirements.

### Compliance Context

The Abrechnungsgebiet (FK 4122) marking ensures that services are grouped by specific contractual or legal requirements for separate remuneration or invoicing. Without automatic record creation, services from different billing areas would be merged, causing incorrect billing.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: New 010x record for additional Abrechnungsgebiet**
Given a patient in the current quarter requires a marking with an additional Abrechnungsgebiet (FK 4122)
When the additional billing area is identified
Then the software generates an additional "010x" data record

**Scenario 2: Update Einlesedatum on card re-read across billing areas**
Given the insurance card is re-read
When the patient has multiple 010x data records with different Abrechnungsgebiet in the current quarter
Then the existing Einlesedatum in all affected 010x data records is updated (see P2-150)
