### User Story

As a **System**, the system must sort treatment days in ascending order within a 010x record and group all fee schedule numbers under their respective service day,
so that the KVDT billing file structure conforms to the required ordering rules.

### Compliance Context

KVDT mandates a specific ordering of treatment days and fee schedule numbers within 010x records. Incorrect ordering causes parsing errors and billing rejections by the KV processing systems.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Ascending order of treatment days [derived]**
Given a 010x record contains multiple treatment days
When the billing file is generated
Then all treatment days are sorted in ascending order

**Scenario 2: GNRs grouped under service day [derived]**
Given a treatment day (FK 5000) has multiple fee schedule numbers
When the billing file is generated
Then all fee schedule numbers of that treatment day are ordered after the service day, each as a separate field under FK 5001
