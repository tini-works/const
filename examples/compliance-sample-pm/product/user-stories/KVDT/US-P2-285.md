### User Story

As the **System**, the system must **detect when a KT-Abrechnungsbereich has expired for a cost unit and block billing and form issuance with that KTAB via an error message**,
so that **no claims are submitted under an invalid contractual billing area**.

### Compliance Context

The KT-Abrechnungsbereich specifies the contractual basis for billing. Contractual regulations between cost units and KVs define validity periods. Billing under an expired KTAB will be rejected because the underlying contractual agreement is no longer in force.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Expired KT-Abrechnungsbereich detected**
Given IK_v is present in the KT-Stammdatei
And KTAB_y is assignable to the patient
And LQ lies above the validity period of `/kostentraeger/kt_abrechnungsbereich_liste/kt_abrechnungsbereich/gueltigkeit/@V`
When the system processes the cost unit
Then a system-generated error message is issued that the KT-Abrechnungsbereich is no longer valid

**Scenario 2: Billing and form issuance blocked for expired KTAB**
Given an expired KT-Abrechnungsbereich has been detected
When the user attempts further processing
Then the software does not support direct further processing for ADT billing with this KTAB
And the software does not support the issuance of contractual physician forms or BFB forms with this KTAB
