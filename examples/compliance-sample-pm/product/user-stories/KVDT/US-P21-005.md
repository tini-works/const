### User Story

As a **Praxisadministrator**, I want to create and manage ASV team numbers in the facility master data,
so that each contract physician can be associated with their ASV team(s) for correct billing.

### Compliance Context

The contractual basis is section 6, para. 3 of the ASV-AV in conjunction with section 116b, para. 6, sentence 12 SGB V. Contract physicians can belong to multiple ASV teams, and multiple physicians in a facility may participate. Without proper ASV team number management, ASV services cannot be correctly attributed and billed.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Creation and management of ASV team numbers**
Given the user needs to configure ASV team membership
When the user accesses facility master data
Then the software enables each user to create and manage their ASV team number(s)

**Scenario 2: ASV team numbers transmitted in billing**
Given ASV team numbers have been configured
When the billing file is generated
Then the software transfers the ASV team number(s) in field FK 0222 (ASV team number) in the "Facility data record" ("besa") of the KVDT file
