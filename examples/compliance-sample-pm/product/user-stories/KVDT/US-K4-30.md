### User Story

As a **System**, the system must derive the VKNR from the IK contained in the Identifier-SA (digits 9-15) per the provisions of Kapitel 2.2.2.1,
so that the correct cost carrier assignment is established for pregnancy termination billing.

### Compliance Context

In pregnancy termination billing, the VKNR cannot be read directly from an insurance card due to anonymization. Instead, it must be derived from the IK embedded in the Identifier-SA. Incorrect VKNR derivation would route billing to the wrong cost carrier, resulting in rejected claims.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Derive VKNR from IK in Identifier-SA** [derived]
Given the user enters an Identifier-SA for pregnancy termination billing
When the system processes the identifier
Then the VKNR is derived from the IK in digits 9-15 of the Identifier-SA
AND the derivation follows the provisions per Kapitel 2.2.2.1
