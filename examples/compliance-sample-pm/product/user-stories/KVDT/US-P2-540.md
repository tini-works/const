### User Story

As a **System**, the system must automatically create a new "010x" data record when a patient's Versichertenart changes within the current quarter,
so that the status change is documented and billing uses the correct Versichertenart per § 21 Absatz 1 Satz 4 BMV-Ae.

### Compliance Context

Per § 21 Absatz 1 Satz 4 BMV-Ae, when the Versichertenart changes mid-quarter, the Versichertenart at the beginning of the quarter is used for billing. A data record separation is needed to document the change. Without this, the status change goes undocumented and billing may use incorrect insurance status.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: New 010x record on Versichertenart change**
Given the Versichertenart (FK 3108) of a patient changes in the current quarter
When the change is detected
Then the software generates an additional "010x" data record

**Scenario 2: Preserve existing Einlesedatum**
Given a new 010x data record is created due to the change of Versichertenart
When the new record is generated
Then no changes to the already existing Einlesedatum (FK 4109) of previous records take place
AND the Einlesedatum is updated only for the newest data record
AND the Einlesedatum is stored and transmitted "true to data record" (see P2-150)
