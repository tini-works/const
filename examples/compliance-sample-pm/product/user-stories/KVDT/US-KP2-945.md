### User Story

As a **Praxisadministrator**, I want **to access the PDF template for the "Patient Declaration European Health Insurance" directly from the software**,
so that **I can retrieve and print the form as needed for patients with European health insurance**.

### Compliance Context

The Patient Declaration European Health Insurance form is required for treating patients insured under European health insurance schemes. Direct access from the software ensures the form is readily available when needed at the point of care.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: PDF template accessible from software**
Given the user needs the Patient Declaration European Health Insurance form
When the user accesses the form within the software
Then the PDF template [EXT_ITA_AHEX_Erklaerung_EHIC_PEB] is available directly from the software
