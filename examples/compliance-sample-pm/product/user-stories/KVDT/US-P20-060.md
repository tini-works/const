### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want a certificate overview statistic showing the ring trial certification status of all material-analyte combinations,
so that I can monitor compliance with ring trial requirements at a glance.

### Compliance Context

The KVDT requires a controlling function that gives practitioners visibility into their ring trial certificate coverage. Without this overview, a facility cannot assess whether it meets the ring trial participation requirements, risking non-compliant laboratory billing.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Certificate overview displays all categories** [derived]
Given the user accesses the certificate overview
When the statistic is displayed at a prominent location (e.g., in connection with billing statistics)
Then the overview shows:
And facility-individual material-analyte combinations for which a certificate is present
And facility-individual material-analyte combinations for which no certificate is present
And facility-individual material-analyte combinations exclusively examined within pnSD/uu
And other material-analyte combinations obviously not examined at the facility
