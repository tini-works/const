### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want to optionally specify a quota for non-reimbursed services that is reflected on the patient receipt,
so that the patient is informed about the expected reimbursement reduction due to limited health insurance fund resources.

### Compliance Context

Due to budgetary limitations, physicians typically receive compensation for only a portion of rendered services. The patient receipt must transparently communicate this to the patient. The quota can be specified based on empirical values from recent quarters, or a standard text is used if no quota is given.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Standard text when no quota specified [derived]**
Given the physician has not specified a quota
When the patient receipt is generated
Then the standard text is displayed: "The amount stated below for the medical services I have rendered will, due to the limitation of the health insurance funds' financial resources, potentially only be partially paid to me. The payment will subsequently be reduced by the health insurance fund to the extent that the funds made available by your health insurance fund are sufficient."

**Scenario 2: Quota-specific text when quota is specified [derived]**
Given the physician specifies a quota (e.g. 5%)
When the patient receipt is generated
Then the text includes the quota percentage: "...The payment will subsequently be reduced by the health insurance fund by X %, so that the funds made available by your health insurance fund are sufficient."
AND the quota value is incorporated into the fee calculation
