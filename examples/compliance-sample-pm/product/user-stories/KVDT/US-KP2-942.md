### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **to explicitly specify whether my share of a combination treatment by two psychotherapists is an individual therapy component (FK 4251 = 3) or group therapy component (FK 4251 = 4)**,
so that **the combination treatment is correctly classified per the Psychotherapy Agreement (Anlage 1 BMV-Ae) and Form PTV 2**.

### Compliance Context

The amended Psychotherapy Agreement and revised Form PTV 2 require explicit specification of the therapy component type in combination treatments. Without this distinction, the billing cannot correctly represent the practitioner's share of the treatment.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Explicit specification of combination treatment component**
Given the user is documenting a combination treatment by two psychotherapists
When the user records their share
Then the user must explicitly indicate whether it involves:
  - "Individual therapy component in treatment by two psychotherapists" (FK 4251 = 3), or
  - "Group therapy component in treatment by two psychotherapists" (FK 4251 = 4)
