### User Story

As a **Praxisadministrator**, I want **to record at least one device type and manufacturer for pnSD/uu when usage is confirmed, with support for multiple devices and permanently stored, editable data**,
so that **the practice's diagnostic device inventory is documented for billing compliance**.

### Compliance Context

When pnSD/uu usage is confirmed under KP20-030, the practice must register its diagnostic devices. Without this data, the RVSA record is incomplete and the practice cannot demonstrate compliance with pnSD/uu documentation requirements during KV audits.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Mandatory recording of device type and manufacturer** [derived]
Given the use of pnSD/uu is confirmed under KP20-030
When the administrator accesses the device configuration
Then at least one device type and manufacturer must be recorded
And the specification of multiple devices must be supported
And the data must be permanently stored and editable

**Scenario 2: Enforcement of at least one device entry** [derived]
Given the use of pnSD/uu is confirmed under KP20-030
When the administrator attempts to save without any device entry
Then the system must prevent saving and prompt for at least one device type and manufacturer
