### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **to access the PDF template for Form PTV 10 directly from the software**,
so that **I can retrieve and print the psychotherapy form as needed**.

### Compliance Context

Form PTV 10 is a required psychotherapy form that must be accessible from the practice software. Direct access ensures timely availability for clinical workflows.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: PTV 10 PDF template accessible**
Given the user needs Form PTV 10
When the user accesses the form within the software
Then the PDF template [EXT_ITA_AHEX_PTV10] is available directly from the software
