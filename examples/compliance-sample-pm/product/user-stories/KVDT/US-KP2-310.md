### User Story

As a **Praxisadministrator** (practice administrator), I want the system to notify me immediately when a patient's insurance has changed during card reading, showing field-specific differences,
so that I am aware of the insurance switch and can review changes before existing data is overwritten.

### Compliance Context

When a patient switches health insurers, their insurance card data changes. The system must alert the user to the switch immediately after reading the new card, displaying field-by-field differences per [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Mapping_KVK]. Without this notification, existing patient data could be unknowingly overwritten during the comparison routine, leading to data loss and billing errors.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Notify user of insurance switch with field-specific differences**
Given a patient already present in the system has switched insurance
When the new insurance card is read
Then the user is notified of the insurance switch through a warning
AND each individual field (per [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Mapping_KVK]) of the insurance card is compared with existing PVS data
AND data deviations are displayed field-specifically in the master data screen
