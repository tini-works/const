### User Story

As a **System**, the system must ensure that a LANR per (N)BSNR is transmitted only once in ADT, SADT, and KADT billing,
so that duplicate LANR entries do not cause billing rejections by the KVen.

### Compliance Context

If a LANR per (N)BSNR is transmitted multiple times (e.g. with different physician names), KVen may reject the entire billing file. This uniqueness constraint applies only to the besa data record transmission, not to internal master data maintenance.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: LANR uniqueness per BSNR in besa data record**
Given multiple physicians are managed in the system
When ADT, SADT, or KADT billing is generated
Then the LANR (FK 0212) occurs only once per BSNR (FK 0201) in the field pairs 0201/0212 of the besa data record
