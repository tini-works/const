### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want the patient receipt to list only my own billed services valued in the SDEBM, with the ability to exclude services for minors,
so that the receipt is accurate, compliant with § 305 SGB V, and protects child/adolescent welfare when needed.

### Compliance Context

The patient receipt per § 305 (2) SGB V must only list the physician's own services that are contained and valued in the SDEBM. Commissioned services are excluded. For patients under 15, services may be excluded for therapeutic or welfare reasons per KBV guideline analogy, without noting incompleteness on the receipt.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: List only own billed services from SDEBM**
Given a patient receipt is generated
When services are listed
Then only services the physician applies for own billing are included
AND only services contained and valued in the SDEBM are printed

**Scenario 2: Exclude commissioned services**
Given the physician has commissioned services (e.g. via Muster 10A)
When the patient receipt is generated
Then commissioned services are not listed

**Scenario 3: Multiplier for repeated services**
Given services were rendered multiple times
When the patient receipt is generated
Then repeated services can be identified by a multiplier for the fee schedule number

**Scenario 4: Exclude services for minors**
Given the patient has not yet completed the 15th year of life
When the user reviews the receipt
Then the user can exclude individual services from the patient receipt
AND no notice about incompleteness of the service listing is displayed on the receipt
