### User Story

As an **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want to capture and transmit manufacturer/supplier name (FK 5074) and article/model number (FK 5075) for material costs (FK 5012),
so that material cost details are properly documented in the ADT billing as required by the ASV billing agreement.

### Compliance Context

The ASV billing agreement originally required these material cost details for costs incurred up to 31.12.2019. From 01.01.2020, they became voluntary for ASV billing, and from 01.04.2021 they can be populated without ASV context. The fields enhance transparency of material cost billing.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Manufacturer/supplier and article/model fields available for material costs**
Given the user is documenting material costs in the ADT billing
When material costs exist in field FK 5012
Then the user has the ability to specify "Manufacturer/Supplier Name" in field FK 5074 and "Article/Model Number" in field FK 5075

**Scenario 2: Material cost details transmitted in billing**
Given the user has populated fields FK 5074 and FK 5075
When the ADT billing file is generated
Then the fields FK 5074 (Manufacturer/Supplier Name) and FK 5075 (Article/Model Number) are transmitted alongside material costs (FK 5012) and material cost description (FK 5011) according to the KVDT data record description
