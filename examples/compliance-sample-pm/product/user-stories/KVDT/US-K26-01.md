### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt** (contract physician), I want the software to implement all requirements for electronic referral using digital Form 6 in doctor-to-doctor communication,
so that I can send and receive electronic referrals in compliance with the bundled specification.

### Compliance Context

The electronic referral via digital Form 6 in doctor-to-doctor communication is a bundle of multiple requirements (K26-02 through K26-08). Implementation is voluntary, but if the software manufacturer decides to implement it, all sub-requirements must be fulfilled. Partial implementation would create interoperability issues in electronic referral workflows.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Fulfill all bundled requirements**
Given the software manufacturer has decided to implement digital Form 6
When the software is evaluated for compliance
Then the software fulfills all of the following requirements:
AND [K26-02](../../compliances/KVDT/K26-02.md) — Automated population
AND [K26-03](../../compliances/KVDT/K26-03.md) — Creation of digital Form 6
AND [K26-04](../../compliances/KVDT/K26-04.md) — Sending digital Form 6
AND [K26-05](../../compliances/KVDT/K26-05.md) — Secure transmission channel
AND [K26-06](../../compliances/KVDT/K26-06.md) — Reception of digital Form 6
AND [K26-07](../../compliances/KVDT/K26-07.md) — Reading received Form 6
AND [K26-08](../../compliances/KVDT/K26-08.md) — Processing read data
