### User Story

As a **Patient**, I want a uniformly designed patient receipt with all required information and layout specifications,
so that I can clearly understand the medical services rendered and the estimated costs per § 305 (2) SGB V.

### Compliance Context

A uniform patient receipt design is mandated to ensure patients receive clear, comparable information about services and costs. The receipt must follow DIN A4 format, monospace font, specific text blocks for quotation, and a defined tabular service listing. Non-compliance affects patient transparency rights.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Paper format and typography [derived]**
Given a patient receipt is generated
When the receipt is rendered
Then it uses DIN A4 paper format, monospace font at 12 CPI, single-spaced line spacing

**Scenario 2: Patient address per DIN 5008**
Given a patient receipt is generated
When the address field is rendered
Then the structure and position of the patient address follows DIN 5008

**Scenario 3: Required content blocks present**
Given a patient receipt is generated
When the content is rendered
Then it includes: health insurance name, insured number, sender (practice address), issue date, subject ("Service and cost information"), explanatory text, quotation text (standard or with quota), treatment period, point value (per P2-830), service listing in tabular form, summary line with costs in EUR, reimbursement calculation (K * (100% - X)), footnotes, and continuous page numbering
AND continuation pages include name and issue date in header

**Scenario 4: Service listing in tabular form**
Given services are listed on the receipt
When the table is rendered
Then it includes columns: Day / GNR / Short description / Points / Fee
AND for EBM services without points in the GO-Stammdatei, the Points column entry can be omitted or replaced by "-"
