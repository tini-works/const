### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want to mark treatment cases for quarterly receipt dispatch and batch-print all marked receipts at quarter-end,
so that patients who agree to quarterly receipts receive them efficiently per § 305 Abs. 2 SGB V.

### Compliance Context

Patients may request quarterly receipts per § 305 Abs. 2 SGB V. The software must support marking cases for dispatch and enable automated sequential printout at quarter-end to reduce administrative burden. Practices may also collect the statutory expense allowance of 1 Euro plus shipping costs.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Mark treatment case for receipt dispatch [derived]**
Given the patient agrees with the dispatch of a quarterly receipt
When the user processes the treatment case
Then the software provides the ability to mark the treatment case for receipt dispatch

**Scenario 2: Batch print marked quarterly receipts [derived]**
Given multiple treatment cases are marked for quarterly receipt dispatch
When the quarter ends and the user initiates batch printing
Then the software sequentially prints all marked quarterly receipts in an automated manner
