### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **the system to notify me about Pseudo-GOP 88131 when an approved psychotherapy has remaining contingent, services were recorded this quarter, and relapse prevention may follow**,
so that **I can submit the termination notification at the appropriate time and enable subsequent relapse prevention**.

### Compliance Context

Section 17 para. 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement (Annex 1 BMV-Ae) requires timely termination notifications when relapse prevention is a possible next step. Missing this notification may delay or invalidate relapse prevention billing. The Psychotherapy Guideline defines criteria for determining whether relapse prevention can follow.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Notification during billing creation for possible relapse prevention**
Given an approved psychotherapy with remaining contingent exists
And the treatment duration amounts to at least 40 therapy units
And approved psychotherapy services have been recorded in the billing quarter
And no Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 has been recorded
And the psychotherapy is not interrupted
When billing creation is initiated
Then the software must notify the user about the possible recording and transfer of a "Termination notification with Pseudo-GOP 88131"

**Scenario 2: Additional criteria may gate notification**
Given the conditions for Scenario 1 are partially met
When the software evaluates the notification criteria
Then the software may make the notification dependent on additional criteria (e.g., patient indication for relapse prevention, treatment duration per Psychotherapy Guideline section 14(3), age of insured, mental disability, remaining contingent amount)

**Scenario 3: Transfer of Pseudo-GOP in ADT billing**
Given the user has specified Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 after notification
When the ADT billing is generated
Then the software transfers the specified Pseudo-GOP in field 5001
