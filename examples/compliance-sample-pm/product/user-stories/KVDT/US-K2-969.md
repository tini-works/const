### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt** (contract physician), I want to create and print lists of approved psychotherapies that are missing termination notifications with Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131,
so that I can track and follow up on psychotherapies requiring termination documentation.

### Compliance Context

Approved psychotherapies require termination notifications (Beendigungsmitteilungen) documented via Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131. Without tracking which therapies are missing these notifications, physicians risk non-compliance with documentation requirements and may face billing issues.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Create and print list of missing termination notifications**
Given approved psychotherapies exist with missing termination notifications
When the user requests a list of psychotherapies with missing Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131
Then the software creates and prints the list analogous to acceptance criterion (1) of functions [KP2-965](../../compliances/KVDT/KP2-965.md), [KP2-966](../../compliances/KVDT/KP2-966.md), [KP2-967](../../compliances/KVDT/KP2-967.md), [KP2-968](../../compliances/KVDT/KP2-968.md), [KP2-970](../../compliances/KVDT/KP2-970.md), and [KP2-971](../../compliances/KVDT/KP2-971.md)
