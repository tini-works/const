### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche** (billing manager), I want the system to enforce that supervisory services from Chapter 31.4 are billed only once within 21 days and require the OP date under FK 5034,
so that supervisory service billing complies with EBM rules and billing justification is properly documented.

### Compliance Context

Supervisory services from EBM Chapter 31.4 have strict billing constraints: they may only be billed once within a 21-day period, and the OP date must be specified under FK 5034 as billing justification. Violating these rules results in rejected claims and potential recourse.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Enforce 21-day billing limit** [derived]
Given a supervisory service from Chapter 31.4 has been billed for a patient
When the user attempts to bill another supervisory service within 21 days
Then the system prevents or warns about the duplicate billing

**Scenario 2: Require OP date as billing justification** [derived]
Given a supervisory service from Chapter 31.4 is being billed
When the billing data is prepared
Then the OP date is specified under FK 5034 as billing justification
