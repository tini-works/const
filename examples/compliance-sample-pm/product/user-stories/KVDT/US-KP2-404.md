### User Story

As a **Praxisadministrator**, I want **the system to receive electronic Ersatzbescheinigungen (eEB) from health insurers via KIM**,
so that **insured persons who cannot present an eGK can still be registered with verified insurance data per § 291 Abs. 9 SGB V**.

### Compliance Context

§ 291 Abs. 9 SGB V allows insured persons without an eGK to request proof of benefit entitlement electronically via their health insurer's app. The eEB is transmitted as a FHIR dataset via KIM to the practice. Non-compliance would prevent practices from accepting patients who rely on this alternative proof of insurance.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: eEB reception functions available**
Given the software implements the Technische Anlage eEB [TA_eEB]
When a health insurer transmits an eEB via KIM to the practice
Then the software provides the user with the functions according to the Technische Anlage eEB [TA_eEB]
