### User Story

As the **System**, the system must **detect when a cost unit has been merged (fusioned) and automatically redirect billing and form issuance to the acquiring cost unit while preserving the original IK**,
so that **billing continues correctly after cost unit mergers without manual intervention**.

### Compliance Context

When a cost unit is merged, the acquiring cost unit takes over billing responsibilities. The system must use the acquiring cost unit's VKNR and Kassenname zur Bedruckung but retain the original IK for billing. Failure to handle fusions correctly results in claims against a non-existent cost unit.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Merged cost unit detected and acquiring unit identified**
Given IK_v is present in the KT-Stammdatei
And LQ lies above the validity period of `/kostentraeger/gueltigkeit/@V`
And `/kostentraeger/existenzbeendigung/@V="F"` (fusion)
When the system processes the cost unit
Then the acquiring cost unit is identified via `/kostentraeger/existenzbeendigung/aufnehmender_kostentraeger/@V`
And billing capability with KTAB_y is verified analogous to P2-210

**Scenario 2: Correct VKNR and IK usage in ADT billing**
Given a merged cost unit is being billed
When the ADT billing is generated
Then the VKNR of the acquiring (new) cost unit is used
And the IK of the original (old) cost unit is used
And P2-136 applies if an insurance card was read

**Scenario 3: Correct names for form issuance**
Given a merged cost unit is identified
When panel physician forms are issued
Then the "Kassenname zur Bedruckung" of the acquiring (new) cost unit is used
And the IK of the original (old) cost unit is used
