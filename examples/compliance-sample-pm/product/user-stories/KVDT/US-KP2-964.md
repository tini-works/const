### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **the system to calculate the current cross-quarter remaining contingent of an approved psychotherapy immediately after service recording and during billing creation**,
so that **I can accurately record termination notifications (Pseudo-GOP 88130/88131) and track remaining therapy units per § 17 Abs. 7 Psychotherapy Agreement**.

### Compliance Context

The Psychotherapy Agreement (Anlage 1 BMV-Ae) § 17 Abs. 7 requires termination notifications with accurate remaining contingent data. The contingent is calculated cross-quarter from approved therapy units minus billed GOPen. Incorrect calculations risk improper termination notifications and potential overspending of approved therapy units.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Remaining contingent calculated after service recording and during billing**
Given an approved psychotherapy exists with recorded services
When services are recorded or billing is created
Then the software calculates the current cross-quarter remaining contingent immediately

**Scenario 2: Exclusion of rejected services from calculation**
Given services were billed in previous quarters but not accepted by the KV
When the user marks such services as not accepted
Then those services are excluded from further calculations of the remaining contingent
