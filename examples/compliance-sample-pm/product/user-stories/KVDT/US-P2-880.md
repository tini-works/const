### User Story

As a **System**, the system must limit service legend line length to 40 characters on the patient receipt and apply line breaks for longer texts,
so that the tabular format of the patient receipt is maintained.

### Compliance Context

The patient receipt uses a tabular format that limits service legend line length to 40 characters. Without enforcing this limit, the table layout breaks, rendering the receipt illegible or non-compliant.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Line break for legends exceeding 40 characters [derived]**
Given a service legend on the patient receipt exceeds 40 characters
When the receipt is rendered
Then a line break occurs at or before the 40-character limit

**Scenario 2: Legends within 40 characters displayed normally [derived]**
Given a service legend is 40 characters or fewer
When the receipt is rendered
Then the legend is displayed on a single line without modification
