### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **the system to notify me about Pseudo-GOP 88131 in a therapy-free quarter when a previous-quarter approved psychotherapy with remaining contingent has no termination notification and relapse prevention can follow**,
so that **I can submit the termination notification and enable relapse prevention billing in a timely manner**.

### Compliance Context

Section 17 para. 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement (Annex 1 BMV-Ae) requires termination notifications even in therapy-free quarters when relapse prevention remains possible. Failure to notify risks missing the window for relapse prevention documentation, causing billing non-compliance.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Notification in therapy-free quarter for possible relapse prevention**
Given an approved psychotherapy with remaining contingent exists
And the treatment duration amounts to at least 40 therapy units
And the last service was recorded in the previous quarter
And no Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 has been transmitted or recorded, and no marking per KP2-966 acceptance criterion 4 has occurred
And the psychotherapy is not interrupted
When billing creation is initiated
Then the software must notify the user about the possible recording and transfer of a "Termination notification with Pseudo-GOP 88131"

**Scenario 2: Additional criteria may gate notification**
Given the conditions for Scenario 1 are partially met
When the software evaluates the notification criteria
Then the software may make the notification dependent on additional criteria per the Psychotherapy Guideline

**Scenario 3: Pseudo treatment case, automatic data transfer, and forgo options apply**
Given the notification per Scenario 1 has been displayed
When the user needs to act on the notification
Then acceptance criteria 2, 3, and 4 of function KP2-966 apply
