### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **the system to immediately notify me when I record a relapse prevention GOP for an approved psychotherapy that lacks a transmitted Pseudo-GOP 88131**,
so that **I can document the required termination notification before proceeding with relapse prevention billing**.

### Compliance Context

Section 17 para. 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement in conjunction with section 20 para. 3 and 6 requires a termination notification via Pseudo-GOP 88131 before relapse prevention services can be billed. Missing this prerequisite could render relapse prevention claims invalid and trigger billing rejections.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Immediate notification for missing Pseudo-GOP 88131 during relapse prevention recording**
Given an approved psychotherapy exists
And no Pseudo-GOP 88131 has been transmitted in the treatment history
When the user records a GOP for relapse prevention (see KBV_ITA_AHEX_Codierungstabelle_PT_Rezidiv)
Then the software immediately notifies the user of the missing termination notification
And enables documentation of GOP 88131
And displays the advisory text: "Attention: You would like to perform/bill a guideline therapy as relapse prevention. A prerequisite for this is a termination notification for the guideline therapy with Pseudo-GOP 88131 (section 17 para. 7 Psychotherapy Agreement). No termination with Pseudo-GOP 88131 has been transmitted in the previous treatment history."
