### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt** (contract physician), I want each GNR in a service chain to require my individual confirmation before being adopted into billing,
so that no service is billed without my explicit acknowledgment as the responsible billing physician.

### Compliance Context

Service chains allow multiple GNRs to be assigned with a single action, but the fee schedule ordinance requires individual physician confirmation for each billed service. Without this safeguard, services could be billed without the physician's explicit approval, creating compliance and liability risks. Exceptions exist only for mandatory eArztbrief functions P50-01, P50-02, and P50-03.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Individual confirmation required for each GNR in a chain** [derived]
Given a service chain assigns multiple GNRs to a patient's service documentation
When the service chain is used for billing purposes
Then each GNR of the chain is adopted into the billing only after individual confirmation by the user
AND this applies to all forms of data entry (document reading, digitizing board, keyboard, scanner, programmed scheduling)

**Scenario 2: Exception for eArztbrief mandatory functions** [derived]
Given the service involves mandatory functions P50-01, P50-02, or P50-03 of [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_eArztbrief]
When the service chain is processed
Then individual confirmation is not required for these exempt functions

**Scenario 3: Sending practice confirmation via billing-relevant result** [derived]
Given a sending practice uses a service chain
When the fee schedule number is substantiated by a billing-relevant result (measurement values, predefined results, or billing-controlling characters)
Then individual confirmation is considered fulfilled
