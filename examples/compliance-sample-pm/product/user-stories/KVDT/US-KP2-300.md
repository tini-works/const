### User Story

As a **System**, the system must compare insurance card data with already stored patient data during card reading to identify existing records, prevent duplicates, and avoid erroneous overwrites,
so that patient master data integrity is maintained and correct patient identification is ensured.

### Compliance Context

During insurance card reading, the system must perform identity matching against existing patient records. Without this comparison, duplicate patient records could be created, or existing records could be unknowingly overwritten, leading to data integrity issues, billing errors, and potential patient safety risks. A recommended search strategy uses IK + Versicherten-ID, then name + date of birth, then user selection.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Identify existing patient record during card reading**
Given an insurance card is being read
When the system compares the card data with stored patient data
Then the system identifies an already existing patient master data record for the patient
AND avoids creating redundant patient master data records
AND avoids erroneous overwrites of patient master data records
