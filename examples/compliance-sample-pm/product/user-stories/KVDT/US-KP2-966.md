### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **the system to notify me in the two subsequent quarters when a previous-quarter approved psychotherapy has no remaining contingent and no Pseudo-GOP 88130/88131 was transferred**,
so that **I can submit the required termination notification and remain compliant with the Psychotherapy Agreement**.

### Compliance Context

Section 17 para. 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement (Annex 1 BMV-Ae) requires that termination notifications are submitted even for psychotherapies whose contingent expired in a previous quarter. Non-compliance may result in incomplete billing records and regulatory findings during KV audits.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Notification in subsequent quarters for unresolved psychotherapy**
Given a previous-quarter approved psychotherapy without remaining contingent exists
And no Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 has been transferred
And no marking per acceptance criterion (4) exists
When billing creation is initiated in either of the two subsequent quarters
Then the software must notify the user about the specification of Pseudo-GOP 88130
And only approved psychotherapies without remaining contingent from 01.01.2020 onwards shall be displayed

**Scenario 2: Creation of pseudo treatment case for Pseudo-GOP transfer**
Given an approved psychotherapy per criterion 1 exists
When the user needs to transfer Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131
Then the software must allow recording a "Pseudo treatment case" solely for transferring Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131
And the transfer within the ADT billing must be possible
And creation must be possible even if the patient was not in the practice in the corresponding quarter
And in that case only the Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 may be transferred
And the user should be notified that the ICD-10-GM code from the last treatment case can be used

**Scenario 3: Automatic transfer of insured person data without health insurance card**
Given no health insurance card has been read for the pseudo treatment case
When the user creates a pseudo treatment case per criterion 2
Then the software should automatically transfer insured person data from patient master data
And the software does not take over a reading date
And the software does not take over fields FKen 3006, 3010, 3011, 3012, 3013 and 4134

**Scenario 4: User forgoes transfer due to follow-up application or interruption**
Given an approved psychotherapy from a previous quarter has no remaining contingent
When the user chooses to forgo the transfer of Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131
Then the software must allow this if a follow-up application has been or is being filed
Or if the psychotherapy is interrupted for longer than 6 months with an informal justification per section 17 para. 6 Psychotherapy Agreement submitted to the health insurance fund
And the software provides the ability to record this in the patient documentation
