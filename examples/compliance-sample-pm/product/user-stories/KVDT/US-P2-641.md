### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want to identify GOPen with a BSNR and LANR during service documentation,
so that every billed service is traceable to the place and provider per the Contract Physician Law Amendment Act.

### Compliance Context

In implementation of the Contract Physician Law Amendment Act, every billed service must be identified with the "(Branch) Practice site number of the place of service delivery" and the "Lifelong physician number of the service provider". In ASV billing by hospital physicians, a Pseudo-LANR per § 2 Nr. 25 ASV-AV may be used. Missing identification leads to rejected services.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Identify GOPen with BSNR and LANR**
Given the user documents services
When a GOP is captured
Then the software enables identification with a practice site number and a lifelong physician number

**Scenario 2: Transmit identification in billing**
Given GOPen have been identified with BSNR and LANR
When the billing is transmitted
Then the (branch) practice site number is transmitted in FK 5098
AND the LANR is transmitted in FK 5099 or the Pseudo-LANR in FK 5101
