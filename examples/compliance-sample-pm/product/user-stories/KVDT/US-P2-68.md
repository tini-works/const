### User Story

As a **Praxisadministrator**, I want to see the connector certificate expiry date and have it transmitted in ADT billing,
so that the KV can proactively support the practice before the connector certificate expires.

### Compliance Context

KVen use the connector certificate expiry date to support practices with appropriate measures before certificate expiration. Without this information in billing, practices may face unexpected TI outages when certificates expire unnoticed.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Read and transmit certificate expiry date**
Given a connector is deployed in a (subsidiary) practice site
When the software reads the connector certificate
Then the expiry date is read from the connector
AND transmitted in FK 0227 ("Ablaufdatum des Konnektorzertifikats") in the billing

**Scenario 2: Implement gematik specification requirements**
Given the software interfaces with the connector
When certificate information is processed
Then the software implements requirements A_22917, A_22918, A_22969, A_13533-01 per [gematik Implementierungsleitfaden Primärsysteme TI]
AND implements TIP1-A_4695-03 per [Spezifikation Konnektor]
