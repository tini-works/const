### User Story

As an **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want the software to enable recording, storage, and transmission of ASV services,
so that ambulatory specialist care (ASV) services are correctly billed per section 116b SGB V.

### Compliance Context

The contractual basis is section 6, para. 3 of the ASV-AV in conjunction with section 116b, para. 6, sentence 12 SGB V. Software must support the full ASV billing lifecycle. Non-compliance means the practice cannot bill ASV services, losing revenue and violating contractual obligations.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Recording and storage of ASV services**
Given the user is documenting ambulatory specialist care (ASV) services
When the user records services within the ASV framework
Then the software enables the user to record and store the ASV services

**Scenario 2: ASV services transferred to billing data record**
Given ASV services have been recorded
When the billing is generated
Then the software transfers the ASV services into the billing data record according to the section "Integration of ASV Billing into the ADT Data Package" from [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Datensatzbeschreibung_KVDT]

**Scenario 3: ASV provisions observed for contract physician forms**
Given ASV services are being documented
When the user issues contract physician forms
Then the software observes the provisions of the section "Ambulante spezialfachaerztliche Versorgung (ASV)" from [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_Formularbedruckung]
