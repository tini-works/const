### User Story

As a **System**, the system must automatically delete a patient's co-payment exemption (gebuehrenfrei) status no later than after a year change,
so that expired co-payment exemptions do not carry over into the new calendar year and prescription forms reflect the current exemption status.

### Compliance Context

Co-payment exemptions are granted for the current calendar year only, based on the insured person reaching their personal burden limit. If the exemption status is not reset after a year change, patients may incorrectly be marked as fee-exempt, leading to incorrect prescription markings and potential recourse claims from health insurers.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Delete co-payment exemption after year change**
Given a patient has been marked as "gebuehrenfrei" in the PVS based on proof of co-payment exemption
When a year change occurs
Then the designation "gebuehrenfrei" is deleted system-side for the patient no later than after the year change
