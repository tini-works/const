### User Story

As an **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want **the system to warn me when the IK on the insurance card is invalid/expired, while still allowing billing if I choose to proceed**,
so that **I am aware of the risk but can still bill in justified cases**.

### Compliance Context

An expired IK on the insurance card typically indicates the cost unit has changed its identifier. While the system must warn the user, billing must remain possible because the underlying cost unit may still be valid. Blocking billing entirely would prevent legitimate claims.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Warning for invalid/expired IK**
Given IK_v is present in the KT-Stammdatei
And LQ lies above the validity period of `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/gueltigkeit/@V`
When the system processes the cost unit
Then a system-generated WARNING is issued that the IK on the insurance card is invalid

**Scenario 2: Billing still possible with invalid IK**
Given the user has received the warning about the invalid IK
When the user chooses to proceed with billing
Then the billing system must enable billing
And billing capability is verified analogous to P2-210
