### User Story

As a **System**, The system must **transfer the LANR field with FK 4299 in the psychotherapy information block for combination treatments in a psychotherapeutic professional practice community**,
so that **billed GOPen can be correctly assigned to the respective psychotherapists in the practice community**.

### Compliance Context

In a psychotherapeutic Berufsausübungsgemeinschaft, combination treatments involve two psychotherapists from the same practice. Without explicit LANR specification in the psychotherapy information block, the KV cannot assign billed services to the correct practitioner, leading to billing errors.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: LANR transferred in psychotherapy information block**
Given a combination treatment by two psychotherapists in a psychotherapeutic professional practice community is documented
When the billing data is generated
Then the LANR field is transferred with FK 4299 in the psychotherapy information block (from field 4235 through 4257)
