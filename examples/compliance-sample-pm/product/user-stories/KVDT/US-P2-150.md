### User Story

As the **System**, the system must **automatically update the last read-in day of the insurance card in the quarter (FK 4109) across all record types 010x when multiple physician-patient contacts occur without insurance changes**,
so that **all services in the current quarter are legitimated by the most recent card reading**.

### Compliance Context

The read-in date must be transmitted for billing to prove that an eGK was presented per section 13 BMV-Ae. Automatic updating ensures the most recent reading legitimates all services in the quarter without requiring separate card readings per visit.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Fill read-in date when none exists**
Given services for the same insured person are provided by the same physician in the current quarter
And no change of Besondere Personengruppe (P2-535), health insurer (P2-530), or insurance type (P2-540) exists
And no read-in date for record types 010x is present
When the insurance card is read in the current quarter
Then the software must fill the read-in date into FK 4109 of all record types "010x" of the ADT data package

**Scenario 2: Overwrite existing read-in date with new reading**
Given services for the same insured person are provided by the same physician in the current quarter
And no change of Besondere Personengruppe, health insurer, or insurance type exists
And a read-in date for record types 010x is already present
When the insurance card is read again in the current quarter
Then the software must overwrite FK 4109 for all record types "010x" with the new read-in date

**Scenario 3: Previous quarter read-in dates are not adjusted**
Given read-in dates exist for ADT data packages "010x" from previous quarters
When a new insurance card reading occurs
Then the read-in date (FK 4109) of previous quarter data packages must not be adjusted
