### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt** (contract physician), I want to receive digital Form 6 via a secure transmission channel,
so that I can process incoming electronic referrals from other physicians securely and in compliance with digital preprint standards.

### Compliance Context

Receiving physicians must be able to accept electronic referrals through the same secure channel (KIM) used for sending. The received form must conform to the technical handbook for digital preprints. Without reception capability, the electronic referral workflow is incomplete and physicians cannot participate in digital doctor-to-doctor communication.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Receive digital Form 6 via secure channel**
Given a digital Form 6 has been sent via KIM per [K26-05](../../compliances/KVDT/K26-05.md)
When the receiving system processes incoming messages
Then the user is enabled to receive the digital Form 6

**Scenario 2: Manufacturer confirmation**
Given the reception function is implemented
When the software is evaluated
Then a corresponding implementation confirmation has been provided by the software manufacturer as part of the supplementary declaration
