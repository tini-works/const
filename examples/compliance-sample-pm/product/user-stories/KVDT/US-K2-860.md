### User Story

As a **Patient**, I want to receive a day-based patient receipt listing the services rendered during my visit,
so that I have transparency about the medical services performed and their associated billing codes.

### Compliance Context

Patients have a right to receive a listing of services rendered. The Patientenquittung must be producible immediately after or during a physician-patient contact. For minors under 15, the physician must be able to exclude individual services for child welfare reasons (per KBV guideline on ePA), and no notice of incompleteness may appear on the receipt.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Day-based patient receipt immediately after contact**
Given a physician-patient contact has occurred or is occurring
When the user generates a patient receipt
Then a service listing of the just-rendered services is produced (day-based)

**Scenario 2: Accumulated quarter-based receipt**
Given services have been rendered across the current quarter
When the user generates a form-based patient receipt
Then all services rendered so far in the quarter are accumulated in the listing

**Scenario 3: Exclude services for minors under 15**
Given the patient has not yet completed the 15th year of life
When the user generates a patient receipt
Then the user has the ability to exclude individual services from the patient receipt
AND no notice about incompleteness of the service listing is displayed on the patient receipt
