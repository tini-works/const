### User Story

As the **System**, the system must **detect when a cost unit is dissolved and prevent billing and form issuance against it with an error message**,
so that **no claims are submitted to a dissolved cost unit, avoiding billing rejections**.

### Compliance Context

Section 11, paragraph 1 and 2 of Anlage 6 BMV-Ae (Vertrag ueber den Datenaustausch auf Datentraegern) requires verification of cost unit validity. Billing against a dissolved cost unit will be rejected, causing reimbursement delays and administrative overhead.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Dissolved cost unit detected**
Given IK_v is present in the KT-Stammdatei
And the validity of the cost unit under `/kostentraeger/gueltigkeit/@V` has expired
And `/kostentraeger/existenzbeendigung/@V="A"` (dissolved)
And LQ lies above the last billable quarter under `/kostentraeger/existenzbeendigung/letztes_quartal/@V`
When the system processes the cost unit
Then a system-generated error message is issued that this cost unit is dissolved

**Scenario 2: Billing and form issuance blocked**
Given a dissolved cost unit has been detected
When the user attempts further processing
Then the software does not support direct further processing for ADT billing at the expense of this cost unit
And the software does not support the issuance of contractual physician forms or BFB forms at the expense of this cost unit
