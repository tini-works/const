### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **the system to notify me quarterly when an approved psychotherapy has remaining contingent but no patient contact has occurred for two quarters**,
so that **I can specify Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 and fulfill the termination notification obligation**.

### Compliance Context

Section 17 para. 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement (Annex 1 BMV-Ae) requires notification about Pseudo-GOP specification when therapy sessions lapse without patient contact. Without this reminder, therapists risk missing mandatory termination notifications, leading to billing irregularities.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Quarterly notification for psychotherapy without APK for two quarters**
Given an approved psychotherapy with remaining contingent exists
And no APK has taken place for at least two quarters
And no Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 has been recorded in the current quarter
When billing creation is initiated
Then the software must notify the user about the specification of Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131
And only approved psychotherapies with remaining contingent shall be displayed

**Scenario 2: Pseudo treatment case, automatic data transfer, and forgo options apply**
Given the notification per Scenario 1 has been displayed
When the user needs to act on the notification
Then acceptance criteria 2, 3, and 4 of function KP2-966 apply
