### User Story

As a **System**, The system must automatically convert age-class-unspecific insured person flat rates and supplementary flat rates into their age-class-specific supplementary numbers,
so that billing transmits only the correctly differentiated flat rates as required by the Bewertungsausschuss.

### Compliance Context

Per decisions of the Bewertungsausschuss (section 87 Abs. 1 Satz 1 SGB V), age-dependent insured person flat rates introduced since 2013 and supplementary flat rates for appointment scheduling since 2019/2023 must be automatically converted. Only age-class-specifically differentiated numbers may be transmitted in billing. Manual entry without conversion violates billing requirements.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Automatic conversion to age-class-specific supplementary number**
Given the user sets an insured person flat rate or supplementary flat rate for appointment scheduling
When the flat rate is differentiated into age-class-specific supplementary numbers in the EBM master data file
Then the software automatically converts the flat rate into the age-class-specifically differentiated supplementary number
And the software applies the stored age rules depending on the patient's age, observing regulation 4.3.5 of the EBM

**Scenario 2: Manual substitution when age cannot be determined**
Given the patient's birth date information is incomplete or missing
When the age group cannot be automatically determined
Then the system gives the user the ability to perform the service substitution manually

**Scenario 3: Service substitution is transparent**
Given an automatic service substitution has been performed
When the user reviews the service documentation
Then the system makes the service substitution transparent for the user
