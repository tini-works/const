### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want **the system to require entry of the billable Satzart 010x or Scheinuntergruppe upon first insurance card reading in a quarter**,
so that **every patient encounter is correctly categorized for billing from the start**.

### Compliance Context

At first contact in a quarter, a billing case type must be established to ensure all subsequent services are correctly attributed. Failure to capture this at the outset leads to incomplete or incorrect billing submissions to the KV.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: First card reading triggers Satzart entry [derived]**
Given an insured person's insurance card is read for the first time in a quarter
When the reading process completes or the first billing-relevant data is captured
Then the system requires entry of the billable Satzart 010x or Scheinuntergruppe
