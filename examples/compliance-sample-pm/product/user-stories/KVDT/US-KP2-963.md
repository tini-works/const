### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want **to print the PDF template for Form PTV 10 directly from the system**,
so that **I can produce the psychotherapy form for patient documentation**.

### Compliance Context

Form PTV 10 must be printable from the certified software to fulfill psychotherapy documentation requirements. The ability to print ensures the form is available for patient records and regulatory compliance.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: PTV 10 printable from system**
Given the user needs to print Form PTV 10
When the user initiates printing
Then the PDF template [EXT_ITA_AHEX_PTV10] is printed directly from the system
