### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt** (contract physician), I want the system to support entering the OP date and exact supervisory service code on the referral form when referring supervisory services,
so that the continuing physician receives the necessary information for proper billing of post-operative supervisory services.

### Compliance Context

When supervisory services (Kapitel 31.4) are provided via referral, the OP date must be entered in the designated referral form field and the exact supervisory service code specified as an order. Without this information, the continuing physician cannot properly bill the supervisory service, leading to billing errors and potential compliance issues.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Enter OP date on referral for supervisory services** [derived]
Given the physician is referring a patient for supervisory services
When the referral form is being prepared
Then the PVS supports entering the OP date in the designated field on the referral form

**Scenario 2: Specify supervisory service code as order** [derived]
Given the physician is referring a patient for supervisory services
When the referral form is being prepared
Then the PVS supports specifying the exact service code of the supervisory service as an order
