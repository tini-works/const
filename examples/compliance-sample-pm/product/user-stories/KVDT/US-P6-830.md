### User Story

As a **Vertragsarzt**, I want to store services in the ADT billing file even when they violate EBM conditions and rules,
so that I can exercise clinical judgment and bill services that the EBM rules may not fully account for.

### Compliance Context

EBM rules are guidelines but cannot cover every clinical scenario. The physician must be able to override check conditions to bill services that are medically justified but may not match automated rule checks. Blocking storage entirely would prevent legitimate billing in edge cases and undermine physician autonomy.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Services stored despite rule violations** [derived]
Given a service violates conditions and rules of the EBM master data file
When the user confirms the service for billing
Then the system allows the storage of the service in the ADT billing file
