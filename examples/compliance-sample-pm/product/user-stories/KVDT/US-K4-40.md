### User Story

As a **System**, the system must pseudonymize the personnel field of the referral form (Form 6) by printing the Identifier-SA in print line 2 (Last Name field) instead of the patient's actual name,
so that patient identity is protected in pregnancy termination referral documentation.

### Compliance Context

Pregnancy termination cases require special anonymization. The patient's identity must not appear on printed referral forms; instead, the Identifier-SA is printed in the Last Name field to pseudonymize the document. For blank form printing, the barcode is omitted. Non-compliance would violate patient privacy protections specific to pregnancy termination cases.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Print Identifier-SA in Last Name field** [derived]
Given a referral form (Form 6) is being printed for a pregnancy termination case
When the personnel field is populated for printing
Then the Identifier-SA is printed in print line 2 (field: Last Name) of the personnel field

**Scenario 2: Omit barcode for blank form printing** [derived]
Given a blank referral form is being printed for a pregnancy termination case
When the form is generated
Then the barcode is omitted
