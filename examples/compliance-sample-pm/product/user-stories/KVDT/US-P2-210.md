### User Story

As the **System**, the system must **identify the cost unit via a valid IK, derive the Abrechnungs-VKNR from the KT-Stammdatei, store the IK patient-related, and use the Kassenname zur Bedruckung for form issuance**,
so that **billing and form issuance use the correct cost unit data when the IK is valid and within its validity period**.

### Compliance Context

When the IK, cost unit, and KT-Abrechnungsbereich are all within their validity periods, the standard billing path applies. The Abrechnungs-VKNR must be derived from the KT-Stammdatei (not the card), and form printing must use the Kassenname zur Bedruckung, not the card's Kostentraegername.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Valid IK within all validity periods**
Given IK_v is present in the KT-Stammdatei under `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/@V`
And LQ lies within the validity period of `/kostentraeger/gueltigkeit/@V` and `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/gueltigkeit/@V` and `/kostentraeger/kt_abrechnungsbereich_liste/kt_abrechnungsbereich/gueltigkeit/@V`
And the KV-Bereich is not listed in `/kostentraeger/unz_kv_geltungsbereich_liste/unz_kv_geltungsbereich/@V`
When the system processes the cost unit
Then the cost unit is billable with KTAB_y
And IK_v is used as the unique key for identifying the cost unit
And the Abrechnungs-VKNR is derived from the KT-Stammdatei for FK 4104
And IK_v is stored patient-related
And the "Kassenname zur Bedruckung" from the KT-Stammdatei is used for form issuance
