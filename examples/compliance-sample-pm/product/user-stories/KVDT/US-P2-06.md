### User Story

As the **System**, the system must **implement the "Anforderungskatalog Formularbedruckung" [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_Formularbedruckung]**,
so that **panel physician forms are printed correctly per KBV specifications**.

### Compliance Context

The form printing requirements catalog is binding for PVS software approved for creating panel physician billing. Non-compliance means forms issued to patients and other parties will not meet KBV standards, potentially invalidating referrals, prescriptions, and other official documents.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: Compliance with form printing requirements** [derived]
Given the software is approved for creating panel physician billing
When forms are printed
Then the software must comply with all requirements of the "Anforderungskatalog Formularbedruckung" [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_Formularbedruckung] in its current version
