### User Story

As a **System**, The system must skip gender-based GOP validation when the patient's gender is "undetermined" or "diverse",
so that patients with variants of gender development are not excluded from gender-specific services.

### Compliance Context

Per section 22, Abs. 3 Personenstandsgesetz, persons with variants of gender development may have gender "undetermined" (X) or "diverse" (D). These patients cannot be categorically excluded from gender-specific services. Blocking such GOPs would deny care to patients whose gender cannot be assigned to male or female.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: GOP allowed without gender check for undetermined/diverse**
Given the patient's gender is "undetermined" or "diverse" (FK 3110 equals X or D)
When the user enters a GOP that has a deviating gender definition in `/gnr/bedingung/administrative_gender_cd` of the GO master data file
Then the software allows the user to enter the GOP
And the software does not display a warning notice
And the GOP is transferred to the billing
