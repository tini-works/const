### User Story

As a **Abrechnungsverantwortliche**, I want **to specify the Vermittlungs-/Kontaktart when creating a Satzart or Scheinuntergruppe**,
so that **treatments resulting from Terminvermittlungen are correctly marked for separate remuneration per § 87a Abs. 3 Satz 5 SGB V**.

### Compliance Context

The TSVG and GKV-FinStG mandate separate remuneration for treatments resulting from appointment mediations or open consultation hours. Without correct marking in FK 4103, the practice risks losing entitled surcharges and violating billing requirements.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: User selects Vermittlungs-/Kontaktart**
Given the user is creating a Satzart or Scheinuntergruppe
When the user selects a Vermittlungs-/Kontaktart
Then the system transfers this information in Feldkennung 4103 to the billing
And the field must not be automatically pre-populated
