### User Story

As a **System**, The system must **allow GNR of minor surgery as an alternative billing justification (FK 5036) for Category-1 services from EBM chapters 31.2/36.2 when performed under anesthesia in children up to age 12**,
so that **Category-1 services billed instead of minor surgery are properly justified when no OPS code is available**.

### Compliance Context

When minor surgery services are replaced by higher-valued Category-1 services under anesthesia for children up to 12, no OPS code exists as justification. The GNR of the minor surgery must be used instead (FK 5036). Without this alternative, these services cannot be properly justified in billing.

### Acceptance Criteria

**Scenario 1: GNR justification via FK 5036 for Category-1 services [derived]**
Given a Category-1 service from EBM chapters 31.2 or 36.2 is billed for a child up to the completed 12th year of life under anesthesia
When no OPS code is available as justification
Then the system ensures the GNR of the minor surgery is transmitted as alternative justification via FK 5036

**Scenario 2: Permissible minor surgery GNRs from GOS [derived]**
Given the GOS defines supplementary condition "5036" (Justification-GNR) for relevant Category-1 services
When the user specifies a GNR justification
Then only the permissible minor surgery numbers listed under the justification list are accepted
