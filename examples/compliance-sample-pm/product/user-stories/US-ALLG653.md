### User Story

As a practice owner, I want the KBV KVDT catalog functions P2-10, P2-20, P2-30, P2-40, P2-51, P2-52, P2-65 apply to the Vertragssoftware unless otherwise specified by the HAEVG catalog, so that general compliance requirements are met.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given KBV KVDT catalog functions P2-10 through P2-65, when the Vertragssoftware processes KVDT data, then all listed functions are applied unless HAEVG specifies otherwise
