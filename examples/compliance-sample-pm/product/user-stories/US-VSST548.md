### User Story

As a practice staff, I want the Vertragssoftware display medications sorted starting with categories 'Gruen' and 'Blau' in all search results, or provide a filter showing only 'Gruen' and/or 'Blau' with a 'Show all' function to override the filter in individual cases, so that the software meets regulatory requirements.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given medication search results, when displayed, then Gruen and Blau categories appear first, or a filter limits display to those categories with a 'Show all' override available
