## E3: Contract & Enrollment

**Goal:** Manage the full patient participation lifecycle — from contract eligibility and consent through enrollment submission and status tracking — so that patients are correctly enrolled in selective healthcare contracts.

**Origin:** AKA Q1-26-1 catalog, sections 3.3 (VERT) and 3.4 (VERE) — 68 obligations covering contract management, patient enrollment workflows, HPM communication, and participation status.

**Scope:**
- Contract activation, deactivation, and quarterly update management
- Patient eligibility checks (age, KV region, insurance, doctor qualification)
- Teilnahmeerklaerung (participation declaration) generation and submission
- HPM enrollment submission and status response handling
- Participation status tracking (active, withdrawn, terminated)
- Consent form generation with patient and contract-specific data
- Online and offline enrollment channels
- Withdrawal and termination workflows with reason documentation
- Cross-contract enrollment rules (single active HZV per patient per insurer)
- External links to therapy facilities and contract partner directories

**Compliance impact:** BG-1a (AKA Certification), BG-3 (Contract Expansion), BG-5 (User Efficiency)
