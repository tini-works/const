### User Story
As a practice staff member, I want the system to have well-defined data models for reading electronic health card (eGK) data, so that insurance and person information from patient health cards can be correctly parsed and used across the application.

### Acceptance Criteria
1. Given an eGK card is read, when insurance data is extracted, then general insurance data (payer, coverage period) and protected insurance data are correctly structured
2. Given an eGK card is read, when person data is extracted, then patient identity information is correctly structured for downstream use
3. Given the data models, when used by other services, then insurance coverage begin/end dates and payer information are accessible in a standard format
