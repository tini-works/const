### Requirement

A combined billing across multiple practice sites is permissible under the following conditions:

1. All jointly billing practice sites use the same PVS.
2. In the besa data record, all physicians practicing at the practice sites relevant to the billing are listed, including their respective LANR.

It is irrelevant whether the combined billing file was generated from a central database or whether the data packages were assembled from the individual billing files of the practice sites.

The combined billing file's name is composed of the practice site number of the billing-generating/consolidating practice site (= sender).
