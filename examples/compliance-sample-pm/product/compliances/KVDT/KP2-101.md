### Requirement

The software ensures that reading a Krankenversichertenkarte (KVK) for statutorily insured persons is rejected with an error message.

### Rationale

Since January 1, 2015, only the electronic Gesundheitskarte serves as proof of entitlement to medical services for statutorily insured persons, thus replacing the KVK.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The system must reject the reading of a KVK for statutorily insured persons with an error message when digits 3-5 of field 4104 are < 800.
   a) No automatic further processing of the rejected data occurs.
   b) The user has the option, at their own discretion and with knowledge of potential recourse claims, to manually enter the card data into the PVS for billing and issuance of panel physician forms (see [KP2-102](KP2-102.md)). Treatment and billing are carried out in accordance with Anlage 4a BMV-Ä (Vereinbarung zum Inhalt und zur Anwendung der elektronischen Gesundheitskarte).
2. The system must support reading and automatic further processing of the KVK for "original" Other Cost Carriers when digits 3-5 of field 4104 are >= 800 and the content of field 4106 (KTAB) is 00-09.

### Condition

Excluded from implementation of this requirement are software systems without APK.

### Notes

These requirements also apply when adopting insurance card data records from a mobile card terminal.
