### Requirement

The software can offer the user the ability to create and print lists of approved psychotherapies with missing termination notification with Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131, analogous to acceptance criterion 1 of functions [KP2-965](KP2-965.md), [KP2-966](KP2-966.md), [KP2-967](KP2-967.md), [KP2-968](KP2-968.md), [KP2-970](KP2-970.md), and [KP2-971](KP2-971.md).

### Rationale

The user should have the ability to create and print lists of approved psychotherapies with missing termination notification with Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user can create and print lists of approved psychotherapies with missing termination notification analogous to acceptance criterion (1) of functions [KP2-965](KP2-965.md), [KP2-966](KP2-966.md), [KP2-967](KP2-967.md), [KP2-968](KP2-968.md), [KP2-970](KP2-970.md), and [KP2-971](KP2-971.md).
