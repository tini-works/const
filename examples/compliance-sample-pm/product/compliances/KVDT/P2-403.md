### Requirement

The system must ensure that the user is provided with the meaning of the values for the DMP-Kennzeichen in field FK 4132.

### Rationale

The user should be supported in identifying DMPs in which an insured person may be enrolled.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user must have the ability to display the meaning of a DMP-Kennzeichen.

### Notes

All permitted DMP-Kennzeichen and their meanings are published under [S_KBV_DMP].

Software manufacturers may also always display the meaning together with the value.
