### Requirement

The software must give the user the ability, when creating an additional "Satzart" or "Scheinuntergruppe" per [P2-501](P2-501.md), to specify the Vermittlungs-/Kontaktart.

### Rationale

Due to the separate remuneration for treatments resulting from Terminvermittlungen or special constellations (e.g. open consultation hours) per § 87a Abs. 3 Satz 5 SGB V (i. d. F. des TSVG und GKV-FinStG), the billed services are to be marked by Schein.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user has the ability when creating a "Satzart" or "Scheinuntergruppe" to select the Vermittlungs-/Kontaktart (FK 4103).
   a) When the user selects a Vermittlungs-/Kontaktart, the system transfers this information in Feldkennung 4103 to the billing.
   b) This field must not be automatically pre-populated.
