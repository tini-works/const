### Requirement

The software ensures that for combination treatment by two psychotherapists in a psychotherapeutic professional practice community, the LANR field is transferred with FK 4299 in the psychotherapy information block (from field 4235 through 4257).

### Rationale

In the execution of a combination treatment by two psychotherapists in a psychotherapeutic professional practice community, without the explicit specification of the respective LANR in the psychotherapy information block, the assignment of the billed GOPs to the psychotherapists cannot be ensured.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. For combination treatment by two psychotherapists in a psychotherapeutic professional practice community, the LANR field is transferred with FK 4299 in the psychotherapy information block.
