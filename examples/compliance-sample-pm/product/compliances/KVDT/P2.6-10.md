## P2.6-10 — Exclusion of Other Cost Carriers

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | P2.6-10 |
| **Type** | Mandatory |
| **Source** | KVDT v6.06 |
| **Section** | 3 Kurärztliche Abrechnung (KADT) |
| **Status** | TBC |
| **Goals** | — |
| **Verification Method** | — |
| **Matched by** | — |
| **Refer** | — |

### Requirement

If a spa physician billing is to be processed via an Other Cost Carrier, the following applies:

1. A system-side **WARNING** is issued that a billing with Other Cost Carriers via KVDT is not possible.
2. Further processing for the purpose of KADT billing with Other Cost Carriers must **not be possible**.
3. Printing of contract physician forms must be **possible** with Other Cost Carriers.

### Notes

The spa physician billing is then made directly with the cost carrier.
