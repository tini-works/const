### Requirement

The software must enable the capture and transmission of the data "Manufacturer/Supplier Name" in field 5074 and "Article/Model Number" in field 5075 for material costs in field 5012 in the ADT billing.

### Rationale

According to the ASV billing agreement, material costs incurred up to 31.12.2019 must be extended with the data "Manufacturer/Supplier Name" and "Article/Model Number". From 01.01.2020, these data can be provided voluntarily as part of ASV billing. From 01.04.2021, the fields can also be populated without ASV context.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. In the ADT billing, the user must have the ability, for existing material costs, to specify these through the data "Manufacturer/Supplier Name" in field 5074 and "Article/Model Number" in field 5075.
2. In the ADT billing, in addition to the existing material costs (FK 5012) and the material cost description (FK 5011), the fields 5074 (Manufacturer/Supplier Name) and 5075 (Article/Model Number) can be transmitted according to the KVDT data record description.

### Notes

In field 5074, the name of the manufacturer is generally to be transmitted. If the manufacturer's name is not indicated on the invoice or is not known, the name of the supplier is to be provided.
