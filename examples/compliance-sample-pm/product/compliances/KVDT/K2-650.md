### Requirement

Billing provisions that the fee schedule ordinance permits may be implemented programmatically (e.g., non-concurrent calculation of items, etc.). As system-side reactions to detected errors, the following are permitted:

1. Error messages may be issued,
2. incorrectly applied GNRs may be eliminated,
3. GNRs may be automatically replaced or added within the scope of programmed scheduling. In this context, the rules for handling service chains must be observed.

The billing physician always bears the responsibility for the correct application of the fee schedule ordinance.
