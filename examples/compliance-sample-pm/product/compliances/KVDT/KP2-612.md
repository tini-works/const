## KP2-612 — Transmission of Type of Disease for Billing Transparency for GOP 11233[G-alpha]

| **Status** | TBC |
| **Goals** | — |
| **Verification Method** | — |
| **Matched by** | — |
| **Refer** | [KP2-624](KP2-624.md) |

### Requirement

The software must enable the specification of the type of disease (FK 5079) for GOP 11233[G-alpha].

### Rationale

According to EBM billing provisions for GOP 11233[G-alpha], the "Type of disease" is to be specified.

### Acceptance Criteria

All provisions of function [KP2-624](KP2-624.md) apply, subject to the following criteria:

1. The software must require the user to provide **exactly** one entry for "Type of disease" (FK 5079) when capturing GOP 11233[G-alpha] within the scope of service documentation.
2. The software transmits with the billing for the service GOP 11233[G-alpha] the "Type of disease" specified by the user for the service in field FK 5079.
3. The software transmits with the billing for the service GOP 11233[G-alpha] not the field FK 5077.
