### Requirement

If from a previous quarter an approved psychotherapy without remaining contingent and without transfer of a Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 exists, the software shall notify the user in the two subsequent quarters about the specification of Pseudo-GOP 88130.

### Rationale

This requirement results from section 17 para. 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement (Annex 1 BMV-Ae).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If from a previous quarter an approved psychotherapy without remaining contingent and without transfer of a Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 or without a marking per acceptance criterion (4) exists, the software must notify the user at the latest during billing creation for each of the two subsequent quarters about the specification of Pseudo-GOP 88130, provided the Pseudo-GOP has not yet been recorded in the current quarter.
   a) Only approved psychotherapies without remaining contingent from 01.01.2020 onwards shall be displayed to the user.
2. The software must allow the user, for an existing approved psychotherapy per 1., to record a so-called "Pseudo treatment case" for the sole purpose of transferring Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 and to transfer it within the ADT billing.
   a) Even if the patient was not in the practice in the corresponding quarter and had no contact with the practice, the creation must still be possible.
      i. In this case, only the Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131 may be transferred.
      ii. In this case, the user should be notified that the ICD-10-GM code from the last treatment case can be used for coding this case.
3. If no health insurance card has been read, the software should support the user through automatic transfer of the insured person's data from the patient master data into the data record per (2).
   a) The software does not take over a reading date.
   b) The software does not take over any data that requires the reading of a health insurance card (fields FKen 3006, 3010, 3011, 3012, 3013 and 4134).
4. The software must allow the user to forgo a transfer of a "Termination notification with Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131" from a previous quarter if at least one of the following conditions is met:
   a) A follow-up application for the continuation of the psychotherapy has been or is being filed.
   b) The psychotherapy is interrupted for longer than 6 months and an informal justification for the interruption per section 17 para. 6 Psychotherapy Agreement is submitted to the health insurance fund. The software provides the user with the ability to record this in the patient documentation.
