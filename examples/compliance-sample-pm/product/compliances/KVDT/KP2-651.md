### Requirement

When transmitting GOPen for providing a vaccination against SARS-CoV-2, the batch number for the vaccine dose must be captured and transmitted for every rendered GOP.

### Rationale

Pursuant to § 13 Abs. 5 Nr. 10 Infection Protection Act (IfSG), for every vaccination against SARS-CoV-2, the batch number of the vaccination must be transmitted from the regional association of statutory health insurance physicians to the Robert Koch Institute.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software system must require the user, when capturing a GOP (in FK 5001) within the scope of service documentation, to provide the "Batch number" (FK 5010) if the value `@V="5010"` is stored for the GOP in the EBM-Stammdatei under `../gnr_zusatzangaben/gnr_zusatzangaben_liste/gnr_zusatzangabe/@V="5010"`.
2. The software system must also allow the user to capture the "Batch number" (FK 5010) for GOPen (in FK 5001) not listed under acceptance criterion 1.

### Condition

Exempt from implementation of this requirement are software systems without APK.

### Notes

Software systems may support their users in capturing batch numbers through suitable measures such as scanning labels, barcodes, or the multiple use of captured batch numbers.
