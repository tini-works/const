### Requirement

The software must enable the transmission of the "Prüfungsnachweis nach VSDM-Aktualisierung" (verification proof after VSDM update) into the ADT billing.

### Rationale

The "Prüfungsnachweis nach VSDM-Aktualisierung" is generated for the panel physician/panel psychotherapist during online verification and update. Providing the proof of performing the online update on the eGK (= "Prüfungsnachweis") is regulated by § 291b Absatz 2 SGB V. Reporting and transmission of the verification proof as part of the billing documents to be submitted to the responsible KV is regulated in § 295 SGB V.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If an eGK is read and an online verification was performed (i.e. a current EF.PN file is present on the eGK), the software must adopt the verification proof into the billing and store it.
   **Note:** The verification proof is encrypted with a symmetric key known only to the user. Only the verification proof for which the user initiated an update may be adopted into the PVS. For the verification proof to be read, the flag "Prüfnachweis lesen" must be set during communication with the connector.
2. The software must not transmit the verification proof when the data is adopted from a mobile card terminal.
3. If during reading in the quarter a verification proof with status 1 or 2 is returned, a VSD online verification and update is performed for all subsequent reading processes. However, no further verification proof -- specifically field codes 3010, 3011, 3012, and 3013 -- is adopted into the billing.
4. If during the first reading in the quarter a verification proof with status 3 to 6 is returned, an online verification and VSD update will be performed during the next reading process. If the new verification proof has status 1 or 2, the already existing verification proof -- specifically field codes 3010, 3011, 3012, and 3013 -- is overwritten in the current quarter in the billing.
5. The software transmits the verification proof with the billing in the respective data record "010x" as follows:
   a) Content of element `/PN/TS` in field FK 3010 ("Datum und Uhrzeit der Onlineprüfung und -aktualisierung (Timestamp)")
   b) Content of element `/PN/E` in field FK 3011 ("Ergebnis der Onlineprüfung und -aktualisierung")
   c) Content of element `/PN/EC` in field FK 3012 ("Error-Code")
   d) Content of element `/PN/PZ` in field FK 3013 ("Prüfziffer des Fachdienstes"), whereby the software must ensure that the base64-encoded verification digit is transmitted as field content without line breaks ("CR" and "LF").
6. The last requested verification proof -- according to Acceptance Criteria 3 and 4 -- must be adopted on a per-patient basis in all data records "010x" of the current quarter and transmitted.
   The software does not allow the user to manually enter this data.
   **Note:** This means that in the context of case separation through Besondere Personengruppe, health insurer, and status changes (see Kapitel 2.3.4 Besondere Personengruppen-, Kassen- und Statuswechsel), the same verification proof is always transmitted per patient and quarter.
7. Since the communication between PVS and connector is the prerequisite for the transmission of the verification proof, the successful completion of the gematik confirmation procedure must be demonstrated and the confirmation letter -- Bestätigung der Konformität des Primärsystems zur Konnektorschnittstelle: Funktionsumfang VSDM -- must be submitted.

### Condition

Excluded from implementation of this requirement are software systems without APK.
