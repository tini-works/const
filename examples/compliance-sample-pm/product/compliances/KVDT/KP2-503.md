### Requirement

The software must give the user the ability to transmit supplementary information as free text when specifying the Vermittlungs-/Kontaktart.

### Rationale

Due to the separate remuneration for treatments resulting from Terminvermittlungen or special constellations (e.g. open consultation hours) per § 87a Abs. 3 Satz 5 SGB V (i. d. F. des TSVG und GKV-FinStG), the billed services are to be marked by Schein.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user has the ability, when specifying the Vermittlungs-/Kontaktart (FK 4103), to transmit supplementary information in FK 4105 to the billing.
