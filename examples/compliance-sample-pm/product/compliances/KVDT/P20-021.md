### Requirement

In a further step, the RV-relevant materials [S_NVV_RV_MATERIAL, OID 1.2.276.0.76.3.1.1.5.2.21] are to be determined.

A dialog/configuration option/selection must be available, through which the RV-relevant materials that are individually used at the facility for the purpose of performing laboratory examinations can be selected.

The selection of materials must be permanently stored and editable at any time.

### Clarification

The specification of the RV materials used serves two purposes:

1. To confirm a possible RV participation obligation by selecting at least one RV-relevant material, and
2. To narrow down the potentially possible analyte-GOP combinations at the facility for the subsequent analyte selection (filter).

Due to the structure of the EBM, it is possible that documented laboratory services linked to RV-relevant analyses could also have been performed on the basis of other, non-RV-relevant materials. The indication of whether RV-relevant materials were used at all can therefore already provide information about the basic RV participation obligation and could therefore already be combined with the initial question of whether laboratory services are basically also billed.
