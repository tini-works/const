### Requirement

**SKT-Versicherte without Versichertenkarte:**

The billing software must ensure that for an SKT-Versicherte without Versichertenkarte (VKNR-Seriennummer 3.-5.Stelle >= 800 or KTAB != 00), Satzart 0101 with Scheinuntergruppe 00 (Satzart "Ambulante Behandlung") can be created multiple times in the same quarter. For each such instance, the validity period of the Abrechnungsschein in FK 4125 (Gültigkeitszeitraum von...bis...) must be captured and transferred, provided the validity information is available.

### Rationale

SKT-Versicherte without Versichertenkarte (e.g. Sozialamt) may receive multiple paper treatment authorizations in a quarter, each with a limited validity period. A separate Abrechnungsdatensatz must be creatable for each treatment authorization.
