### Requirement

Preconditions:
- IK_v is an IK from the insurance card and present under element `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/@V` in the KT-Stammdatei
- LQ is the service quarter
- KTAB_y is a KT-Abrechnungsbereich assignable to the patient

**If** LQ lies above the validity period of `/kostentraeger/gueltigkeit/@V` **and** `/kostentraeger/existenzbeendigung/@V="F"`, **then**:

1. The cost unit has been merged (fusioned). The acquiring cost unit is linked via `/kostentraeger/existenzbeendigung/aufnehmender_kostentraeger/@V` with the corresponding data record.
2. The billing capability of the acquiring cost unit with KTAB_y must be verified through further processing analogous to "CASE 1 - IK is valid" (see function [P2-210](P2-210.md)). The KTAB of the **acquiring** cost unit applies. The properties of the acquiring cost unit are relevant for further processing.
3. For ADT billing, considering item (2):
   a) "VKNR" of the **acquiring** (new) cost unit is to be used.
   b) "IK" of the **original** (old) cost unit is to be used.
   c) If an insurance card was read, function [P2-136](P2-136.md) "Name des Kostenträgers von der Versichertenkarte" additionally applies.
4. For the issuance of contractual physician forms, considering item (2):
   a) "Kassenname zur Bedruckung" of the **acquiring** (new) cost unit is to be used.
   b) "IK" of the **original** (old) cost unit is to be used.
