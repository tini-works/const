### Requirement

The software enables the user to capture billing justifications within the scope of service documentation for visits outside the medical practice.

### Rationale

For a (home) visit of an insured person by the user, billing of additional "travel allowance" is possible.

To receive "travel allowance", it is necessary to specify either a zone or the one-way distance in kilometers, or the location of the visit, or the specification of a KV-specific fee schedule number as part of the billing by the user.

Specifically, this is subject to the regulatory authority of the regional associations of statutory health insurance physicians.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software provides the user with corresponding input fields for capturing information for visits outside the medical practice. These are:

   | Designation | ADT Field Identifier |
   |-------------|---------------------|
   | Double kilometers (DKM) | 5008 |
   | Visit location for home visits | 5017 |
   | Zone for visits | 5018 |

2. The software transmits the data captured by the user according to the table above as part of the billing.

### Condition

Exempt from implementation of this requirement are software systems without APK.
