### Requirement

The applicable provisions per Kapitel 2.2.2.1 apply with the following case distinction:

1. For plausible identifiers, the VKNR to be stored is derived from the IK contained in the Identifier-SA (digits 9-15).
7. For implausible identifiers, the cost carrier cannot be automatically derived. The user must therefore have the option to manually assign the cost carrier.

### Notes

The health insurance fund is then to be manually taken from the authorization certificate.
