### Requirement

The software ensures during reading of the insurance card, through comparison with already stored patient data, that correct identification is performed system-side. Neither master data records may be created in duplicate nor may existing ones be unknowingly overwritten.

### Rationale

A comparison of insurance card data with already stored patient data is necessary during card reading in order to:
- identify an existing patient master data record for the patient,
- avoid redundant patient master data records for a patient,
- not unknowingly overwrite an existing patient master data record.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. During reading of the insurance card, the software ensures through comparison with already stored patient data that the system-side:
   a) identification of an already existing patient master data record for the patient,
   b) avoidance of redundant patient master data records for a patient, and
   c) avoidance of erroneous overwrites of patient master data records
   is ensured.

### Notes

The following search strategy is recommended for identification of already existing patient data:

1. IK, Versicherten-ID or Versichertennummer, if not present or not unique...
2. Name, Vorname, date of birth, if not present...
3. User-oriented selection procedure (selection window)
