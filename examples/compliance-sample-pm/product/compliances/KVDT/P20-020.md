### Requirement

The confirmed RV participation obligation can only be derived from the totality of information on the materials used, the analytes to be examined, including partial or exclusive use of unit-use reagents, as well as the laboratory services actually billed.

The primary objective is to determine whether laboratory services according to the key table [S_NVV_RV_ZERTIFIKAT, OID 1.2.276.0.76.3.1.1.5.2.22] are billed at all, from which a possible RV participation obligation could arise.

For this purpose, the billing shall be **automatically** parsed for whether potentially RV-relevant laboratory services are billed. If this is the case, at the latest at the end of the quarter, within the framework of billing, the further parameters must be documented according to functions [P20-021](P20-021.md) through [P20-070](P20-070.md).

Alternatively or additionally, there shall be the option to specify whether laboratory services are billed at the facility, directly and interactively (preferably via configuration).

The documented parameters must be permanently and editably stored and transmitted with the RVSA data record within the billing.

### Notes

In subsequent quarters, provided there is no change in the service spectrum, no changes to the configuration need to be made. However, it does not hurt to upload and confirm the settings once per quarter.

### Clarification

If no laboratory services are billed at all, no further parameters are necessary.
