### Requirement

Preconditions:
- IK_v is an IK from the insurance card and present under element `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/@V` in the KT-Stammdatei
- LQ is the service quarter

**If** LQ lies above the validity period `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/gueltigkeit@V`, **then**:

1. A system-generated **WARNING** is issued that the IK present on the insurance card is invalid.
2. If the user nevertheless wishes to bill using the invalid IK, the billing system must enable this. The billing capability of the cost unit with the KT-Abrechnungsbereich to be assigned must be verified analogous to the processing routines per "CASE 1 - IK is valid" (see function [P2-210](P2-210.md)).
