### Requirement

The software must transform the data fields of the Krankenversichertenkarte (KVK) for further processing within the scope of billing according to the requirements of the Mappingtabelle_KVK.

### Rationale

The contractual basis of this requirement is Anlage 4a BMV-Ä as well as the Technische Anlage zu Anlage 4a (BMV-Ä).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must implement all required requirements according to the Mappingtabelle_KVK in the respectively current version, cf. [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Mapping_KVK].

### Notes

For further processing and the subsequent mandatory functions, it is assumed that a valid eGK data record according to Mappingtabelle_KVK is available, whether through reading an eGK or through correct conversion of the KVK data fields.

Regardless of whether the data originally come from a read eGK or were generated through reading and subsequent conversion of a KVK, the term "Versichertenkarte" (insurance card) is used in the following wherever possible.
