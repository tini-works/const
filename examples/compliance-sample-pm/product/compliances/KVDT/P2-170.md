### Requirement

When billing-relevant insurance card information valid for the current quarter has been read from the insurance card, the corresponding data must be adoptable into the patient master data.

The software must ensure that the user, after reading the eGK with the help of the connector and before adopting the insurance card data, is shown all changes in comparison to the already existing patient master data. See requirement "VSDM-A_2538 PS: Anzeige Delta VSD" from the gematik Implementierungsleitfaden.

### Rationale

In Anlage 4a des BMV-Ä, Anhang 1, Nr. 1.5, it is defined that data from cards must only be adopted when the field values conform to the "Technische Anlage zur Anlage 4a".
