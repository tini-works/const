### Requirement

The software must verify the validity of a cost unit (specifically: the possible dissolution of a cost unit).

### Rationale

This requirement results from § 11, paragraph 1 and 2 of Anlage 6 BMV-Ae (Vertrag ueber den Datenaustausch auf Datentraegern).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software checks the following conditions:
   a) IK_v is an IK from the insurance card and present under element `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/@V` in the KT-Stammdatei
   b) LQ is the service quarter
   - **If** the validity of the cost unit under `/kostentraeger/gueltigkeit/@V` has expired **and** the cost unit is dissolved (`/kostentraeger/existenzbeendigung/@V="A"`), **then** the last billable quarter of the cost unit under `/kostentraeger/existenzbeendigung/letztes_quartal/@V` is defined.
   - If LQ lies above the last billable quarter, **then**:
2. A system-generated **error message** is issued that this cost unit is dissolved.
3. The software does not support direct further processing for the purpose of ADT billing at the expense of this cost unit.
4. The software does not support the issuance of contractual physician forms or BFB forms at the expense of this cost unit.
