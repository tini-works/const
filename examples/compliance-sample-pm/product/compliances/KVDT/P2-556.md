### Requirement

If in the current quarter a patient of the same insurer requires a marking with an additional Abrechnungsgebiet (FK 4122), the software must automatically create a new "010x" data record for billing.

### Rationale

Through the marking of the Abrechnungsgebiet, billed services are grouped based on specific contractual or legal requirements and can thus be separately remunerated or invoiced to the health insurers by the medical practice.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If a patient in the current quarter requires a marking with an additional Abrechnungsgebiet (FK 4122), the software must generate an additional "010x" data record.
2. The system may inform the user about the creation of a new "010x" data record.
3. If the insurance card is re-read, an already existing Einlesedatum in all "010x" data records with different Abrechnungsgebiet of the current quarter must be updated (see [P2-150](P2-150.md)).
