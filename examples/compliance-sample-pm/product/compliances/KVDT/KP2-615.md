### Requirement

The software must enable the specification of the HGNC gene symbol (FK 5077), the type of disease (FK 5079), and a valid ICD-10-GM code (FK 6001) for GOPen 19424[G-alpha], 19453[G-alpha], and 19456[G-alpha].

### Rationale

According to EBM billing provisions for GOPen 19424[G-alpha], 19453[G-alpha], and 19456[G-alpha], the "Type of examination (Gene symbol per HGNC)" and the "Type of disease coded per ICD-10-GM" are to be specified; additionally the "Type of disease" can be specified.

### Acceptance Criteria

All provisions of functions [KP2-621](KP2-621.md), [KP2-624](KP2-624.md), and [KP2-618](KP2-618.md) apply, subject to the following criteria:

1. The software system must require the user, when capturing GOPen 19424[G-alpha], 19453[G-alpha], and 19456[G-alpha], to provide **at least** one "HGNC gene symbol" (FK 5077) within the scope of service documentation. The user may also enter multiple "HGNC gene symbols" for the mentioned GOPen.
2. For GOPen 19424[G-alpha], 19453[G-alpha], and 19456[G-alpha], the "Type of disease" (FK 5079) does not need to be mandatorily captured. However, the user must be given the ability to capture one or more "Types of disease".
3. For entering multiple HGNC gene symbols for the mentioned GOPen, HGNC gene symbol chains according to function [KP2-623](KP2-623.md) can be used.
