## KP2-513 — Selection/Proposal of Time-Staggered Surcharges Based on Mediated Appointments

| **Type** | Conditional Mandatory |
| **Source** | KVDT v6.06 |
| **Section** | 2.3.1 Behandlungen auf Grundlage des Terminservice- und Versorgungsgesetzes |
| **Status** | TBC |
| **Goals** | — |
| **Verification Method** | — |
| **Matched by** | — |
| **Refer** | [P6-804](P6-804.md) |

### Requirement

If a physician-patient contact has resulted from a Terminvermittlung, the software must suggest to the user the time-staggered surcharge (differentiated by age classes and time limits) for adoption into the billing, provided the user has captured the relevant information.

### Rationale

To support the user in billing based on available information about the Vermittlungs-/Kontaktart (FK 4103), the Tag der Terminvermittlung (FK 4115) and the Leistungstag (FK 5000), the appropriate time-staggered surcharge should be displayed/suggested.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software enables automatic assignment of the time-staggered surcharge (of a GOP) by age classes and time limits and proposes adoption of the time-staggered surcharge into the billing based on the following algorithm/decision tree:
   a) The time-staggered surcharges must be defined in the GO-Stammdatei for the KV responsible for the user, identified by the practice through the SDEBM XML element `../kv/kennzeichen/arztpraxis/@V = true`.
2. When a Schein of a patient is marked as TSS-Akutfall (FK 4103 equals 2) and the day of Terminvermittlung (FK 4115) is present and the Versicherten-, Grund- und Konsiliarpauschale (VP/GP/KP) is set, the software must propose the following for adoption:
   a) If FK 5000 minus FK 4115 is less than or equal to 1 calendar day, the Schein is augmented with the surcharge identifier A.
   b) If FK 5000 minus FK 4115 is greater than 1 calendar day, the user receives the warning: *"The patient was not treated on the current day or following day and therefore cannot be marked as TSS-Akutfall."*
3. When a Schein of a patient is marked as TSS-Terminfall, HA-Vermittlungsfall, or Routine-Termin (FK 4103 equals 1, 3, or 6) and the day of Terminvermittlung (FK 4115) is present and the VP/GP/KP is set, the software must propose the following for adoption:
   a) If FK 5000 minus FK 4115 is less than or equal to 4 calendar days: surcharge identifier B.
   b) If FK 5000 minus FK 4115 is greater than 4 and less than or equal to 14 calendar days: surcharge identifier C.
   c) If FK 5000 minus FK 4115 is greater than 14 and less than or equal to 35 calendar days: surcharge identifier D.
   d) If FK 5000 minus FK 4115 is greater than 35 calendar days: warning: *"No time-staggered surcharge can be billed anymore, as the 35-calendar-day deadline has passed."*
4. The software must give the user the ability to confirm the adoption of the time-staggered surcharges into the billing.
   a) The proposed time-staggered surcharge can always be overridden by the user.

### Notes

The function KP2-513 does not need to be executed immediately following a Terminzuordnung, but can also run after setting the VP/GP/KP. The VP/GP/KP services can be programmatically identified from the EBM-Stammdateien based on the Grundleistungsliste (SDEBM XML element `../gnr/regel/grundleistungen_liste/..`). The surcharge services for Terminvermittlung can be identified based on the occurrence of the term "TSS-Terminvermittlung" in the short text (SDEBM XML element `../gnr/allgemein/legende/kurztext/..`). When selecting the appropriate surcharge service, the patient's age class per requirement [P6-804](P6-804.md) should also be considered.
