### Requirement

The software must give the user the option, within the framework of billing psychotherapy services, to transfer additional psychotherapy service data alongside the billing data.

### Rationale

The user must have the option to record and transfer billing data for psychotherapy services in the predefined fields.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user has the option, within the framework of billing psychotherapy services, to transfer fields 4234, 4235, 4236, 4247, 4250, 4251, 4252, 4253, 4254, 4255, 4256, and 4257 as defined in the [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Datensatzbeschreibung_KVDT].
