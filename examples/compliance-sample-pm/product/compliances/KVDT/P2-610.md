### Requirement

1. For the assignment of justification texts to GNRs the following applies:
   a) One or more justification texts must be able to be assigned to any fee schedule number.
   b) Justification texts must be able to be transmitted under the respectively prescribed field identifier.
   c) The billing system must allow the user to differentiate between justification types during service documentation.
   d) All justification texts must be stored with the respective field identifier "behind" the corresponding GNR.
2. For the field with FK 5012 "Material costs/Supply costs in Cent" the following additionally applies:
   a) If the treatment day (FK 5000) contains no GNR (FK 5001), the FK 5012 must for formal reasons be preceded by the **Pseudo-GNR** "88999" (FK 5001), unless a deviating rule for Pseudo-GNR is defined under field identifiers 9410/9411 of the KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei.
   b) Some KVs require, when billing material costs, a cost FK 5012 before every FK 5012 in general, even if the treatment day contains a fee schedule number that is one of the special Pseudo-GNRs for costs (FK 5001), which are stored under field identifier 9410 in the KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei.
3. If the KV-specific rule defined via the KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei concerning Pseudo-GNR as per point 2.b) is not observed by the user, then either:
   a) a corresponding **warning** must be issued using the data from field identifiers 9410/9411 of the KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei, or
   b) the special **Pseudo-GNR must be automatically** transmitted, which is stored as the sole Pseudo-GNR under field identifier 9410 in the KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei,
   unless the FK 5012 is not preceded by a fee schedule number that matches one stored in the EBM-Stammdatei under `../gnr_zusatzangaben/gnr_zusatzangaben_liste/gnr_zusatzangabe/@V="5012"`.

### Notes

In the context of ASV billing, the requirements of chapter 3.6.5.1 "Billing of ASV services that are not part of the EBM" from [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Datensatzbeschreibung_KVDT] also apply. The function [P2-610](P2-610.md) 2. b) does not apply if FK 5012 is used within ASV billing for the transmission of GOÄ prices.
