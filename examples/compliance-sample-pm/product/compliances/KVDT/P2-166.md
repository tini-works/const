### Requirement

The billing software must verify the cost carrier's obligation to provide services when reading an insurance card by checking the "Versicherungsschutz" (insurance coverage) (eGK: elements `..//Versicherungsschutz/Beginn` and `..//Versicherungsschutz/Ende`; KVK: Objekttag '8D' "GültigkeitsDatum"). Two fundamental cases must be distinguished:

**Case 1) -- Reading eGK data:**

a) If the read-in date (FK 4109) >= VersicherungsschutzBeginn and -- if a corresponding element is present on the eGK -- <= VersicherungsschutzEnde, then: This card is valid. Reading the data of this insurance card must be possible. Billing of services and issuance of panel physician forms are permissible.

b) If the read-in date (FK 4109) < VersicherungsschutzBeginn or -- if a corresponding element is present on the eGK -- > VersicherungsschutzEnde, then: Reading the data of this insurance card must be rejected with an appropriate error message. This card is invalid.

**Case 2) -- Reading KVK data:**

A transformation of the KVK field Objekttag '8D' "GültigkeitsDatum" in format "MMJJ" to the form "JJJJMMTT" must occur, where TT = last possible day of the month and JJJJ = concatenation ('20',JJ). This value must be transmitted under FK 4110 ("VersicherungsschutzEnde") in the ADT billing, cf. "Mappingtabelle_KVK" [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Mapping_KVK].

a) If the read-in date (FK 4109) <= VersicherungsschutzEnde, then: Reading the data of this insurance card must be possible. This card is valid. Billing of services and issuance of panel physician forms are permissible.

b) If the read-in date (FK 4109) > VersicherungsschutzEnde, then: Reading the data of this insurance card must be rejected with an appropriate error message. This card is invalid.

**Case 1) and Case 2):**

These requirements also apply when adopting insurance card data records from a mobile card terminal.

**Case 1) (b) and Case 2) (b):**

Direct further processing of the data of the insurance card for the purpose of ADT billing and issuance of panel physician forms to the debit of this cost carrier is **not** permissible.

With rejection of the insurance card, the user must retain the ability to view the rejected card data in "copyable format" pursuant to [KP2-102](KP2-102.md). An "Einlesedatum" pursuant to [P2-140](P2-140.md) must not be generated.

Treatment and billing are at the physician's discretion in accordance with Anlage 4a BMV-Ä ("Vereinbarung zum Inhalt und zur Anwendung der elektronischen Gesundheitskarte").
