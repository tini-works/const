### Requirement

Due to the lump-sum nature of the spa physician billing, no services are billed. The proof of treatments performed is provided through documentation of service days, each with the specification of the pseudo code "00001U."

### Notes

Intercurrent diseases, material costs, etc. are also covered by the lump-sum arrangement.
