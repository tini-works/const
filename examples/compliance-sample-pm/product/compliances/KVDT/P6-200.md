### Requirement

Through appropriate organizational measures, it must be ensured that for all users who bill laboratory cases (Muster 10 or 10A), the valid Arztverzeichnis-Stammdatei (SDAV) based on the data set description SDAV0308.nn is available for the billing software.

**Annotation:** The SDAV delivery is intended to help keep the inventory of practice and physician numbers in the laboratory/laboratory community as current as possible. This is of particular importance when the assignment of the referring physician is not done directly by capturing the billing number from the laboratory referral form, but rather through indirect keys.
