### Requirement

The software must automatically convert the age-class-unspecific insured person flat rates and the supplementary flat rates for appointment scheduling into the age-class-specific supplementary numbers using the EBM master data file.

### Rationale

In the EBM, age-dependent insured person flat rates for family physicians and pediatricians ("age-class-specific coded supplementary numbers") were introduced on October 1, 2013. On September 1, 2019 and January 1, 2023, further age-dependent supplementary flat rates for treatment due to appointment scheduling were likewise introduced in the form of age-class-specifically coded supplementary numbers.

According to the decisions of the Bewertungsausschuss per § 87 Abs. 1 Satz 1 SGB V, an automated conversion of these flat rates into the age-class-specific supplementary numbers must be performed. In the context of billing, only the age-class-specifically differentiated insured person flat rates and supplementary flat rates may be transmitted.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If the user sets an insured person flat rate or a supplementary flat rate for treatment due to appointment scheduling for service documentation and the flat rate is differentiated into age-class-specific supplementary numbers, the software must automatically convert this flat rate into the age-class-specifically differentiated supplementary number. The software takes into account the stored age rules depending on the patient's age, observing the regulation in the general provision 4.3.5 of the EBM.
   a) If the age group cannot be automatically determined due to incomplete or missing birth date information of the patient (e.g., when the birth date is unknown), the system must give the user the ability to perform the service substitution manually.
2. The system must make the service substitution transparent for the user.

### Notes

The age-class-specific identification is performed within the rule "sub_gop_liste" for the insured person and supplementary flat rate in the EBM master data file. The rule is valid within a certain reference period and defines the supplementary numbers depending on age conditions (`"./regel/sub_gop_liste/bezugsraum/gnr/altersbedingung_liste/"`).
