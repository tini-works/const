### Requirement

If the user records a GOP for relapse prevention within the framework of service documentation of an approved psychotherapy and no Pseudo-GOP 88131 has been transmitted in the treatment history, the software must notify the user about the missing "Termination notification with Pseudo-GOP 88131."

### Rationale

This requirement results from section 17 para. 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement in conjunction with section 20 para. 3 and 6 of the Psychotherapy Agreement.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If the user records a GOP for relapse prevention within the framework of service documentation of an approved psychotherapy (see [KBV_ITA_AHEX_Codierungstabelle_PT_Rezidiv]) and no Pseudo-GOP 88131 has been transmitted in the treatment history, the software immediately notifies the user of this situation and enables documentation of GOP 88131.

Advisory text: "Attention: You would like to perform/bill a guideline therapy as relapse prevention. A prerequisite for this is a termination notification for the guideline therapy with Pseudo-GOP 88131 (section 17 para. 7 Psychotherapy Agreement). No termination with Pseudo-GOP 88131 has been transmitted in the previous treatment history."
