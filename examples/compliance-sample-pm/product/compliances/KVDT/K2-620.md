### Requirement

The use of service chains for billing purposes is permissible under the following conditions:

Each GNR of a chain may only be adopted into the billing **after individual confirmation** by the user. This applies to all forms of data entry (e.g., document reading, digitizing board, keyboard, scanner) as well as programmed scheduling.

A service chain exists:

- when multiple GNRs can be assigned to a patient's service documentation with a single action;
- when one or more GNRs can be assigned to multiple patient service documentations with a single action.

**Exception:**

Exempt from this are the mandatory functions P50-01, P50-02, and P50-03 of the requirements catalog [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_eArztbrief].

**Note for sending practices:**

Individual confirmation of services in a service chain at sending practices is considered fulfilled when the fee schedule number to be billed is substantiated by a billing-relevant result.

Billing-relevant results are:

- manually captured or online-fed measurement values,
- manually captured or to-be-confirmed predefined results in text form,
- manually captured or to-be-confirmed predefined billing-controlling characters as confirmation of a rendered service.
