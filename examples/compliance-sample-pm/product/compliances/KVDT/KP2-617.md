## KP2-617 — Transmission of ICD-10-GM Code for Billing Transparency for GOPen 11302[G-alpha], 11303[G-alpha], 19402[G-alpha], 32901-32918[G-alpha]

|-------|-------|
| **ID** | KP2-617 |
| **Type** | Conditional Mandatory |
| **Source** | KVDT v6.06 |

| **Status** | TBC |

### Requirement

The software must enable the specification of the "Type of disease" coded per ICD-10-GM for GOPen 11302[G-alpha], 11303[G-alpha], 19402[G-alpha], 32901[G-alpha], 32902[G-alpha], 32904[G-alpha], 32906[G-alpha], 32908[G-alpha], 32910[G-alpha], 32911[G-alpha], 32915[G-alpha], 32916[G-alpha], 32917[G-alpha], and 32918[G-alpha].

### Rationale

According to EBM billing provisions for GOPen 11302[G-alpha], 11303[G-alpha], 19402[G-alpha], 32901[G-alpha], 32902[G-alpha], 32904[G-alpha], 32906[G-alpha], 32908[G-alpha], 32910[G-alpha], 32911[G-alpha], 32915[G-alpha], 32916[G-alpha], 32917[G-alpha], and 32918[G-alpha], the "Type of disease coded per ICD-10-GM" is to be specified.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. All provisions of function [KP2-618](KP2-618.md) apply.
2. The software transmits with the billing for the above-mentioned GOPen not the fields FK 5070 and FK 5077 and FK 5079.
