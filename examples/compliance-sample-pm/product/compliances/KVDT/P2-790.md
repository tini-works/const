## P2-790 — PKV Card — Exclusion from GKV Billing

### Requirement

1. Immediately after reading a "Card für Privatversicherte" (card for privately insured), a system-side notice must indicate that private insurance is present.
2. The read data from a "Card für Privatversicherte" must not flow into the processing routines for KVDT billing.

### Notes

A PKV card is recognizable with KVK storage cards by the data fields for VKNR and IK being filled with zeros.

A PKV card is recognizable with an eGK by the content of the information element Version_XML of the container EF.StatusVD per [gematik Speicherstrukturen der eGK für die Fachanwendung VSDM], indicating that it contains the Versichertenstammdaten-Schema-Version.
