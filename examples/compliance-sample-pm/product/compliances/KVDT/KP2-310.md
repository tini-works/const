### Requirement

The software ensures during an insurance switch (see [P2-530](P2-530.md)) of a patient already present in the system that immediately after reading the new insurance card, the user is notified of the insurance switch.

### Rationale

The user is to be informed about the insurance switch of the insured person already present in the system, so that within the comparison routine, field values are not unknowingly overwritten.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. During an insurance switch of a patient already present in the system, immediately after reading the new insurance card:
   a) the user is notified of the insurance switch through a warning
   b) each individual field -- defined in [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Mapping_KVK] -- of the insurance card is compared with the existing data in the PVS. In case of data deviations, the differences are to be displayed field-specifically in the master data screen.
