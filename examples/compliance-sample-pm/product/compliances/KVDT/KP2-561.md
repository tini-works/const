### Requirement

The software must enable the user to input, store, and transfer the "original Auftragstext" of the referring gynecologist from the "Auftrag" section of Muster 39.

### Rationale

On January 1, 2020, the organized program for early detection of cervical carcinoma was launched. The basis is the Richtlinie für organisierte Krebsfrüherkennungsprogramme (oKFE-RL). The differentiated (partial) orders (specifically: Zytologie or KO-Test or HPV-Test) in Primärscreening or in Abklärungsdiagnostik are entered by the referring gynecologist in the Auftrag section of Muster 39.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If a Muster 39 (= Satzart 0102 with Scheinuntergruppe 21) is captured by the user, the software shall request the user to adopt the Auftragsinformationen of the referring gynecologist from the "Auftrag" section of Muster 39.
   a) The software prompts the user to enter or select the following information during manual entry:
      i. First, for "Abrechnungsart":
         - P = for Primärscreening
         - A = for Abklärungsdiagnostik
      ii. Second, for the "Auftrag":
         - Zyto = zytologische Untersuchung
         - HPV = HPV-Test
         - KoTest = Ko-Test
      The software combines both pieces of information with a hyphen "-" for transfer into field FK 4205 (e.g. "P-HPV").
   b) The software may also allow direct entry of the values P-HPV, P-Zyto, P-KoTest, A-HPV, A-Zyto or A-KoTest.
2. The software does not pre-populate the entry field with a default value (e.g. "Zytologische Untersuchung") nor does the billing automatically transfer a default value.
3. If Auftragsinformationen are imported into the software via Satzart 8215 "Auftrag" (LDT 3), the software may automatically adopt the content from fields FK 8630 and FK 8629 of the object "Obj_0034 (Obj_Krebsfrueherkennung Zervix-Karzinom (Muster 39))" combined with a hyphen "-" into field FK 4205 (KVDT) and transfer with the billing.
