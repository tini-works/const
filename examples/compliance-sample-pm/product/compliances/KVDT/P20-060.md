### Requirement

At a prominent location, e.g., in connection with an already existing billing statistics or similar, the user shall be explicitly able to access a statistic with at least the following content:

a) Facility-individual material-analyte combinations for which a certificate is present,
b) Facility-individual material-analyte combinations for which no certificate is present,
c) Facility-individual material-analyte combinations that are exclusively examined within the framework of pnSD/uu,
d) Other material-analyte combinations that are obviously not examined at the facility.
