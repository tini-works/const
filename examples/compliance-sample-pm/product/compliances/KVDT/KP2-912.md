### Requirement

Instead of minor surgery services, the higher-valued Category-1 services from EBM chapters 31.2 or 36.2 may also be billed when these services are performed under anesthesia in children up to the completed 12th year of life.

Since no OPS is available as justification for Category-1 services in these cases, a billing system must ensure that these are alternatively justified with the GNR of the minor surgery and transmitted via FK 5036 in the billing.

### Condition

Exempt from implementation of this requirement are software systems without APK.

### Notes

In the GOS, this circumstance is represented for the relevant Category-1 services through the supplementary condition "5036" (Justification-GNR), whereby the permissible numbers of minor surgery that may be used as justification are listed under the justification list.
