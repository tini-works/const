### Requirement

The software must verify the validity of a cost unit (specifically: the validity of a Kostentraeger-Abrechnungsbereich for the given cost unit).

### Rationale

The Kostentraeger-Abrechnungsbereich specifies the contractual agreement or legal regulation on the basis of which billing is to be performed.

This requirement results from contractual regulations between a cost unit and the Kassenaerztliche Vereinigung(en).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software checks the following conditions:
   a) IK_v is an IK from the insurance card and present under `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/@V` in the KT-Stammdatei
   b) LQ is the service quarter
   c) KTAB_y is a KT-Abrechnungsbereich assignable to the patient and a validity period for the KT-Abrechnungsbereich exists under `/kostentraeger/kt_abrechnungsbereich_liste/kt_abrechnungsbereich/gueltigkeit/@V`
   - **If** LQ lies above the validity period `/kostentraeger/kt_abrechnungsbereich_liste/kt_abrechnungsbereich/gueltigkeit/@V`, **then**:
2. A system-generated **error message** is issued that the Kostentraeger-Abrechnungsbereich for the given cost unit is no longer valid.
3. The software does not support direct further processing for the purpose of ADT billing at the expense of this cost unit with this KT-Abrechnungsbereich.
4. The software does not support the issuance of contractual physician forms or BFB forms at the expense of this cost unit with this KT-Abrechnungsbereich.
