### Requirement

In the case of a referral for in-vitro-diagnostic services (IVD-Leistungen) via Muster 10 or 39, the Behandlungstag bei IVD-Leistungen should be transferred in field FK 4214 ("Behandlungstag bei IVD-Leistungen") in the Abrechnungsdatensatz. The rule also applies to related services per Abschnitt 32.2 EBM via Muster 10A.

### Rationale

Per the Allgemeine Bestimmungen 3.8.5 of the EBM, for in-vitro-diagnostic services the day of Probenentnahme counts as Behandlungstag. For applying billing rules (e.g. "once per Behandlungstag"), it is thus -- independent of the day on which all obligatory Leistungsinhalte were fully performed (Leistungstag) -- to be based on the day of Probenentnahme.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If a referral matching the following criteria is captured:
   a) Muster 10 (= Satzart 0102 with Scheinuntergruppe 27) or
   b) Muster 10A (= Satzart 0102 with Scheinuntergruppe 28) or
   c) Muster 39a (= Satzart 0102 with Scheinuntergruppe 21)
   the software must request the user to adopt the Behandlungstag of the referrer, unless it does not support capture per acceptance criteria (3) and (4).
2. The software transfers the Behandlungstag captured by the user to the billing in field FK 4214.
3. The value of Behandlungstag for IVD-Leistungen must correspond to either the Probenentnahmedatum or the Ausstellungsdatum of the referral or the Probeneingangsdatum at the sending practice during automatic capture. The following priority applies:
   - 1st priority: Probenentnahmedatum
   - 2nd priority (if Probenentnahmedatum unknown): either Ausstellungsdatum or Probeneingangsdatum
4. The software can support capture of Behandlungstag for IVD-Leistungen as follows:
   a) If a Probenentnahmedatum for a referral per acceptance criterion 1 is known, the software may automatically capture and transfer it for the user in field FK 4214 with the billing.
   b) If no Probenentnahmedatum is available, the software may automatically capture the Ausstellungsdatum instead and transfer it in field FK 4214.
   c) If no Probenentnahmedatum for a referral is available, the software may automatically capture the Probeneingangsdatum for the user and transfer it in field FK 4214.
5. The user must have the ability to adjust the automatically adopted date for field FK 4214.
6. If capture of the date for field FK 4214 is done manually by the user, the software shows the user the priorities per acceptance criterion 3.
7. If the date of FK 4214 is greater than the date of FK 5000, the software must display a warning to the user. The date of FK 4214 must not lie in the future.
8. If the date of FK 4214 is more than 60 days less than the date of FK 5000, the software must display a warning to the user. The difference between FK 4214 (Behandlungstag bei IVD-Leistungen) and FK 5000 (Leistungstag) should not be greater than 60 days.
