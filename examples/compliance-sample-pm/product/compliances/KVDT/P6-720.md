### Requirement

The software must provide the user with the ability to modify or extend the EBM data basis.

### Rationale

Due to contract closures during the quarter or errors in the delivered EBM master data file, extension or correction measures in the data master may become necessary to avoid billing problems.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must provide the user with the ability to modify or extend the EBM data basis.

### Notes

Correction measures or extensions of the EBM data basis may also be performed by the software vendor as a service for their users as needed.
