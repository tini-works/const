### Requirement

The software ensures that for combination treatment by two psychotherapists within the framework of psychotherapy, the user explicitly specifies the combination treatment as "Individual therapy component in treatment by two psychotherapists" (FK 4251 = 3) or as "Group therapy component in treatment by two psychotherapists" (FK 4251 = 4).

### Rationale

Due to the amendment of the provisions in Annex 1 of the BMV-Ae (Psychotherapy Agreement) and the revision of Form PTV 2, the user must specify their share of the combination treatment by two psychotherapists as either "Individual therapy component" or "Group therapy component."

### Acceptance Criteria

1. When documenting the combination treatment by two psychotherapists, the user must explicitly indicate whether their share of the combination treatment involves:
   a) "Individual therapy component in treatment by two psychotherapists" (FK 4251 = 3)
   b) or "Group therapy component in treatment by two psychotherapists" (FK 4251 = 4).
