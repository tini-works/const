### Requirement

The (update) version of the KV-Specifika master data files delivered in the current quarter is valid for use starting from the beginning of the following quarter (**not** for the current billing quarter) and must be deployed at the beginning of the following quarter (not earlier!).

**Annotation:** The earliest possible deployment timing of the **cost carrier** master data file is regulated differently. Because of the "history tracking" in the KT master data file, this file may be deployed directly after delivery.

**Example SDKV:**
**DELIVERY** Mid 2nd quarter 20xx
**DEPLOYMENT IN PRACTICE** Beginning 3rd quarter 20xx (not earlier!!)
