### Requirement

If an approved psychotherapy with remaining contingent exists and no APK has taken place for two quarters, the software must notify the user about the specification of Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131.

### Rationale

This requirement results from section 17 para. 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement (Annex 1 BMV-Ae).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If for an existing approved psychotherapy with remaining contingent no APK has taken place for at least two quarters, the software shall notify the user every quarter at the latest during billing creation about the specification of Pseudo-GOP 88130 or 88131, provided the Pseudo-GOP has not yet been recorded in the current quarter.
   a) Only approved psychotherapies with remaining contingent shall be displayed to the user.
2. Acceptance criteria 2, 3, and 4 of function [KP2-966](KP2-966.md) apply.
