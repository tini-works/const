### Requirement

Relating to the various reference periods (treatment day, cycle case, ...), final checks are permitted.

1. System-side changes of GNRs (regarding GNRs documented by the user) may be performed through final checks based on the (check) conditions and rules of the EBM master data file.
2. The user must be able to recognize system-side changes to service documentation and, if applicable, reverse them.
3. The storage of services in the ADT billing file must also be possible **against the (check) conditions and rules** of the EBM master data file.
