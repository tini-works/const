### Requirement

The software must ensure that for OPS code 5-662.5, a side localization is always transmitted.

### Rationale

The deviation of the side specification in Annex 2 or in the GO master data from the decision of the Bewertungsausschuss per § 87 Abs. 1 Satz 1 SGB V to amend the Einheitlichen Bewertungsmaßstabes (EBM) in its 167th session (written resolution), effective January 1, 2009, in which it was agreed that for the specification of OPS 5-622.5 in the contractual physician care area, a corresponding specification of side localization R (right) or L (left) is to be provided for the specification of the operative measure.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software ensures that for OPS code 5-622.5, a side specification is always required and transmitted.
   a) The side specification is transmitted in FK 5041.

### Condition

Excluded from the implementation of this requirement are software systems without APK.

### Notes

The provisions of requirement [KP6-871](KP6-871.md) do not apply for OPS code 5-622.5.
