### Requirement

The software ensures that the cost carrier with VKNR 74799 is not transmitted to the KVs as part of billing.

### Rationale

For testing purposes by gematik, the cost carrier with VKNR 74799 was included in the cost carrier master data file.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software processes eGKs with the cost carrier (VKNR = 74799). This includes, for example, reading in and adopting the insured person's data.
2. The software does **not** support the printing/issuance of contractual physician forms with the data of the cost carrier (VKNR = 74799).
3. The software ensures that the cost carrier is not written to and transmitted in the billing file.
