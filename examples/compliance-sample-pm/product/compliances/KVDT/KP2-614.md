### Requirement

The software must enable the specification of the HGNC gene symbol (FK 5077) and the type of disease (FK 5079) for GOPen 11513[G-alpha] and 11522[G-alpha].

### Rationale

The calculation of GOPen 11513 and 11522 requires the justification that contains the type of disease, and the specification of the type of examination (HGNC gene symbol).

### Acceptance Criteria

All provisions of functions [KP2-621](KP2-621.md) and [KP2-624](KP2-624.md) apply, subject to the following criteria:

1. The software system must require the user, when capturing GOPen 11513[G-alpha] and 11522[G-alpha], to provide **at least** one HGNC gene symbol (FK 5077) **and** one "Type of disease" (FK 5079) within the scope of service documentation.
   a) The user may also specify multiple HGNC gene symbols and multiple entries for "Type of disease" for the mentioned GOPen.
   b) If multiple entries for "Type of disease" are made, the software system must transmit field 5079 multiple times in the billing file.
2. For entering multiple HGNC gene symbols for the mentioned GOPen, HGNC gene symbol chains according to function [KP2-623](KP2-623.md) can be used.
