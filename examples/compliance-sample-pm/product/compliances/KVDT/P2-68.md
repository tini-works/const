## P2-68 — Transmission of Connector Certificate Expiry Date and Display of Device Certificates

### Requirement

The software must display the expiry date of the certificate stored in the connector to the user and transmit this date in the ADT billing.

### Rationale

The Kassenärztliche Vereinigungen can use this information to support practices with appropriate measures in connection with the expiry of the connector certificate.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software implements requirements A_22917, A_22918, A_22969 and A_13533-01 according to the gematik specifications [gematik Implementierungsleitfaden Primärsysteme Telematikinfrastruktur] and requirement TIP1-A_4695-03 according to the gematik specifications [Spezifikation Konnektor].
2. The software reads the expiry date of the connector certificate from the connector deployed in a (subsidiary) practice site and transmits the date in Field FK 0227 ("Ablaufdatum des Konnektorzertifikats").

### Notes

It is possible that more than one connector is present in a (subsidiary) practice site. The connector selected for billing transmission should correspond to the one meeting Acceptance Criterion 1 b) of requirement [P2-66](P2-66.md). If all connectors present in a (subsidiary) practice site have the same compatibility-relevant components of the product type version, the software transmits the earliest expiry date. The display should present the expiry dates of all available connectors clearly.
