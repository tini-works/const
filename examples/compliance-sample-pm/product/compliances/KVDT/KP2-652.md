## KP2-652 — Capture and Transmission of Implant Register Information After Successful Registration

### Requirement

The software must enable the user to capture, for an inserted implant, the Melde-ID, the "Hash-String", and the "Hash-Wert" of the registration confirmation of the implant register after successful registration in the implant register, and to transmit them service-related in the billing.

### Rationale

In the Implant Register Act (IRegG) and in the Implant Register Operating Regulation (IRe-gBV), it is stipulated that healthcare institutions must transmit data to the implant register after an implant-related procedure. Medical practices must transmit, as proof of this notification in the billing, the Melde-ID, the "Hash-String", and the "Hash-Wert" of the registration confirmation of the implant register.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must provide the user the ability to capture one or more "Melde-IDs" including the respective "Hash-Strings" and "Hash-Wertes" from the registration confirmation of the implant register on a service-related basis.
2. The software system must require the user, when capturing a GOP (in FK 5001) within the scope of service documentation, to provide the "Melde-ID Implantateregister" (FK 5050) if the value `@V="5050"` is stored for the GOP in the EBM-Stammdatei under `../gnr_zusatzangaben/gnr_zusatzangaben_liste/gnr_zusatzangabe/@V="5050"`.
3. The software system must require the user, when capturing a GOP (in FK 5001) within the scope of service documentation, to provide the "Hash-String Implantateregister" (FK 5051) if the value `@V="5051"` is stored for the GOP in the EBM-Stammdatei under `../gnr_zusatzangaben/gnr_zusatzangaben_liste/gnr_zusatzangabe/@V="5051"`.
4. The software system must require the user, when capturing a GOP (in FK 5001) within the scope of service documentation, to provide the "Hash-Wert Implantateregister" (FK 5052) if the value `@V="5052"` is stored for the GOP in the EBM-Stammdatei under `../gnr_zusatzangaben/gnr_zusatzangaben_liste/gnr_zusatzangabe/@V="5052"`.
5. The software may additionally support the user, in complement to acceptance criterion 1 and considering acceptance criteria 2, 3, and 4 during capture, by automatically adopting the "Melde-IDs" including the respective "Hash-Strings" and "Hash-Wertes" after notification of an implant-related procedure from the registration confirmation of the implant register into the fields "Melde-ID Implantateregister" (FK 5050), "Hash-String Implantateregister" (FK 5051), and "Hash-Wert Implantateregister" (FK 5052).
   a) If automatic capture occurs, the software may suppress manual adjustment of automatically adopted values.
6. The software transmits the "Melde-ID of the registration confirmation of the implant register" in the billing in field identifier 5050.
   a) If the user captures more than one Melde-ID, the software transmits each Melde-ID in a separate field identifier 5050 in the billing.
   b) The software ensures that each Melde-ID (FK 5050) also includes the child elements "Hash-String Implantateregister" (FK 5051) and "Hash-Wert Implantateregister" (FK 5052).
7. The software transmits the "Hash-String of the registration confirmation of the implant register" in the billing in field identifier 5051.
8. The software transmits the "Hash-Wert of the registration confirmation of the implant register" in the billing in field identifier 5052.

### Condition

Exempt from implementation of this requirement are software systems without APK.

### Notes

Under [BMG_Implantatenregister_Spezifikation], the technical information for connecting to the implant register is published.

If from the first quarter of 2025 onward, late cases from the year 2023/2024 are to be billed and the transmission of implant register data is technically necessary, the data must be transmitted in the structure valid from the first quarter of 2025.
