### Requirement

The printing of the personnel field of the contract physician preprint Form 6 (referral form) is pseudonymized by specifying the Identifier-SA. The Identifier-SA must be printed in print line 2 (field: Last Name) of the personnel field.

### Notes

For blank form printing, the barcode is omitted.
