### Requirement

If the patient's gender is "undetermined" or "diverse", the software must not check whether a gender reference is defined in the GO master data file for the GOP specified by the user as part of service documentation, nor verify matching of this gender data.

### Rationale

The legal basis is § 22, Abs. 3 of the Personenstandsgesetz.

Persons with variants of gender development can be assigned to neither the female nor the male gender. In this case, a patient may have an 'undetermined' or 'diverse' gender. Therefore, patients with "undetermined or diverse gender" cannot fundamentally be excluded from gender-specific services.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If the patient's gender is "undetermined" or "diverse" (content of field FK 3110 equals X or D), the software allows the user to enter a GOP even with a deviating gender definition for this GOP in the V-attribute of the element `/gnr/bedingung/administrative_gender_cd` of the GO master data file.
   a) The software does not display a warning notice and transfers the GOP specified by the user to the billing.
