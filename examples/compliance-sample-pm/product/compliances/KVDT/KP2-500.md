### Requirement

Upon **first reading of the insurance card** of an insured person in a quarter, the system must require entry of the billable **"Satzart 010x" or "Scheinuntergruppe"**. This can occur either in direct connection with the reading process or during the first capture of billing-relevant data.

### Notes

Excluded from implementing this requirement are software systems without APK.
