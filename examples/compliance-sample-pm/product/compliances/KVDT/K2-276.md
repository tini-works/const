### Requirement

1. If the billing-VKNR transmitted by the Kassenaerztliche Vereinigung for an "unknown IK" is already present in the official KT-Stammdatei, then this IK may be added to the corresponding master data record in the official KT-Stammdatei together with the associated KT-Abrechnungsbereich.
2. Erroneously made extensions must be correctable by the user.
