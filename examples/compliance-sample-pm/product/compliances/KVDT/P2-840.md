### Requirement

Due to regulations to prevent excessive expansion of his activities, the physician typically receives compensation for only a portion of rendered services. This circumstance should also be reflected on the patient receipt.

### Configurability

The physician can specify an empirical value from recent quarters. This value must be managed by the PVS and must be taken into account when calculating the physician's fee.

If the physician does not specify a quotation, the following standard text shall be positioned below the first paragraph on the patient receipt:

1. *"The amount stated below for the medical services I have rendered will, due to the limitation of the health insurance funds' financial resources, potentially only be partially paid to me. The payment will subsequently be reduced by the health insurance fund to the extent that the funds made available by your health insurance fund are sufficient."*

If the physician specifies a quota (e.g., 5%), the text block (1) on the patient receipt shall be replaced by the following text block; the quota is incorporated into the text (here: X):

2. *"The amount stated below for the medical services I have rendered will, due to the limitation of the health insurance funds' financial resources, potentially only be partially paid to me. The payment will subsequently be reduced by the health insurance fund by X %, so that the funds made available by your health insurance fund are sufficient."*
