### Requirement

IK_v is an IK from the insurance card and is not present in the KT-Stammdatei (i.e. IK_v does not match any IK under element `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/@V` in the KT-Stammdatei).

Then:

1. A system-generated **WARNING** is issued requesting the user to contact the respective Kassenaerztliche Vereinigung.
2. **Temporary master data record:**
   Based on the insurance card presented and/or additional information provided by the Kassenaerztliche Vereinigung, the required data for the relevant cost unit are either:
   a) manually created as a **temporary KT-Stammsatz**, or
   b) the corresponding **IK is added** to an existing master data record.

### Notes

The "unknown" IK is presumably a new cost unit.
