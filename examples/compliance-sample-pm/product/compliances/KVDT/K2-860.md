### Requirement

1. Immediately after or during a physician-patient contact, a service listing of the just-rendered services is produced by means of a patient receipt (day-based).
2. This service listing can also accumulate all services rendered so far in the quarter (form-based).
3. If the patient has not yet completed the 15th year of life, the user must have the ability to exclude individual services from the patient receipt.
   a) A notice about incompleteness of the service listing must not be displayed on the patient receipt.

### Notes

For the group of persons mentioned in acceptance criterion 3, by analogy to the KBV guideline for transmission and storage of data in the ePA [KBV_Richtlinie_§ 75 Abs 1 Nr. Z SGB V], the omission of listing fee schedule positions on the patient receipt does not violate contractual physician obligations, provided significant therapeutic reasons oppose it or significant indications of danger to the welfare of a child or adolescent exist and the listing of fee schedule positions would jeopardize the effective protection of the child or adolescent.
