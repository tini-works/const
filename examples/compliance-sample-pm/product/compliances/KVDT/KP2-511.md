### Requirement

The software must provide the user with the ability, when mediating an appointment to a specialist, to transfer the Betriebsstättennummer of that specialist as rationale for a GOP into the billing.

### Rationale

Physicians participating in contractual medical care are obligated to transmit the Arztnummer of the specialist to whom the appointment was mediated when billing for services for the mediation of a medically urgent treatment appointment (see § 295 Absatz 1 Satz 1 Nr. 3 SGB V). The operationalization occurs through specific Gebührenordnungspositionen 03008 and 04008 of the EBM and the specification of the Betriebsstättennummer of the specialist to whom the appointment was mediated. For this purpose, KVDT-Feld 5003 "(N)BSNR des vermittelten Facharztes" is used so that the Betriebsstättennummer information can be directly assigned to the surcharge.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software offers the user the ability, when mediating an appointment to a specialist, to transfer the Betriebsstättennummer of that specialist as rationale for a GOP into the billing.
2. The software must allow the user to:
   a) enter the Betriebsstättennummer as free text, or
   b) copy and insert the Betriebsstättennummer from a search result, for example from [KBV_Kollegensuche], or
   c) adopt the Betriebsstättennummer from the data per [KP2-508](KP2-508.md).
3. The software transfers the specified Betriebsstättennummer into the billing in Feld 5003.
