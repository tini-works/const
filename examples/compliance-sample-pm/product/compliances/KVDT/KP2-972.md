### Requirement

If an ongoing approved psychotherapy is interrupted, the software shall offer the user the ability to record this information and store it in the system.

### Rationale

This requirement results from section 10 para. 6 and 7 of the Psychotherapy Agreement (Annex 1 BMV-Ae).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must offer the user the ability to record the start date of the interruption of an ongoing approved psychotherapy, as well as the presence of an informal justification to the health insurance fund per section 17 para. 6 of the Psychotherapy Agreement, and to store it in the system.
2. The software must offer the user the ability to record the end date of the interruption of an ongoing approved psychotherapy and to store it in the system.
