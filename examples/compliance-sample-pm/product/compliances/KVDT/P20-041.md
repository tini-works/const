### Requirement

For validation of the facility-individual analytes, a comparison with the service documentation shall be possible. The billing shall be parsed (note: facility-related!) for potential GOPs that are linked to RV analytes according to the key table [S_NVV_RV_ZERTIFIKAT, OID 1.2.276.0.76.3.1.1.5.2.22]. The thus potentially relevant analytes shall be highlighted in color, taking into account the previously specified RV materials used (not to be confused with pre-assignment!).

### Notes

Analogous to the validation of RV analytes against service documentation, the same functionality can be used to support the user in selecting analytes. Thus, it would be possible, prior to marking the relevant analytes, to first perform a validation against the service documentation to highlight potentially relevant analytes in color and thereby direct the user to the most likely analytes.

This "suggestion method" is particularly useful toward the end of the quarter when the service documentation reflects the entire service spectrum. Practically, the service spectrum could be captured with additional information from previous quarters.

### Clarification

The color highlighting must under no circumstances be interpreted as an error being detected; it shall only represent a visual aid. The GOPs can generally also be combined with any other, non-RV-relevant materials and could therefore also be legitimately billed without an RV certificate.
