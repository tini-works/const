### Requirement

The "Anforderungskatalog Formularbedruckung" [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_Formularbedruckung] applies mandatorily for software used to prescribe remedies for contract physician practices that are authorized to create prescriptions.

### Rationale

Contract physicians must prescribe remedy prescriptions exclusively on the form agreed upon per § 87 paragraph 1 sentence 2 SGB V (Muster 13).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software implements all required requirements of the requirements catalog "Anforderungskatalog Formularbedruckung", cf. [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_Formularbedruckung] regarding the printing of Muster 13.
