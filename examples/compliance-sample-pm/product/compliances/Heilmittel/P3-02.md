### Requirement

The software system documents all remedies prescriptions made by the user.

### Rationale

The user must have the ability to access the patient-specific individual prescription history.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software documents all issued prescriptions on a patient-specific basis.
2. The software must document the following information of a prescription:
   - Personal data fields as per Technischer Anlage zur Anlage 4a (BMV-A) [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Mapping_KVK]
   - Date of issue of the prescription
   - Remedies discipline (physiotherapy, podiatric therapy, speech-language-swallowing therapy, occupational therapy, or nutritional therapy) as per [P3-10](P3-10.md)
   - Home visit as per [P3-14](P3-14.md)
   - Therapy report as per [P3-15](P3-15.md)
   - Treatment-relevant diagnosis/es (ICD-10-GM code) as per [P3-06](P3-06.md)
   - Treatment-relevant diagnosis/es (plain text/free text) as per [P3-12](P3-12.md)
   - Diagnosis group as per [P3-07](P3-07.md)
   - Key symptom (letter-coded and patient-individual) as per [P3-08](P3-08.md)
   - Key symptom (plain text) as per [P3-08](P3-08.md)
   - Prescribed priority remedies as per [P3-19](P3-19.md) and [P3-21](P3-21.md) (incl. free text for further specification as per [P3-21](P3-21.md) point (6))
   - Prescribed supplementary remedy as per [P3-22](P3-22.md) and [P3-23](P3-23.md)
   - Prescribed standardized remedy combination as per [P3-24](P3-24.md)
   - Number of treatment units (per remedy) as per [P3-11](P3-11.md)
   - Therapy frequency as per [P3-13](P3-13.md)
   - If applicable, therapy goals / further medical findings and notes as per [P3-21](P3-21.md)
   - Urgent treatment need as per [P3-17](P3-17.md)
   - Indicator of special prescription need as per [P3-18](P3-18.md)
   - Indicator of long-term remedies need as per [P3-18](P3-18.md)
   - Date of an acute event as per [P3-18](P3-18.md) point (7)
   - Patient-specific approval of long-term remedies need as per [P3-01](P3-01.md) and [P3-37](P3-37.md)
   - Cancellation date of the prescription as per [P3-03](P3-03.md)
   - Blank prescription as per [P3-16](P3-16.md)
3. Additionally, it must be possible to store supplementary information regarding the prescription.
4. The user must have access to the documented prescriptions.

### Notes

The usual documentation obligations within the scope of contractual medical practice remain unaffected.

In the documentation, it must be distinguishable whether a remedy was prescribed as a priority or supplementary remedy. Documented standardized remedy combinations are uniquely identifiable by their designation (SDHM Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/standardisierte_heilmittel_kombination/name/@V).
