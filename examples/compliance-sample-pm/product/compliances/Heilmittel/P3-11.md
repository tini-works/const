### Requirement

The user must be given the option, within the scope of the prescription in the field "Behandlungseinheiten" (treatment units), to specify the number of treatment units per remedy. The maximum quantity per prescription and the guiding treatment quantity must be observed.

### Rationale

The remedy catalog contains a maximum quantity per prescription for each diagnosis group. This maximum quantity per prescription must not be exceeded as a matter of principle, but may be fallen short of.

When prescribing measures of Physiotherapy, Voice-, Speech-, Language- and Swallowing Therapy, and Occupational Therapy, multiple "priority remedies" may be prescribed simultaneously. The allocation of the prescription quantity is to be specified on a single prescription form. The sum of the prescription quantities of the individual priority remedies in the field "Behandlungseinheiten" must not exceed the value of the maximum quantity per prescription according to the Heilmittel-Stammdatei (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/hoechstmenge_verordnung/@V).

Exceptions for the determination of the maximum quantity per prescription:

Deviating from the above, for insured persons with a long-term remedy need according to § 8, the necessary remedies per prescription may be prescribed for a treatment duration of up to 12 weeks. This also applies to prescriptions based on ICD-10 codes, in conjunction with the corresponding diagnosis group, that establish a special prescription need according to § 106b paragraph 2 sentence 4 SGB V. If subject to an age restriction, the age of the insured person is also relevant in determining the maximum prescription quantity per prescription. The number of treatment units to be prescribed is to be measured depending on the therapy frequency. If a frequency range is specified on the prescription, the highest value of the range is to be used for determining the maximum prescription quantity. The guiding treatment quantity per the remedy catalog is not to be considered.

For the prescription of measures of nutritional therapy, there are no specifications for the maximum quantity per prescription in the Heilmittel-Richtlinie of the G-BA. However, the physician must specify a value on the prescription according to the guidelines of the Heilmittel-Richtlinie of the G-BA, which can be determined based on the therapy frequency for a treatment duration of up to 12 weeks.

For prescriptions of podology (diagnosis groups DF, NF, and QF) and nutritional therapy, there are no guiding treatment quantities defined in the Heilmittel-Richtlinie of the G-BA.

The prescription quantity of a "standardized remedy combination" and measures of massage therapy according to § 18 paragraph 3 number 1 through 6 are limited to 12 units each per prescription case, provided the remedy catalog does not specify anything different.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Within a prescription, the prescription quantity (see glossary) must be calculated from the treatment units entered by the user. The prescription quantity per prescription must not exceed the maximum quantity per prescription (SDHM XML-Element: ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/hoechstmenge_verordnung/@V), though the value may be lower. When selecting a priority remedy and, if applicable, an additionally prescribed supplementary remedy (per [P3-21](P3-21.md) or [P3-22](P3-22.md)), a standardized remedy combination (per [P3-24](P3-24.md)), or an independently prescribed supplementary remedy (per [P3-23](P3-23.md)), the software must notify the user that the entered value of the priority remedy, standardized remedy combination, or independently prescribed supplementary remedy is too high and replace the too-high value with the actual maximum permissible value. Ending the prescription process with a value that is too high is not permissible.
    a. If no maximum quantity per prescription is assigned to a diagnosis group (SDHM XML-Element: ../diagnosegruppe/), this check does not apply.
    b. Within a prescription that meets at least one of the following criteria, this check does not apply (see point (10)).
        1. A selected ICD-10-GM code, the diagnosis group, and an optional existing age criterion fulfill the criteria per the BVB-/LHM-Stammdatei (SDHMA XML-Element ../verordnungsbedarf/icd_code/@V corresponds to the selected code and the XML-Element ../verordnungsbedarf/heilmittel_liste/heilmittel/sekundaercode/@V is not defined, and the XML-Element ../kapitel/diagnosegruppe_liste/diagnosegruppe/@V corresponds to the selected diagnosis group and the optional SDHMA XML-Elements ../verordnungsbedarf/heilmittel_liste/heilmittel/untere_altersgrenze/@V and/or ../verordnungsbedarf/heilmittel_liste/heilmittel/obere_altersgrenze/@V correspond to the age of the patient).
            i. If, due to an incomplete or missing date of birth of the patient, the fulfillment of an age condition cannot be unambiguously assessed (e.g., if the date of birth is unknown), the age criterion is assumed as not fulfilled, meaning the check applies.
            ii. For special prescription needs that apply only regionally (per SDHMA XML-Element ../verordnungsbedarf/geltungsbereich_kv/@V), the check applies.
        2. Two selected ICD-10-GM codes, the diagnosis group, and an optional existing age criterion fulfill the criteria per BVB-/LHM-Stammdatei (SDHMA XML-Elements ../verordnungsbedarf/icd_code/@V and ../verordnungsbedarf/heilmittel_liste/heilmittel/sekundaercode/@V correspond to the selected codes, and the XML-Element ../kapitel/diagnosegruppe_liste/diagnosegruppe/@V corresponds to the selected diagnosis group and the optional SDHMA XML-Elements ../verordnungsbedarf/heilmittel_liste/heilmittel/untere_altersgrenze/@V and/or ../verordnungsbedarf/heilmittel_liste/heilmittel/obere_altersgrenze/@V correspond to the age of the patient).
            i. If, due to an incomplete or missing date of birth of the patient, the fulfillment of an age condition cannot be unambiguously assessed (e.g., if the date of birth is unknown), the age criterion is assumed as not fulfilled, meaning the check applies.
            ii. For special prescription needs that apply only regionally (per SDHMA XML-Element ../verordnungsbedarf/geltungsbereich_kv/@V), the check applies.
        3. The selected diagnosis group alone and an optional existing age criterion fulfill the criteria per BVB-/LHM-Stammdatei (SDHMA XML-Element ../kapitel/diagnosegruppe_liste/diagnosegruppe/@V corresponds to the selected diagnosis group and XML-Elements ../verordnungsbedarf/icd_code/@V and ../verordnungsbedarf/heilmittel_liste/heilmittel/sekundaercode/@V are not defined and the optional SDHMA XML-Elements ../verordnungsbedarf/heilmittel_liste/heilmittel/untere_altersgrenze/@V and/or ../verordnungsbedarf/heilmittel_liste/heilmittel/obere_altersgrenze/@V correspond to the age of the patient).
            i. If, due to an incomplete or missing date of birth of the patient, the fulfillment of an age condition cannot be unambiguously assessed (e.g., if the date of birth is unknown), the age criterion is assumed as not fulfilled, meaning the check applies.
            ii. For special prescription needs that apply only regionally (per SDHMA XML-Element ../verordnungsbedarf/geltungsbereich_kv/@V), the check applies.
        4. A valid patient-specific approval of long-term remedy need exists for a prescription to be issued, when:
            i. the information present in [P3-01](P3-01.md) point (1) regarding the approval of one or two ICD-10-GM codes are complete and the diagnosis group matches the corresponding information of the prescription,
            ii. all remedies specified on the prescription match the existing approval information, if at least one remedy is specified there,
            iii. the validity end date has not been reached at the time of the prescription,
            iv. no change of the cost bearer without a merger relationship to the previous cost bearer has taken place, and
            v. no dissolution or invalidity of the approving cost bearer is determined.
2. When selecting multiple priority remedies and, if applicable, an additionally prescribed supplementary remedy (per [P3-21](P3-21.md) or [P3-22](P3-22.md)), the software must allow the user to allocate the prescription quantity to the individual priority remedies and assign them to the individual form line fields. The sum of the individual treatment units of the priority remedies must not exceed the maximum quantity per prescription of the diagnosis group. The software must notify the user when the entered value is too high. Ending the prescription process is not permissible after notification display.
    a. If no maximum quantity per prescription is assigned to a diagnosis group (SDHM XML-Element: ../diagnosegruppe/), this check does not apply.
    b. Within a prescription that meets at least one of the criteria per point (1) b., this check does not apply (see point (10)).
3. The software must calculate the prescription quantity (see glossary) of all existing prescription cases to which the prescription can be assigned, by summing the prescription quantities of all prescriptions of the prescription case. The software must notify the user when any of the sums exceeds the specified guiding treatment quantity (SDHM XML-Element: ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge/@V). The following notification is to be displayed without interrupting the workflow:

    "Die orientierende Behandlungsmenge des Verordnungsfalls ist ueberschritten. Es sind weitere Verordnungen moeglich, sofern das angestrebte Therapieziel nicht erreicht wurde. In diesem Fall sind die individuellen medizinischen Gruende in die Patientendokumentation zu uebernehmen."

    ("The guiding treatment quantity of the prescription case has been exceeded. Further prescriptions are possible, provided the targeted therapy goal has not been reached. In this case, the individual medical reasons are to be documented in the patient documentation.")

    If a remedy of the diagnosis groups UI1 or UI2 (remedy area Podologische Therapie) (SDHM XML-Element ../kapitel/@V equal to "II. Maßnahmen der Podologischen Therapie") is prescribed, the following specific notification text is to be displayed instead, without interrupting the workflow:

    "Die orientierende Behandlungsmenge des Verordnungsfalls ist ueberschritten, welche sich auf einen Zehennagel bezieht. Es sind weitere Verordnungen moeglich, sofern das angestrebte Therapieziel nicht erreicht wurde oder mehrere Zehennaegel therapiert werden. In diesem Fall sind die individuellen medizinischen Gruende in die Patientendokumentation zu uebernehmen."

    ("The guiding treatment quantity of the prescription case is exceeded, which refers to a single toenail. Further prescriptions are possible, provided the targeted therapy goal has not been reached or multiple toenails are being treated. In this case, the individual medical reasons are to be documented in the patient documentation.")

    The flowchart below (see Figure 3) describes under which conditions a calculation of the guiding treatment quantity of the prescription case (per [P3-09](P3-09.md)) is performed.

    The following special considerations apply:
    a. The Heilmittel-Stammdatei may contain an additional value for the guiding treatment quantity (SDHM Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge_hoechstalter/@V) that applies for a specific maximum age of the patient (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge/hoechstalter_jahre/@V). The appropriate value is to be used based on the patient's age.
        i. The age is to be determined based on the prescription date of the respective prescription.
        ii. If, due to an incomplete or missing date of birth, the fulfillment of an age condition cannot be unambiguously assessed (e.g., if the date of birth is unknown), the smaller value for the guiding treatment quantity is to be used (SDHM XML-Element: ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge/@V or ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge_hoechstalter/@V).
    b. The Heilmittel-Stammdatei may contain an additional value for the guiding treatment quantity (SDHM Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge_icd_code/@V), which applies for a list of treatment-relevant diagnoses given in the form of their ICD-10-GM codes (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge/icd_code_liste/icd_code/@V). The existence of at least one of these diagnoses is a prerequisite for calculating this guiding treatment quantity. The matching of the specified ICD-10-GM codes is to be performed per [P3-06](P3-06.md) accordingly.
    c. For the diagnosis groups of Podological Therapy (per SDHM ../diagnosegruppe/@V equal to "DF" or "NF" or "QF") as well as Nutritional Therapy (per SDHM XML-Element ../kapitel/@V equal to "V. Maßnahmen der Ernaehrungstherapie"), no guiding treatment quantities are defined. No check is performed and no notification is given.
    d. For prescriptions for which the criteria per point (1) b. above do not apply, the guiding treatment quantity (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge/@V) is not to be considered. No notification is given.
    e. For a prescription that, based on the specification of two ICD-10-GM codes, can be assigned to two different prescription cases as a treatment-relevant diagnosis, the prescription quantity of both prescription cases must be calculated and compared with the guiding treatment quantity (see Figure 3).
4. Within a prescription of massage techniques (SDHM ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/vorrangiges_heilmittel_liste/vorrangiges_heilmittel/massagetechnik/@V or ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/ergaenzendes_heilmittel_liste/ergaenzendes_heilmittel/massagetechnik/@V equal to true), the entered treatment units including prior prescriptions of the same prescription case must not exceed the maximum prescription quantity for massage techniques (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge_massage/@V).

    Note: This principle also applies for prescriptions that fulfill the criteria of a special prescription need and/or long-term remedy need (per [P3-18](P3-18.md)) or when a valid patient-specific long-term approval exists (per [P3-01](P3-01.md)).

    See also note regarding [P3-09](P3-09.md) in the case of prescriptions with two treatment-relevant diagnoses.
5. Within a prescription of standardized remedy combinations (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/standardisierte_heilmittel_kombination/), the entered treatment units including prior prescriptions of the same prescription case must not exceed the maximum prescription quantity for standardized remedy combinations (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge_standardisiert/@V).

    Note: This principle also applies for prescriptions that fulfill the criteria of a special prescription need and/or long-term remedy need (per [P3-18](P3-18.md)) or when a valid patient-specific long-term approval exists (per [P3-01](P3-01.md)).

    See also note regarding [P3-09](P3-09.md) in the case of prescriptions with two treatment-relevant diagnoses.
6. If the user exceeds the defined prescription quantity per criteria (4) and (5) (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge_standardisiert/@V or SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/verordnungsmenge/orientierende_behandlungsmenge_massage/@V), the following notification must be displayed:

    "Die Verordnungsmenge fuer Massagetechniken/Standardisierte Heilmittelkombination ist fuer die gewaehlte Diagnosegruppe auf max. 12 Einheiten je Verordnungsfall begrenzt."

    ("The prescription quantity for massage techniques/standardized remedy combination is limited to a maximum of 12 units per prescription case for the selected diagnosis group.")

    Ending the prescription process (printing or saving) is not permissible after notification display.
7. A pre-population of treatment units based on data from the Heilmittel-Stammdatei is permissible. This pre-population must be overridable by the user during the prescription.
8. If, alongside "priority remedies", a "supplementary remedy" is prescribed, the treatment units of the "supplementary remedy" must not exceed the treatment units of the "priority remedy". If the entered value of the "supplementary remedy" is too high, the too-high value must be replaced with the actual maximum permissible value. Ending the prescription process with a value that is too high is not permissible.
9. Not assigned.
10. Within a prescription that meets at least one of the criteria per point (1) b., the following notification must be displayed to the user when the treatment units of the remedies divided by the therapy frequency per week, summed across all prescribed priority remedies or a single independently prescribed supplementary remedy, yields a value greater than 12.

    Sum from i=1 to n of (Behandlungseinheiten_i / Therapiefrequenz (Anzahl pro Woche)_i) > 12:

    "Die Verordnungsmenge ist in Abhaengigkeit von der Therapiefrequenz so zu bemessen, dass die Behandlungsdauer hoechstens 12 Wochen umfasst."

    ("The prescription quantity is to be measured depending on the therapy frequency such that the treatment duration covers a maximum of 12 weeks.")

    Ending the prescription process (printing or saving) is not permissible after notification display.
    a. When specifying the therapy frequency as a range, the highest value of the range is to be used for the calculation.
    b. If a recommended therapy frequency from the Heilmittel-Stammdatei is selected (per [P3-13](P3-13.md) point (1)), the "Minimum Number" parameter of the therapy frequency is to be used for the calculation if the therapy frequency specification does not contain a "Maximum Number". Otherwise, the latter is to be used (see [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Schnittstelle_SDHM]):
        i. Minimum Number:
            1. XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/frequenzempfehlung_liste/frequenzempfehlung/minimale_anzahl/@V
            2. It denotes the recommended minimum number, even when a maximum number is defined.
        ii. Maximum Number (optional):
            1. XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/frequenzempfehlung_liste/frequenzempfehlung/maximale_anzahl/@V
            2. It denotes the recommended maximum number and must be used when present for the calculation.
        iii. Frequency recommendation type:
            1. XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/frequenzempfehlung_liste/frequenzempfehlungstyp/@V
            2. The type of frequency recommendation defines whether the frequency is specified as a number of treatment units "per day" or "per week" or as a number of weeks between two treatment units.
    c. For the following diagnosis groups (SDHM XML-Element ../kapitel/diagnosegruppe/@V), the above calculation is not performed and the notification is not displayed: ST3, DF, NF, and QF.
    d. For the following diagnosis groups (SDHM XML-Element ../kapitel/diagnosegruppe/@V), the above calculation and notification are performed only if the therapy frequency was specified as a number of treatment units "per week" according to [P3-13](P3-13.md): SAS, CF, UI1, and UI2.
11. At the time when the prescription meets at least one of the criteria per point (1) b., the following notification must be displayed to the user without interrupting the workflow:

    "Die Kriterien, um von der Hoechstmenge je Verordnung abzuweichen, sind erfuellt. Die Anzahl der Behandlungseinheiten kann in Abhaengigkeit der Therapiefrequenz fuer eine Behandlungsdauer von bis zu 12 Wochen bemessen werden."

    ("The criteria to deviate from the maximum quantity per prescription are fulfilled. The number of treatment units may be measured depending on the therapy frequency for a treatment duration of up to 12 weeks.")

    Massage techniques prescribed within a standardized remedy combination are not counted in connection with acceptance criterion (4).

### Notes

Due to the exclusion of the calculation of possible prescription quantity in point (10) c., only recommended therapy frequencies that are specified as a number of treatment units "per week" are to be used for the calculation. Conversions from other frequency recommendation types are not necessary.
