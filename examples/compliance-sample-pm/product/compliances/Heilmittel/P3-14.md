### Requirement

The user must be given the option, within the scope of the prescription, to choose between the fields "Ja" (Yes) or "Nein" (No) under Hausbesuch (home visit).

### Rationale

Section § 11 of the HeilM-RL defines the conditions under which a home visit by the remedy service provider is permissible. As a rule, pre-populating with "Nein" can be considered appropriate, since it can be assumed that the majority of patients are able to independently visit the remedy service provider.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user must be given the option, within the scope of the prescription, to choose between the fields "Ja" or "Nein" under Hausbesuch.
2. The simultaneous selection of both fields is not permissible; exactly one field must always be selected.
3. A pre-population is permissible as follows:
    a. Based on the most recently completed prescription
    b. Based on user settings
4. The user must be able to override the pre-population during the prescription.
