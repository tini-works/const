### Requirement

The user must be given the option, during the creation of the prescription but at the latest at the time of the input dialog for the number of treatment units (see [P3-11](P3-11.md)), to record the patient-specific approval of long-term remedy need according to [P3-01](P3-01.md).

### Rationale

For prescriptions based on a "long-term remedy need" according to § 8 para. 3 of the HeilM-RL (patient-specific approval of long-term remedy need), it applies that the necessary remedies per prescription may be prescribed for a treatment duration of up to 12 weeks.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user must have the option, without interrupting the workflow and at the latest at the time of the input dialog for the number of treatment units (see [P3-11](P3-11.md)), to record information according to [P3-01](P3-01.md) point (1).
2. The information captured up to this point (ICD-10-GM code and diagnosis group) are automatically transferred into the entry dialog.
    a. If the user has entered two ICD-10 codes, the option must be given to select only one of them.
3. After entering the information required per [P3-01](P3-01.md) point (1), the prescription is directly marked as long-term approved and visibly indicated in the entry dialog (see [P3-18](P3-18.md) point (9)).
4. A patient-specific approval of long-term remedy need created in this way can be processed in the follow-up according to [P3-01](P3-01.md).
