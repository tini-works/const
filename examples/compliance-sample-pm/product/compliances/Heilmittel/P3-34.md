### Requirement

The software provides a function whereby the user receives a prescription statistics of all completed prescriptions and per patient.

### Rationale

The user should be given the opportunity to obtain an overview of the remedy prescriptions they have initiated, for orienting information purposes. The prescription statistics maps various parameters that are relevant in the context of prescriptions.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must offer the option to display a prescription statistics of all completed prescriptions to the user.
    a. Per displayed prescription, at least the following information must be shown in a tabular overview of all completed prescriptions:
        i. Issue date of the prescription
        ii. Name of the patient
        iii. Date of birth of the patient
        iv. Insurance number of the patient
        v. Diagnosis group
        vi. Presence of a blank prescription
        vii. Treatment-relevant diagnosis(es) (one or two ICD-10-GM codes)
        viii. Prescription quantity of the prescription (if no blank prescription is present)
        ix. Total price of the prescription (if no blank prescription is present)
    b. The software must offer the option, from the tabular overview of all completed prescriptions, to select a specific prescription for display of the prescription documentation per [P3-02](P3-02.md).
    c. The software must calculate and display the sum of the displayed prescription quantities and the sum of the displayed prices of all remedies of the prescriptions shown in the prescription statistics.
    d. The software must display the following notification in connection with the prescription statistics, without interrupting the workflow:

        "Die moeglichen Kosten fuer Hausbesuche oder Therapieberichte oder die Erstbefundung durch den Heilmitteltherapeuten werden nicht beruecksichtigt."

        ("The possible costs for home visits or therapy reports or the initial assessment by the remedy therapist are not taken into account.")

2. The software must offer the option to narrow down the tabular overview of all completed prescriptions using filter or sorting criteria.
    a. At least the following information from the prescription documentation per [P3-02](P3-02.md) must be available as filter or sorting criteria in any combination:
        i. Time range of the issue date of prescriptions (from, to)
        ii. Name of the patient
        iii. Date of birth of the patient
        iv. Insurance number of the patient
        v. Remedy area (Physiotherapy, Podological Therapy, Voice-, Speech-, Language- and Swallowing Therapy, Occupational Therapy, or Nutritional Therapy)
        vi. Request for a home visit (selection field "Ja" or "Nein")
        vii. Request for a therapy report
        viii. Treatment-relevant diagnosis(es) (one or two ICD-10-GM codes)
        ix. Diagnosis group
        x. Marking of a special prescription need (selection field yes/no)
        xi. Marking of a long-term remedy need (selection field yes/no)
        xii. Presence of a patient-specific approval of long-term remedy need (selection field yes/no)
        xiii. Presence of a blank prescription (selection field yes/no)
        xiv. Prescribed priority remedies (one to three)
        xv. Prescribed supplementary remedy
        xvi. Prescribed standardized remedy combination
    b. If certain filter or sorting criteria are predefined by the software without settings by the user, the user must have the option to adjust the predefined criteria, e.g., to deactivate them.
    c. Filtering or sorting by prescribed priority or supplementary remedy must relate exclusively to the prescribed remedies, i.e., the remedies optionally specified within a prescribed standardized remedy combination are not to be included in the filtering or sorting.
    d. When at least one filter or sorting criterion for the tabular overview is selected, the user must be notified in an appropriate manner that the displayed view is filtered or sorted.
    e. When the displayed tabular overview is filtered, the filtering must be removable with a single user action or limited to predefined criteria (per point b.) to display the unfiltered or predefined view.
    f. If the prescribed priority remedies, the prescribed supplementary remedy, or the prescribed standardized remedy combination is selected as a filter or sorting criterion, prescriptions that contain additional remedies alongside the selected remedies are also to be displayed.

### Notes

Beyond this, further evaluations may be provided within the scope of the prescription statistics that access all information documented during the prescription process in the software. A default filter criterion for the time reference when calling up the statistics may be independently determined by the manufacturer.
