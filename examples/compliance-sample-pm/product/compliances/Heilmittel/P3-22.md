### Requirement

The user must be given the option, for prescriptions within the remedy areas "Physiotherapy" and "Occupational Therapy", to additionally prescribe a supplementary remedy.

### Rationale

According to § 12 para. 3 of the HeilM-RL, where medically necessary, a maximum of one supplementary remedy listed in the remedy catalog may be prescribed in addition to priority remedies.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. In addition to selecting the priority remedy according to [P3-21](P3-21.md), the user may prescribe a supplementary remedy that is eligible for prescription according to the remedy areas Physiotherapy and Occupational Therapy (SDHM XML-Element ../kapitel/@V equal to "I. Maßnahmen der Physiotherapie" or "IV. Maßnahmen der Ergotherapie") and the selected diagnosis group per Heilmittel-Stammdatei (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/ergaenzendes_heilmittel_liste/ergaenzendes_heilmittel/).
2. The selected remedy name (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/ergaenzendes_heilmittel_liste/ergaenzendes_heilmittel/name/@V) is to be written into the text field "Ergaenzendes Heilmittel".
3. A maximum of one supplementary remedy may be prescribed.
