### Requirement

The user must be given the option, for prescriptions within the remedy area "Physiotherapy", to independently prescribe the supplementary remedies "Electrotherapy", "Electrostimulation", or "Heat therapy using ultrasound".

### Rationale

According to § 12 para. 3 of the HeilM-RL, Electrotherapy or Electrostimulation or Ultrasound-Heat therapy may also be prescribed in isolation, provided the remedy catalog provides for these measures as indication-based supplementary remedies.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user has the option of selecting "Electrotherapy", "Electrostimulation", or "Heat therapy using ultrasound", provided they are eligible for prescription according to the remedy area Physiotherapy (SDHM XML-Element ../kapitel/@V equal to "I. Maßnahmen der Physiotherapie") and the selected diagnosis group per Heilmittel-Stammdatei (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/ergaenzendes_heilmittel_liste/ergaenzendes_heilmittel/).
2. The selected remedy name (SDHM XML-Element ../diagnosegruppe/heilmittelverordnung/ergaenzendes_heilmittel_liste/ergaenzendes_heilmittel/name/@V) is to be written into the text field "Ergaenzendes Heilmittel".
3. In this case, no further remedy may be prescribed.
