### Requirement

The software must provide a transfer of all or selected prescription data into spreadsheet programs.

### Rationale

To enable custom evaluations and, in the event of economic efficiency reviews, to have the option to evaluate custom prescription data and to incorporate them into the statement of position procedure, an export of all prescription data must be possible.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software offers the option to export the documented prescription data (see [P3-02](P3-02.md)) into spreadsheet programs.
