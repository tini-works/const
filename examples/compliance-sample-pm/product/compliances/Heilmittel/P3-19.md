### Requirement

The user must be given the ability to select remedies according to the Remedy Master Data File for populating the "Remedy as per Catalog" field.

### Rationale

The remedy catalog comprises, depending on the diagnosis group, the various prescribable remedies.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The selection of available remedies must be restricted according to the selected diagnosis group in line with the Remedy Master Data File. The prescribing of the following combinations of prescribable priority and supplementary remedies or a standardized remedy combination depends on the remedy area:
   - one to three priority remedies (see [P3-21](P3-21.md)) or
   - one to three priority remedies and one supplementary remedy ([P3-21](P3-21.md) and [P3-22](P3-22.md)) or
   - one independent supplementary remedy ([P3-23](P3-23.md)) or
   - one standardized remedy combination (see [P3-24](P3-24.md))

### Notes

The following functions provide a more detailed specification of the requirements for the selection of remedies as per the catalog. Two particularities should be noted: In the remedy area of Podology, the listed remedies are only prescribable when certain lead symptomatologies are present. In the area of Physiotherapy, the prescribability of the remedies "KG-ZNS" and "KG-ZNS-Kinder" depends on certain age conditions.
