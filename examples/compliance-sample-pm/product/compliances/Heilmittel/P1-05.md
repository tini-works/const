### Requirement

It must be ensured that the user can use the software's functionalities for prescribing remedies without restriction, even when advertising overlays are displayed.

### Rationale

The user must not be impaired by the display of advertising content and must be able to carry out the prescription process without disruption.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Advertising overlays must not interrupt the prescription workflow.
2. For advertising related to pharmaceuticals, the requirements catalog [EXT_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_AVWG] as per Anlage 23 BMV-A in its currently valid version applies.
