### Requirement

The user must be given the option, within the scope of the prescription, to select the field "dringlicher Behandlungsbedarf innerhalb von 14 Tagen" (urgent treatment need within 14 days).

### Rationale

According to § 15 paragraph 1 of the HeilM-RL, treatment must begin within 28 calendar days of prescription by the contract physician. If there is an urgent treatment need within 14 calendar days, this can be indicated on the prescription.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user must be given the option, within the scope of the prescription, to select the field "dringlicher Behandlungsbedarf, innerhalb von 14 Tagen".
