### Requirement

The user must be given the option to view in the software the respectively current version of Anhang 1 of Anlage 2 of the framework provisions pursuant to § 106b Abs. 2 SGB V for the cost-effectiveness review of medically prescribed services (diagnosis list of special prescription needs) [Heilmittel_Anhang_1].

### Rationale

The user must be able to access all information on special prescription needs at any time.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user must be given the option to view in the software the respectively current version of Anhang 1 of Anlage 2 of the framework provisions pursuant to § 106b Abs. 2 SGB V for the cost-effectiveness review of medically prescribed services (diagnosis list of special prescription needs) [Heilmittel_Anhang_1].
