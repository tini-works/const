### Requirement

The user must be given the option to enter corresponding therapy goals as well as additional medical findings and notes in the field "ggf. Therapieziele / weitere med. Befunde und Hinweise".

### Rationale

The specification of therapy goals may be done according to § 13 para. 2 lit. l. of the HeilM-RL. The field is not a mandatory component of a valid prescription and therefore does not need to be filled in.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The entry of free text and the transfer of texts from the patient documentation are to be enabled.
2. Specifying therapy goals or additional medical findings and notes is not mandatory for the user.
