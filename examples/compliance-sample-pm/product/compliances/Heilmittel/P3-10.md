### Requirement

The user must select the remedy area within the scope of the prescription.

### Rationale

The G-BA Remedy Directive distinguishes different remedy areas. The selected remedy area must be documented on the prescription.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user must select exactly one remedy area within the scope of the prescription, choosing from the following options analogous to the chapter names in the Remedy Master Data File (SDHM XML-Element ../kapitel/@V):
   - Physiotherapy ["I. Maßnahmen der Physiotherapie"]
   - Podological Therapy ["II. Maßnahmen der Podologischen Therapie"]
   - Speech, Language, and Swallowing Therapy ["III. Maßnahmen der Stimm-, Sprech-, Sprach- und Schlucktherapie"]
   - Occupational Therapy ["IV. Maßnahmen der Ergotherapie"]
   - Nutritional Therapy ["V. Maßnahmen der Ernährungstherapie"]

### Notes

The selected remedy area determines the available diagnosis groups and the corresponding prescribable remedies. Alternatively, the remedy area can be automatically derived from the selected diagnosis group.
