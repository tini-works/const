### Requirement

The user must be given the option, within the scope of the prescription, to select the field "Therapiebericht" (therapy report).

### Rationale

According to § 13 para. 2 of the HeilM-RL, the physician may request a therapy report from the remedy service provider by marking with a corresponding cross.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user must be given the option, within the scope of the prescription, to select the field "Therapiebericht".
2. A pre-population is permissible as follows:
    a. Based on the most recently completed prescription
    b. Based on user settings
3. The user must be able to override the pre-population during the prescription.
