### Requirement

The user's settings made within the software must be preserved after a software update.

### Rationale

The time and bureaucratic effort for the user in connection with installing an update should be kept as low as possible.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user settings stored by the user, as per requirement [P1-03](P1-03.md), remain stored in the system even after a software update.
