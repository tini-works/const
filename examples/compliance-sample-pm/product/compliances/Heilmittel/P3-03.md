### Requirement

The user must be given the option to cancel documented or saved prescriptions.

### Rationale

The physician is responsible for the cost-effectiveness of the prescription. Therefore, the option to cancel unredeemed prescriptions must exist.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user must have the option to cancel the entire prescription. The following information must be stored (see also [P3-02](P3-02.md)):
   a. Cancellation of the entire prescription
   b. Date of cancellation
2. Cancelled prescriptions must not be included in the prescription statistics as per [P3-34](P3-34.md).
