### Requirement

The following DMPs must be integrated per KBV specifications: eDMP Diabetes Mellitus Type 1, Type 2, Koronare Herzkrankheit, Asthma, COPD, and Brustkrebs

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given the Vertragssoftware, when DMP functionality is accessed, then eDMP modules for Diabetes Type 1, Type 2, KHK, Asthma, COPD, and Brustkrebs are available per KBV specifications
