### Requirement

On a separate document without fixed layout, additional information for the FaV cover letter must be printable, including lab values and current medication loaded from the patient record, with an appropriate hint text

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a FaV patient, when the supplementary document is created, then lab values and current medication can be loaded from the patient record and printed with an appropriate hint text
