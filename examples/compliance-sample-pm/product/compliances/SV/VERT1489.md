## VERT1489 — The Vertragssoftware must provide a list of all patients with...

### Requirement

The Vertragssoftware must provide a list of all patients with HZV contract status (Beantragt, Aktiviert, or Beendet) per active HZV contract, showing at minimum: patient name, Versichertennummer, birth date, status, dates, and Betreuarzt

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given an active HZV contract, when the user opens the patient list, then all patients with status Beantragt/Aktiviert/Beendet are shown with name, Versichertennummer, birth date, status, dates, and Betreuarzt
