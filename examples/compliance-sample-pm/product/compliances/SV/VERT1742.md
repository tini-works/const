### Requirement

The Vertragssoftware must enable enrollment and activation of contract participation across all Betriebsstaetten of the same LANR when they share the same software, to prevent redundant per-location activations

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a physician with multiple Betriebsstaetten using the same software, when contract participation is activated for a patient, then it applies across all Betriebsstaetten of that LANR without separate per-location activation
