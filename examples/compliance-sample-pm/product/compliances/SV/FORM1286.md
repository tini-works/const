## FORM1286 — The Vertragssoftware must provide a function to fill, print, and...

### Requirement

The Vertragssoftware must provide a function to fill, print, and save the 'Praeventionsverordnung' form, which must auto-open when a confirmed diagnosis from the AKA-Basisdatei Diagnosenliste is first documented for the patient in the quarter

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a first-in-quarter confirmed diagnosis from the AKA Diagnosenliste, when documented, then the 'Praeventionsverordnung' form auto-opens
2. Given the form, when filled, then it can be printed and saved per AKA rules
