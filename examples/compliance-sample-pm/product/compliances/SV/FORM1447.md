### Requirement

When opening Muster 10 or Muster 10A lab referral forms for an active HZV/FaV/BV/IV participant, the Vertragssoftware must display the hint text provided in the AKA-Basisdatei for Hinweistexte

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given an active HZV/FaV/BV/IV participant, when Muster 10 or 10A is opened, then the AKA-Basisdatei Hinweistext for lab referrals is displayed
