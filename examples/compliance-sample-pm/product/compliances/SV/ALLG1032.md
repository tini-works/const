### Requirement

KVK data records must be converted to eGK format per current KBV specifications (KBV_ITA_VGEX_Mapping_KVK.pdf), including for late-arriving cases (Nachzuegler)

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given KVK data records including Nachzuegler cases, when imported, then they are converted to eGK format per current KBV mapping specifications
