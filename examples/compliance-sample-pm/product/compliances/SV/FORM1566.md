### Requirement

The user must be able to fill and print a Teilnahmeerklaerung (participation declaration) per AKA-Basisdatei Volldruck rules; the form is not transmitted but remains with the practice and the patient

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a patient, when the Teilnahmeerklaerung is opened, then it can be filled and printed as Volldruck per AKA template; the form is stored locally, not transmitted
