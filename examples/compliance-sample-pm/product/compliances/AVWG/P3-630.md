### Requirement

The prescribing software must enable the creation of a replacement prescription pursuant to § 31 Absatz 3 Satz 7 SGB V.

### Rationale

When a pharmaceutical must be re-prescribed due to a pharmaceutical recall or due to a restriction of usability made known by the competent authority, § 31 Absatz 3 Satz 7 SGB V provides that the renewed prescription at the expense of the statutory health insurance is exempt from co-payment. This requires a marking of the prescription. The marking also ensures that the replacement prescription can be taken into account as a practice-related special circumstance in the event of a cost-effectiveness review pursuant to § 106b Absatz 1b SGB V.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must enable a replacement prescription pursuant to § 31 Absatz 3 Satz 7 SGB V exclusively for PZN-based finished pharmaceutical products (Merkmal 001).
   - Only one PZN-based finished pharmaceutical product may be prescribed on a replacement prescription.
   - For a paper-based prescription at the expense of the statutory health insurance (Muster 16, BtM-Rezept, T-Rezept), the marking is made through:
     - the entry in the status field (positions 6 and 7) of the Personalienfeld in accordance with the specifications of the "Technische Anlage zu Anlage 4a", and
     - the printout "Ersatzverordnung nach § 31 Absatz 3 Satz 7 SGB V"
   - For an electronic prescription at the expense of the statutory health insurance, the marking is made via the element Composition.extension:Rechtsgrundlage.value[x]:valueCoding with value "10", "11", "14", or "17" in Composition.extension(https://fhir.kbv.de/StructureDefinition/KBV_EX_FOR_Legal_basis).valueCoding.code in profile KBV_PR_ERP_Composition.
2. No product other than the replacement pharmaceutical may be prescribed on the replacement prescription. The replacement prescription is exempt from co-payment.
   - For a paper-based prescription at the expense of the statutory health insurance, the marking is made through the automatic printout of a cross in the "Gebuehr frei" field.
   - For an electronic prescription at the expense of the statutory health insurance, the marking is made via the element MedicationRequest.extension:Zuzahlungsstatus.value[x]:valueCoding set to "1" in profile KBV_PR_ERP_Prescription.
3. When issuing a replacement prescription, the prescribing software displays the following notice to the user without interrupting the workflow: "The renewed prescription of a pharmaceutical as a replacement prescription pursuant to § 31 Absatz 3 Satz 7 SGB V may only be made on the basis of a pharmaceutical recall or a restriction of usability of a pharmaceutical made known by the competent authority."
