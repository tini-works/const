### Requirement

Physician samples pursuant to § 47 Absatz 3 und 4 AMG (Merkmal 016 article type: Ärztemuster gemäß AMG) must not be displayed initially in the prescribing software.

### Rationale

Since physician samples can neither be prescribed nor purchased at a pharmacy, their initial listing in prescribing software is not meaningful.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. When a medicinal product carries the Merkmal 016 physician sample pursuant to § 47 Absatz 3 und 4 AMG, it must not be listed initially in the prescribing software.
2. A configuration setting must allow these physician samples to be included in the overview lists.
3. A physician sample must not be prescribed.
4. A physician sample may be adopted into the medication plan.
