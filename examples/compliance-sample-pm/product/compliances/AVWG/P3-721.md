### Requirement

The prescribing software must ensure that the necessary information on medicinal products and other products included in the pharmaceutical supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V from the pharmaceutical master data (products with PZN) is printed on paper-based prescriptions.

### Rationale

The prescribing of medicinal products and other products included in the pharmaceutical supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V is carried out in accordance with the legal requirements. When printing a pharmaceutical prescription form, all prescription-relevant information must be printed.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must print the following information from the prescription based on the pharmaceutical master data in the specified order:
   - Trade name
   - If not already included in the trade name:
     - Strength
     - Dosage form
     - Pack size (quantity and unit) or standard size
     - The simultaneous specification of both pack size and standard size is permitted.
   - PZN (in the format PZN01234567); the PZN must be printed without a line break.
   - Dosage ([**P3-625**](P3-625.md))
2. The prescribing software must ensure that the following information is printed when the corresponding fields have been filled in or the indicators have been set by the contract physician:
   - Replacement prescription (per [**P3-630**](P3-630.md)) in the first line
   - "Verordnung nach § 27a SGB V" (prescription in the context of artificial fertilization)
   - Dispensing instructions
