**Reference Database Data pursuant to § 31b SGB V**

The data of the reference database pursuant to § 31b SGB V are to be used for populating the corresponding fields in medication plans, in particular those in the Bundeseinheitlichen Medikationsplans BMP. They must not be used for prescribing purposes. The reference database is to be obtained from BfArM.

Currently, only the portion of the information provided by the reference database that is needed for populating the BMP is adopted. Consideration of further information from the reference database will be reviewed once the electronic medication plan has been implemented as an application in the telematics infrastructure.

The designations in square brackets correspond to the authoritative technical documentation "Bereitstellung der Referenzdatenbank gemaess § 31b SGB V". Merkmale 300, 301, 302, and 306 occur once each; Merkmale 303, 304, and 305 may occur multiple times.

|---------|-------------|
