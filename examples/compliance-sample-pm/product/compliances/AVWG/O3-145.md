### Requirement

The implementation of further non-package-related information in the prescribing software is possible in accordance with the following acceptance criteria.

### Rationale

Additional information can meaningfully support the prescribing process, provided it is manufacturer-independent, follows objective criteria, and has no advertising character.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Additional information must be manufacturer-independent, follow objective criteria, and must not have an advertising character.
2. The online form of the reporting form for adverse drug reactions or for medication errors of the Drug Commission of the German Medical Association (AkdÄ) is permitted.
