## P3-265 — Display of the Contents of Resolutions

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | P3-265 |
| **Type** | Mandatory |
| **Source** | AVWG v5.8 |
| **Section** | 3.2 Beschlüsse des G-BA nach § 35a SGB V |
| **Status** | TBC |
| **Goals** | — |
| **Verification Method** | — |
| **Matched by** | — |
| **Refer** | [P3-260](P3-260.md), [P3-250](P3-250.md) |

### Requirement

The essential contents of a resolution must be displayed at a minimum.

### Rationale

In order to inform about the essential contents of the resolutions, at least the described contents are necessary.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. After selecting a specific resolution information per [**P3-260**](P3-260.md), the prescribing software must display at least the following contents per patient group (Merkmal 115):
   - Approved area of application of the resolution (Merkmal 134)
   - Patient group name (Merkmal 135)
   - If the information on "Determined extent and probability of additional benefit" (Merkmal 146) is available, it must be displayed.
   - If the name of the underlying appropriate comparative therapy (Merkmal 149) is empty or not available, only then must the information on the G-BA-determined appropriate comparative therapy (Merkmal 141) be displayed, if available.
   - If a reserve antibiotic assessment (Merkmal 114 ="Beschluss_antib") exists, the information on the extent of additional benefit (Merkmal 147) is displayed.
   - If a resolution is marked as an orphan assessment (Merkmal 114 ="Beschluss_orph"), the text "Orphan-Bewertung" is displayed.
   - If a resolution is marked as a reserve antibiotic assessment (Merkmal 114 ="Beschluss_antib"), the text "Reserveantibiotikum-Bewertung" is displayed.
   - If information on at least one endpoint category -- Mortality (Merkmal 155 und 156), Morbidity (Merkmal 158 und 159), Quality of Life (Merkmal 161 und 162), or Adverse Events (Merkmal 164 und 165) -- is available, the legend of the graphical summary of the effects of the relevant clinical endpoints (Merkmal 166) must be displayed.
   - If information on endpoint category Mortality is available (Merkmal 155 und 156), at least the graphical illustration (Merkmal 155) and the textual result (Merkmal 156) must be displayed.
   - If information on endpoint category Morbidity is available (Merkmal 158 und 159), at least the graphical illustration (Merkmal 158) and the textual result (Merkmal 159) must be displayed.
   - If information on endpoint category Quality of Life is available (Merkmal 161 und 162), at least the graphical illustration (Merkmal 161) and the textual result (Merkmal 162) must be displayed.
   - If information on endpoint category Adverse Events is available (Merkmal 164 und 165), at least the graphical illustration (Merkmal 164) and the textual result (Merkmal 165) must be displayed.
   - If information on quality-assured application (Merkmal 170) is available, it must be displayed.
   - If information on accompanying data collection pursuant to § 35a Absatz 3b SGB V (Merkmal 128) is available, it must be displayed.

2. The prescribing software must be able to display the following contents per patient group (Merkmal 115) with at most one additional interaction:
   - Hyperlink early benefit assessment (Merkmal 113)
   - If a hyperlink Richtlinie Qualitätssicherung 'ATMP' (Merkmal 111) is available, it must be displayed (see also Merkmal 110 regarding the text to be displayed).
   - If a summary of the supporting reasons of the resolution of the Federal Joint Committee (Merkmal 168) is available, it must be displayed.
   - Resolution date (Merkmal 131)
   - If a validity end of the resolution (Merkmal 132) is available, the date must be displayed with a preceding "valid until".
   - If an approval with orphan status (Merkmal 105 = "1") exists, "Approval with orphan status" must be displayed.
   - If an approval subject to special conditions (Merkmal 106 = "1") exists, "Approval subject to special conditions" must be displayed.
   - If an approval in exceptional cases (Merkmal 107 = "1") exists, "Approval in exceptional cases" must be displayed.
   - If an approval for advanced therapy medicinal products (Merkmal 108 = "1") exists, "Approval for advanced therapy medicinal products (ATMP)" must be displayed.
   - ICD-10-GM Code (Merkmal 136)
   - ICD-10-GM Description (Merkmal 137)

3. All further Merkmale of the machine-readable version of the resolutions pursuant to § 35a SGB V listed under [**P3-250**](P3-250.md) Acceptance Criterion 1 that were not already listed under Acceptance Criteria 1 and 2 of this mandatory function may additionally be implemented by the prescribing software. The following elements must not be visible:
   - Identification number resolution (Merkmal 102)
   - Identification code resolution (Merkmal 103)
   - Identification number patient group (Merkmal 115)
   - Identification number trade name (Merkmal 118)
   - Alpha-ID code (Merkmal 138)

4. The prescribing software may display the contents of Acceptance Criteria (1) and (2) and, if applicable, (3) together at once.
