### Requirement

Automatic population of the "aut idem" prescription field is impermissible. Manufacturer-specific, product-specific, or entire-assortment-related "aut idem" configurations are impermissible. For repeat prescriptions, adopting an "aut idem" checkbox previously set by the physician for the same patient is permissible. The final selection must be made by the physician.
