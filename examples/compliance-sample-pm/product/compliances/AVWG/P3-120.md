### Requirement

In both the tabular selection lists according to [**P3-315**](P3-315.md), [**P3-320**](P3-320.md), [**P3-400**](P3-400.md) and within each prescription process (including in particular repeated prescriptions from a patient history, prescriptions from an in-house pharmacy, and from a medication plan pursuant to § 31a SGB V as well as the eMedication list of the ePA), the software must display at least the Merkmale listed below from the drug master data according to [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) at the **first level** by default.

### Rationale

During a prescribing process or when selecting a package, the fundamental information should be directly visible before making the specific selection.
