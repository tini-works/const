### Requirement

The contract physician must have the ability to view all Rote-Hand-Briefe per EAMIV (archived as well as current) in the prescribing software.

### Rationale

Pursuant to § 3 Abs. 4 EAMIV, all Rote-Hand-Briefe must be continuously archived or retrievable in the prescribing software.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The contract physician must have the ability in the prescribing software to view all Rote-Hand-Briefe published from 01.04.2023 onward, per EAMIV (archived as well as current).
2. The contract physician must be shown at least the following information per Rote-Hand-Brief in a suitable manner (e.g., tabular) per EAMIV:
   - a. Title of the Rote-Hand-Brief per EAMIV [Merkmal 354]
   - b. Validity start of the Rote-Hand-Brief per EAMIV [Merkmal 351]
   - c. If available, validity end of the Rote-Hand-Brief per EAMIV [Merkmal 352]
   - The information of the Rote-Hand-Brief pursuant to § 3 Abs. 3 EAMIV [Merkmal 353] must be retrievable per entry.
3. The prescribing software must provide filter options for the information required by Acceptance Criterion 2 to the contract physician.
   - a. The prescribing software may provide additional filter options to the contract physician.

### Notes

All Rote-Hand-Briefe published before 01.04.2023 do not necessarily need to be listed in the archive. An additional listing of these older Rote-Hand-Briefe is, however, optionally possible. For all Rote-Hand-Briefe published before 01.04.2023, an assignment to finished medicinal products is not required, but optionally possible.
