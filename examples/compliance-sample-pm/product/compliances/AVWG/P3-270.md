## P3-270 — Display of Resolution Contents pursuant to § 35a SGB V with Resolution Date after 30.06.2020

### Requirement

If a medicinal product for which a G-BA resolution pursuant to § 35a SGB V with a resolution date after 30.06.2020 exists is accessed, the resolution contents must be actively displayed in accordance with [**P3-265**](P3-265.md).

### Rationale

The information offering for physicians regarding the benefit assessment of new medicinal products pursuant to § 35a SGB V shall be improved.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must ensure that each resolution pursuant to § 35a SGB V (Merkmal 103) with a resolution date after 30.06.2020 (Merkmal 131) is actively displayed once per [**P3-265**](P3-265.md) Acceptance Criterion 1 or, if applicable, 4.
2. When accessing the medicinal product in the prescribing software, if one or more ICD-10-GM codes of the patient are present and these match at least one ICD-10-GM code ("ICD-10-GM code of the patient" equal to Merkmal 136), only the corresponding resolution information may be displayed per Acceptance Criterion 1; all other resolution information must be accessible with an additional action.
3. When accessing the medicinal product in the prescribing software, if no ICD-10-GM code of the patient is present, all resolution information of all resolutions of the medicinal product must be displayed per Acceptance Criterion 1.
4. The display is presented in a clearly perceptible, recognizable, and uniform form without additional user interaction.
5. The prescribing software should no longer actively display a resolution per Acceptance Criteria 1 and 2 once it has been displayed.
