### Requirement

The G-BA resolutions pursuant to § 35a SGB V for a medicinal product are to be presented in a resolution overview.

### Rationale

A clear presentation of multiple resolutions assigned to a medicinal product is necessary in order to easily access the information of the respective resolution.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must display the resolution information "Approved area of application of the resolution" (Merkmal 134) per resolution for a selected medicinal product pursuant to § 35a SGB V in the form of an overview.
   - If a resolution applies to multiple patient groups, the first "Approved area of application of the resolution" (Merkmal 134) is always used for display.
2. When the prescribing software contains one or more ICD-10-GM codes of the patient and these match at least one ICD-10-GM code ("ICD-10-GM code of the patient" equal to Merkmal 136 of any patient group of the resolution), only the corresponding resolution information may be displayed per Acceptance Criterion 1; all other resolution information must be accessible with an additional action.
3. When the prescribing software contains one or more ICD-10-GM codes of the patient and these do not match any ICD-10-GM code ("ICD-10-GM code of the patient" equal to Merkmal 136 of any patient group of the resolution):
   - A notice must be displayed that no resolution information matches the ICD-10-GM code(s) of the patient.
   - All resolution information for the medicinal product must be accessible per Acceptance Criterion 1 with at most one additional action.
4. When no ICD-10-GM code of the patient is available in the prescribing software, all resolution information for the medicinal product must be displayed per Acceptance Criterion 1.
5. The prescribing software must allow a resolution information to be selected and the further information pursuant to [**P3-265**](P3-265.md) to be made available.
