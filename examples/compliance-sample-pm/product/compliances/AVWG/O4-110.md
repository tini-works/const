### Requirement

The prescribing software may display a warning function regarding the utilization of the guideline volume. The function is governed by the legal requirements in § 106 Absatz 5a SGB V and is updated in accordance with the KV guideline volumes.

The corresponding information is provided by the contractual partners at the state level. The PVS vendor maintains an interface for this purpose.

Background: The legal basis of § 84 Absatz 7a SGB V (average cost regulation) has been repealed.
