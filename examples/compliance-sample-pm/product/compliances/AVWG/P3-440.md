### Requirement

Access to price comparison searches pursuant to [**P3-325**](P3-325.md) and substitution suggestions pursuant to [**P3-400**](P3-400.md) must be provided in a comparable and easily discoverable manner. The display of the various result lists must be in a uniform format and at the same location within the program interface. The type of comparison search/substitution suggestion must be clearly indicated. Any applied filters and sort orders must be clearly displayed.
