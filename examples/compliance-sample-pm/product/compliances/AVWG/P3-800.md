### Requirement

The prescribing software must contain the functions and information necessary for the creation and updating of the medication plan pursuant to § 31a SGB V.

The requirements and technical specifications of the agreement concluded at the federal level between the KBV, the German Medical Association (BAeK), and the relevant peak organization of pharmacists representing the commercial interests, pursuant to § 31a Absatz 4 SGB V, including its annexes, apply (source: KBV website).
