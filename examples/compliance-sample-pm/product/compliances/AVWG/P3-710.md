### Requirement

The prescribing software must enable the electronic prescription. The requirements of the gematik are to be implemented.

### Rationale

The electronic prescription is intended for the prescribing of pharmacy-only medicinal products; the technical design for the transmission of an electronic prescription is provided by gematik.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must implement the requirements of the Technical Annex for electronic pharmaceutical prescriptions [KBV ITA VGEX TECHNISCHE ANLAGE ERP].
2. The prescribing software must implement the requirements of the gematik (per mandatory function P5-01 of the document [KBV ITA VGEX TECHNISCHE ANLAGE ERP]) for the transmission of the electronic prescription.
3. The electronic prescription may only be implemented for:
   - Pharmacy-only medicinal products (Merkmal 010 or 011) from the pharmaceutical master data ([**P3-621**](P3-621.md)), excluding narcotics and T-prescription medicines (Merkmal 012 and 013)
   - Active ingredient prescriptions ([**O3-622**](O3-622.md))
   - Compound prescriptions ([**O3-623**](O3-623.md)), and
   - Free-text prescriptions per ([**P3-624**](P3-624.md))
4. The prescribing software must inform the contract physician when selecting free-text prescriptions that only pharmacy-only medicinal products can be prescribed electronically.

### Notes
