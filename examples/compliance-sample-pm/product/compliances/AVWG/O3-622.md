### Requirement

The prescribing software may offer a structured active substance prescription. This does not apply to pharmaceuticals subject to the Betäubungsmittelverschreibungsverordnung (BtMVV) or to T-Rezept pharmaceuticals.

### Rationale

The structured capture of the information required under the Arzneimittelverschreibungsverordnung facilitates the correct and unambiguous issuance of an active substance prescription.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must implement an active substance prescription in accordance with the contents of the Technische Anlage elektronische Arzneimittelverordnung.
2. The prescribing software must ensure that an active substance prescription does not contain a PZN.
3. The "aut idem" marker must not be set for active substance prescriptions.
4. Active substances from the AM-RL Anlage VII Teil B (see [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) Merkmal 060) in the dosage forms described therein must not be prescribed as active substance prescriptions.
5. The prescribing software may provide the contracted physician with a selection list of active substances with the associated ASK number ([**P2-110**](P2-110.md) Merkmal 020).
6. The prescribing software may offer a selection list of dosage forms.
7. To avoid unclear prescriptions, the prescribing software must ensure that the active substance prescription (in terms of the combination of active substance, strength, dosage form, and package size or standard size) can be assigned to at least one product from the pharmaceutical master data.
8. The prescribing software may implement the active substance prescription through "translation" of the information of a finished pharmaceutical product from the pharmaceutical master data.
9. For printing a paper-based prescription, [**K3-722**](K3-722.md) must be observed.
10. A structured active substance prescription may only be implemented for pharmaceuticals (Merkmal 023 pursuant to [P2-110](P2-110.md)). For pharmaceuticals subject to the BtMVV (Merkmal 012) and T-Rezept pharmaceuticals (Merkmal 013), it must not be implemented.
