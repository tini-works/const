|-------|-------|

| **Matched by** | — |
| **Refer** | [P3-120](P3-120.md), [P2-110](P2-110.md), [P3-130](P3-130.md), [P3-270](P3-270.md), [K2-120](K2-120.md) |

### Requirement

When selecting a package, the software must display on the **second level**, in addition to the information from [P3-120](P3-120.md), at least the Merkmale listed below from the drug master data according to [**P2-110**](P2-110.md).

### Rationale

After selecting a specific package, additional properties and information relevant to the prescription should be visible.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. In the display of the additional package-related information, at least the following Merkmale according to [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) must be displayed without further user action (e.g., scrolling):

| Merkmal | Content per Pflichtfunktion [P2-110](P2-110.md) |
|---------|-----------------------------------|
| Co-payment | If Merkmal 038 applies, then 039; otherwise 035 |
| Additional costs | 036 |
| Fixed amount level | 033 |
| Labelling: pharmacy-only or prescription-only or BtM or T-Rezept medicinal product | 010 oder 011 oder 012 oder 013 |
| Labelling: documentation obligation pursuant to Transfusion Act | 014 |
| Labelling if product is on the negative list | 050 |
| Labelling if product falls under the prescribing exclusion of medicinal products for improving quality of life pursuant to § 34 Absatz 1 Satz 7 SGB V (lifestyle medicinal product) | 051 |
| Labelling if exceptions from the prescribing exclusion of medicinal products for improving quality of life pursuant to § 34 Absatz 1 Satz 7 SGB V (conditionally prescribable lifestyle medicinal product) are possible | 052 |
| Labelling for adolescents over 12 years without developmental disorders and adults, if exceptions from the statutory prescribing exclusion (OTX) exist for this medicinal product | 053 |
| Labelling if this medicinal product is subject to prescribing restrictions of AM-RL Anlage III | 055 |
| Labelling if this medicinal product is subject to prescribing exclusions of AM-RL Anlage III | 056 |
| Labelling if a therapy advisory from the G-BA exists for this medicinal product | 058 |
| Labelling if lower-priced products are available | Must be calculated |
| Labelling (if the selected product is not under rebate), that other rebated products with the same active substance are available for the health insurance fund of the respective patient | Must be calculated |
| Labelling if it is a prescribable medical device pursuant to § 31 Absatz 1 Nummer 2 SGB V | 210 |
| Labelling if it is a prescribable dressing pursuant to § 31 Absatz 1a SGB V | 410 |
| Labelling if the article status is: Out of Distribution, Not marketable, Withdrawn | 009 |
| Labelling import medicinal product | 015 |

2. The display of advisory texts must occur upon the described selection of a product in accordance with [**P3-130**](P3-130.md) or for medicinal products pursuant to § 35a SGB V in accordance with [**P3-270**](P3-270.md).
3. The display must not interrupt the prescribing workflow.
4. All remaining Merkmale pursuant to [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) and [**K2-120**](K2-120.md) must be available within at most two additional actions.
