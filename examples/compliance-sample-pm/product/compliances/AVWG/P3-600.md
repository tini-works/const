### Requirement

The AM-RL pursuant to § 92 Absatz 1 Satz 2 Nummer 6 SGB V including its annexes must be made available in text form as a PDF file at the current version in accordance with [**P2-100**](P2-100.md). Changes to the AM-RL and its annexes are to be incorporated upon entry into force as part of the next update of the pharmaceutical master data.
