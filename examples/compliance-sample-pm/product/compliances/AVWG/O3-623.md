### Requirement

The prescribing software may offer prescriptions of compounded preparations (pharmaceuticals that are individually prepared in the pharmacy based on a prescription) in a structured form.

### Rationale

The structured capture of the necessary information for a compounded preparation is intended to facilitate the correct and unambiguous issuance of compounding prescriptions.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must implement a prescription of pharmaceuticals to be prepared in the pharmacy (compounding prescription) in accordance with the contents of the Technische Anlage elektronische Arzneimittelverordnung.
2. The system enables the selection of one or more components each from the pharmaceutical master data or free text.
   - When selecting a product from the pharmaceutical master data, the trade name, dosage form, and corresponding PZN must be automatically populated and must no longer be manually modified. Quantity and unit may, however, be adjusted as needed.
   - For free-text entry, the field "PZN des Bestandteils" must remain empty.
3. The prescribing software must enable patient-independent storage of standard compounding formulas captured in structured form.
4. The prescribing software may store compounding formulas from the Neues Rezeptur-Formularium and make them available to the contracted physician for selection.
5. For printing a paper-based prescription, [**K3-723**](K3-723.md) must be observed.
