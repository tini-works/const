### Requirement

Additional search and filter options are permissible, provided the panel physician has transparency regarding the search or filter criteria used and the criteria are equally applicable to all medicinal products and other products included in the drug supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V.

A search for medicinal products and other products included in the drug supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V, or a filtering of search results according to [P3-300](P3-300.md), can be performed using, for example, the following criteria:

- Potency
- Dosage form
- Additional ingredients
- N-designation according to the packaging size regulation
- For medicinal products with a G-BA resolution pursuant to § 35a SGB V (Merkmal 061): "Abbreviated area of application" (Merkmal 171)

A combined query is possible.
