### Requirement

For medicinal product prescriptions, notices regarding the availability of lower-priced, active-substance-equivalent medicinal products suitable for substitution must be provided. These notices may be conveyed through designation but must occur without a separate user action during the course of the prescribing dialogue. Notices must not interrupt the workflow.

The detailed display of alternative medicinal products is triggered upon request by the physician pursuant to [**P3-400**](P3-400.md). [**P3-325**](P3-325.md) must be observed.
