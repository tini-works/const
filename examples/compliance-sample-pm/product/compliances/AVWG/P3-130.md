### Requirement

If a product is subject to one of the following regulations, the complete texts from the AM-RL or advisories from drug agreements must be displayed to the physician before or during the prescribing process:

- Prescribing exclusions of AM-RL Anlage III for corresponding medicinal products (see [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) Merkmal 057)
- Prescribing restrictions of AM-RL Anlage III for corresponding medicinal products (see [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) Merkmal 057)
- Exception indication per OTC exception list (AM-RL Anlage I; see [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) Merkmal 054) (only display for adolescents from age 12 up to age 18 without developmental disorders and adults)
- Prescribability of medical devices pursuant to AM-RL Anlage V (medically necessary cases and time limitation of prescribability, see [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) Merkmal 203 und 211)
- Advisories on regulations of drug agreements pursuant to § 84 Absatz 1 SGB V, insofar as data from the state level is provided for this purpose, e.g., active substance quota regulation (see [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) Merkmal 080 as well as [**P3-610**](P3-610.md))

The display of advisories is presented in a clearly perceptible, recognizable form, always at the same location on the user interface, without requiring additional user interaction, and must not interrupt the workflow. If multiple advisories exist, they are to be displayed in the order described here.

If advisory texts exceed a length of 250 characters (including spaces), at least the first 250 characters must be displayed. The complete advisory text must be displayable upon request through a single user interaction. The structuring of AM-RL advisory texts by paragraphs, line breaks, and bullet points must be reproduced in a comparable manner.

The physician must additionally be able to decide during the prescription that an advisory text for products of the same ATC code is no longer actively displayed. However, it must remain apparent that an advisory exists, and the corresponding advisory text must be retrievable with a user action. In case of changes to an advisory or advisory text, the new advisory text must initially be displayed again as per the default setting.
