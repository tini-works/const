### Purpose

The referral record (0102) captures all billing-relevant data for referral cases. It extends the outpatient treatment fields with referral-specific information such as the referring physician, initial requester, referral orders, diagnoses/suspected diagnoses, and findings. It is also used for in-vitro diagnostic orders (Scheinuntergruppe 27) and laboratory community orders (Scheinuntergruppe 28).
