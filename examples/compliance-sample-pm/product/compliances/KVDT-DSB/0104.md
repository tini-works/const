### Purpose

The emergency service/substitution/emergency record (0104) captures billing data for emergency duty cases, physician substitution cases, and emergency cases. It includes a field for identifying the continuing physician (Weiterbehandelnder Arzt) and otherwise follows a similar structure to the outpatient treatment record with patient demographics, insurance data, services, and diagnoses.
