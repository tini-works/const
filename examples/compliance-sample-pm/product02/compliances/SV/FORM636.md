### Requirement

The Vertragssoftware must provide a patient-specific FaV cardiology report (Befundbericht FaV - Kardiologie) for printing, containing at minimum: patient master data, diagnoses, ICD codes, anamnesis, pre-medication, lab values, diagnostic findings, assessment, therapy proposal, and a 'Teilnahme am Facharztvertrag' note

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a FaV cardiology patient, when the Befundbericht is generated, then it contains: patient master data, diagnoses, ICD codes, anamnesis, pre-medication, lab values, findings, assessment, therapy proposal, and 'Teilnahme am Facharztvertrag' note
