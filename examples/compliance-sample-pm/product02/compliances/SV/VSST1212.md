### Requirement

For contract participants aged 12 to 17, deviating from SGB V section 34(1), non-prescription medications (OTC) must automatically be prescribed on the Kassenrezept (Muster 16) under AM-RL Anlage II/III and Negativliste rules; manual change of prescription type must remain possible

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a participant aged 12-17, when OTC medication is prescribed, then it is automatically placed on Muster 16 (Kassenrezept)
2. Given manual override needed, then the prescription type can be changed
