### Requirement

The Vertragssoftware must provide a function to confirm the currency of employment status/type; the confirmation date must be stored in the 'Datum letzte Ueberpruefung' field

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given the employment data confirmation function, when the user confirms currency, then the current date is stored in 'Datum letzte Ueberpruefung'
