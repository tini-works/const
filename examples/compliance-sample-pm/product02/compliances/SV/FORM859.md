### Requirement

The Vertragssoftware must provide a patient-specific FaV PNP report (Befundbericht FaV - PNP) for printing, containing at minimum: patient master data and diagnoses

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a FaV PNP patient, when the Befundbericht is generated, then it contains at minimum: patient master data and diagnoses
