### Requirement

In addition to KBV AVWG function P2-130, the Vertragssoftware must be capable of performing regular medication database updates at least every 14 days, but no less than quarterly

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given the medication database, when an update cycle runs, then updates are applied at least every 14 days or quarterly at minimum
