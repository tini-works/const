### Requirement

The Vertragssoftware must provide a patient-specific FaV gastroenterology report (Befundbericht FaV - Gastroenterologie) for printing, containing: patient master data, diagnoses, ICD codes, anamnesis, lab values, diagnostic findings, assessment, and therapy proposal

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given a FaV gastroenterology patient, when the Befundbericht is generated, then it contains: patient master data, diagnoses, ICD codes, anamnesis, lab values, findings, assessment, and therapy proposal
