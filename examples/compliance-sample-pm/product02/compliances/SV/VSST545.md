### Requirement

The Vertragssoftware must display HPM medication recommendation results in tabular form classified by ATC groups, sorted ascending by priority from 0, with medications color-coded by category and all information from the result file shown

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Given HPM medication recommendation results, when displayed, then they appear in a table grouped by ATC group sorted ascending by priority, with medications color-coded by category
