### Requirement

The software must enable the recording, storage, and transmission of services within the framework of ASV.

### Rationale

The contractual basis is section 6, para. 3 of the ASV-AV in conjunction with section 116b, para. 6, sentence 12 SGB V.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must enable the user to record and store services within the framework of ASV.
2. The software transfers services recorded within the ASV framework into the billing data record according to the provisions of the section "Integration of ASV Billing into the ADT Data Package" from [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Datensatzbeschreibung_KVDT].
3. The software observes the provisions of the section "Ambulante spezialfachaerztliche Versorgung (ASV)" from [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_Formularbedruckung] when issuing contract physician forms.

### Notes

The Institute of the Valuation Committee publishes on its website ([IDB_ASV]) machine-processable lists (e.g., CSV files) that represent the billable ASV services. These files are based on the appendices in which the Federal Joint Committee (G-BA) defines the scope of treatment for each ASV indication.
