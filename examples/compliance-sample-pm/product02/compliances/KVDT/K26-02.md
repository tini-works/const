### Requirement

When creating the electronic referral, the software automatically populates the digital form.

### Rationale

To support the physician in their work, the system must automatically adopt the data for creation of the electronic referral into the digital form.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. When creating the electronic referral according to requirement [K26-03](K26-03.md), the software must automatically populate the fields of the digital form.
2. Before the automated population, the data is displayed to the physician who has the option to modify the data. Excluded from this modification option are the data of the health insurance card (see listing "Data of an insured person's data record" in Chapter 2.2).
3. The user must not manually populate the PDF file of the digital form.
