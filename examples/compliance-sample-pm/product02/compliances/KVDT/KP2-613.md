### Requirement

The software must enable the specification of the HGNC gene symbol (FK 5077) and the type of disease (FK 5079) for GOPen 11511[G-alpha], 11512[G-alpha], 11516[G-alpha] to 11518[G-alpha] and 11521[G-alpha].

### Rationale

The calculation of GOPen 11511, 11512, 11516 to 11518, and 11521 requires the justification that contains the type of disease, and the specification of the type of examination (HGNC gene symbol).

### Acceptance Criteria

All provisions of functions [KP2-621](KP2-621.md) and [KP2-624](KP2-624.md) apply, subject to the following criteria:

1. The software system must require the user, when capturing the above-mentioned GOPen, to provide **exactly** one entry of an HGNC gene symbol (FK 5077) **and** one "Type of disease" (FK 5079) within the scope of service documentation. The user must not have the ability to have multiple HGNC gene symbols or multiple entries for "Type of disease" for a single service number to be captured and transmitted in the billing.
