## KP2-946 — Printing of Patient Declaration European Health Insurance

|-------|-------|

| **Section** | 2.3.12 Patientenerklärung Europäische Krankenversicherung |
| **Status** | TBC |
| **Goals** | — |
| **Verification Method** | — |
| **Matched by** | — |
| **Refer** | — |

### Requirement

The software enables printing of the PDF template "Patient Declaration European Health Insurance" both in full and in parts.

### Rationale

The user must have the ability to access and print the document via the software.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The user can:
   a) print the PDF template of the "Patient Declaration European Health Insurance" in full **or**
   b) also print parts of the PDF template
   directly from the system.
2. The software prints on each individual page of the patient declaration, below the contract physician stamp, the ADT verification number (PRF.NR.) from the KVDT procedure at the following position:
   a) The verification number must be printed at the defined position in Arial, font size 5.

### Notes

The form "Patient Declaration European Health Insurance" is to be produced exclusively by means of certified software and a printer by the contract physician in the practice, in accordance with Annex 20 of the Bundesmantelvertrag-Aerzte (Agreement on the Use of the European Health Insurance Card).
