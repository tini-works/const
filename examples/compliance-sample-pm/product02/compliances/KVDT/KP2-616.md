### Requirement

The software must enable the specification of the HGNC gene symbol (FK 5077), the "Type of disease" (FK 5079), and a valid ICD-10-GM code (FK 6001) for GOPen 19421[G-alpha], 19451[G-alpha], and 19452[G-alpha].

### Rationale

According to EBM billing provisions for GOPen 19421[G-alpha], 19451[G-alpha], and 19452[G-alpha], the "Type of examination (HGNC gene symbol)" and the "Type of disease coded per ICD-10-GM" are to be specified; additionally the "Type of disease" can be specified.

### Acceptance Criteria

All provisions of functions [KP2-621](KP2-621.md), [KP2-624](KP2-624.md), and [KP2-618](KP2-618.md) apply, subject to the following criteria:

1. The software system must require the user, when capturing GOPen 19421[G-alpha], 19451[G-alpha], and 19452[G-alpha], to provide **exactly** one HGNC gene symbol (FK 5077) within the scope of service documentation.
2. For GOPen 19421[G-alpha], 19451[G-alpha], and 19452[G-alpha], the "Type of disease" (FK 5079) does not need to be mandatorily captured. However, the user must be given the ability to capture exactly one "Type of disease".
