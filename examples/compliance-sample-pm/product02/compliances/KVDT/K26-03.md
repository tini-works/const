### Requirement

The software must create a digital Form 6 for commissioning in doctor-to-doctor communication according to the provisions of the BMV-Ae (see: [KBV_BMV-Ae]), Annex 2b of the BMV-Ae (see: [KBV_BMVAe_Anlage_2b]), and the technical handbook for digital preprints (see: [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Technisches_Handbuch_DiMus]).

### Rationale

The preprint agreement for digital preprints ([KBV_BMVAe_Anlage_2b]) as well as the agreement on teleconsultation ([KBV_BMV_Ae_Anlage 31a]) regulate the requirements for electronic commissioning of the radiological teleconsultation as well as the requirements for the referral from doctor to doctor as such.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The system creates digital Form 6 in doctor-to-doctor communication according to the provisions of the technical handbook for digital preprints [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Technisches_Handbuch_DiMus]. This includes in particular:
   a) When creating Form 6, all required PDF form fields are populated according to the provisions of Chapter "2.10 Formularfelder" and Chapter "3.1.2 Formularfelder digitales Muster 6." The provisions regarding the properties of the PDF form fields, as described in the technical handbook, are also observed.
   b) The notes on file naming are observed according to Chapter "2.5 Dateinamen."
   c) The qualified electronic signature required on the form corresponds to the provisions of Chapter "2.7 Qualifizierte elektronische Signatur."
   d) The KBV verification number is applied to digital Form 6 according to Chapter "2.10.7 KBV-Pruefnummer."
   e) The file format of the digital form was not altered and corresponds to PDF/A-2a format. The provisions of Chapter "2.8 (File) Format of the digital forms (PDF/A-2a)" were observed.
   f) The metadata values of digital Form 6 were not altered according to Table 2 of Chapter "2.9 Metadaten."
   g) The metadata values were populated according to Table 1 of Chapter "2.9 Metadaten" within the framework of populating the personnel field with the respective values.
