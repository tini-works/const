### Requirement

The software must provide the user with the functions based on Kapitel 2 "Umsetzung der Spezifikation - 116117 Terminservice Vermittlungscode" and Kapitel 3 "Umsetzung der Bedruckung" of the Anforderungskatalog Terminservice.

### Rationale

Due to the separate remuneration for treatments resulting from Terminvermittlungen through the Terminservice, the billed services are to be marked by Schein and the relevant information is to be transmitted per the defined requirements.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must implement the functions per Kapiteln 2 "Umsetzung der Spezifikation 116117 Terminservice Vermittlungscode" and 3 "Umsetzung der Bedruckung" of the Anforderungsdokument "Anforderungskatalog Terminservice" in version 3.0.0 [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_116117_TSS] and provide the user with issuance of Überweisungen (Muster 6 and PTV 11).
2. The software must have successfully completed the audit for the application "116117 Terminservice Vermittlungscode" at kv.digital. As proof, the audit certificate must be submitted to KBV within the certification process "116117 Terminservice Vermittlungscode".
