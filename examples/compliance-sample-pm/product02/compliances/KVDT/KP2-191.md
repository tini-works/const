### Requirement

The software ensures that statutorily insured patients under 18 years of age in the system, with the exception of Muster 4 (prescription for patient transport), are automatically managed as "gebührenfrei" (fee-exempt).

### Rationale

For children and adolescents under 18 years of age, there is no co-payment obligation. Therefore, patients under 18 can automatically be designated as "gebührenfrei". An exception is transportation costs. Children and adolescents under 18 years are not automatically exempt from co-payments for transportation.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. Patients under 18 years are automatically managed as "gebührenfrei" in the PVS.
   a) It must be considered that the automatic co-payment exemption ends at the beginning of the quarter in which the patient completes their 18th year of life. (Applies for GKV health insurers with 3rd-5th digit of the VKNR < 800)
2. When the patient enters co-payment obligation, the user is informed by the PVS about the change.
3. In Muster 4, the user must explicitly indicate whether a co-payment exemption exists or does not exist for the patient.

### Condition

Excluded from implementation of this requirement are software systems without APK.
