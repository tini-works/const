### Requirement

If the patient agrees with the dispatch of a quarterly receipt, the software must provide the user with the ability to mark a treatment case for receipt dispatch. The software must offer a functionality that enables the sequential printout of all marked quarterly receipts at quarter-end in an automated manner.

### Notes

To support practice staff, the collection of shipping costs and the expense allowance pursuant to § 305 Abs. 2 SGB V in the amount of 1 Euro plus shipping costs can optionally be documented.
