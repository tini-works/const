### Requirement

The software must enable the user to create and manage their ASV team number(s) in the facility master data.

### Rationale

The contractual basis is section 6, para. 3 of the ASV-AV in conjunction with section 116b, para. 6, sentence 12 SGB V.

A contract physician/contract psychotherapist can be a member of multiple ASV teams and must therefore be able to create and manage multiple ASV team numbers.

Furthermore, multiple contract physicians/contract psychotherapists of a facility can be members of one or multiple ASV teams.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must enable each user to create and manage their ASV team number(s) in the facility master data.
2. The software transfers the ASV team number(s) specified by the user with the billing in field FK 0222 (ASV team number) in the "Facility data record" ("besa") of the KVDT file.
