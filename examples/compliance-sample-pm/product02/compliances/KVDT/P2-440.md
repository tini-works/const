### Requirement

The additional data required by the respective responsible Kassenaerztliche Vereinigung for billing of Sonstige Kostentraeger -- according to Satzart "kvx3" of the KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei -- must be usable (captured and transmitted) by the user.

### Notes

For handling sonstige Kostentraeger in ADT billing, there are **no nationwide uniform regulations**. For example, there are special Sonstige Kostentraeger that may not be billed nationwide but only within a particular Kassenaerztliche Vereinigung.

General billing rules for a Sonstiger Kostentraeger are defined in the respective Kostentraeger-Stammsatz. Each Kassenaerztliche Vereinigung additionally defines its required billing information in a KV-Spezifika-Stammdatei (SDKV).
