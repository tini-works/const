### Requirement

The software ensures that the co-payment exemption for a patient is deleted system-side no later than after a year change has occurred.

### Rationale

Insured persons of statutory health insurers must participate in their health expenses in the form of co-payments. When co-payments reach the personal burden limit, a co-payment exemption is granted for the current year. This must be noted by the physician on the corresponding prescription forms.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If a patient has provided proof of co-payment exemption and this is managed within the PVS as "gebührenfrei" (fee-exempt), the designation "gebührenfrei" is deleted system-side for the patient no later than after a year change has occurred.

### Condition

Excluded from implementation of this requirement are software systems without APK.
