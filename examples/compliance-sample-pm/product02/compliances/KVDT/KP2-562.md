### Requirement

In the case of a referral for in-vitro-diagnostic Auftragsleistungen via Muster 10 or a Laborüberweisung via Muster 10a, the Ausstellungsdatum must be transferred in field 4102 in the Abrechnungsdatensatz as part of the billing.

### Rationale

For temporal assignment of the treatment case in labor clearing, it is necessary that the Ausstellungsdatum (FK 4102) is transferred for Muster 10 and Muster 10A.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software prompts the user to capture the Ausstellungsdatum from the respective Muster for in-vitro-Diagnostik orders.
2. The software must validate during entry that the Ausstellungsdatum is not later than the system date.
   a) If the Ausstellungsdatum is later than the system date, the software rejects the entry with an error message.
3. The software transfers the Ausstellungsdatum in field 4102 as part of the billing.

### Notes

When transmitting a laboratory order via LDT 3, the value from Feldkennung 8213 (Timestamp_Erstellung_Untersuchungsanforderung) in Objekt Auftragsinformation can be used as the Ausstellungsdatum (FK 4102 in KVDT).
