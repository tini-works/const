### Requirement

The software must support the reception of an eEB from health insurers according to the regulations of the Technische Anlage eEB [TA_eEB].

### Rationale

The legislator has established in § 291 Absatz 9 SGB V that an insured person who cannot present an electronic Gesundheitskarte at the first physician-patient contact in a quarter may alternatively request proof of benefit entitlement via one of the services offered by their health insurer through their app.

The contractual partners of the Bundesmantelvertrag-Aerzte (BMV-Ae) have adopted corresponding regulations enabling the transmission of the Ersatzbescheinigung in electronic form -- in short, the electronic Ersatzbescheinigung (eEB). The general provisions are contained in Anlagen 4a and 4b BMV-Ae.

The procedure provides that insured persons can request the transmission of insured person data (per § 291a Abs. 2 and 3 SGB V) via their health insurer's app to a selected practice. The health insurers transmit the data as a (FHIR) dataset on demand directly via KIM (Kommunikation im Medizinwesen) to the selected medical practice.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software provides the user with the functions according to the Technische Anlage eEB [TA_eEB].

### Notes

The regulations according to the Technische Anlage eEB can be deployed immediately and must be available in medical practices **no later than July 2025**.
