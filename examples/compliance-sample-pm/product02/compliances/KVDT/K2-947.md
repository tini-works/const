### Requirement

If the software offers the user the option to create daily profiles, the calculation basis for the psychotherapeutic services 30931[G-alpha], 30932[G-alpha], 35140[G-alpha], 35141[G-alpha], 35150[G-alpha], 35151[G-alpha], 35152[G-alpha], 35401[G-alpha], 35402[G-alpha], 35405[G-alpha], 35411[G-alpha], 35412[G-alpha], 35415[G-alpha], 35421[G-alpha], 35422[G-alpha], 35425[G-alpha], 35431[G-alpha], 35432[G-alpha], and 35435[G-alpha] according to Annex 3 of the EBM must be not the examination time (in minutes) but the calculation time (in minutes) per Annex 3 of the EBM.

### Rationale

This requirement results from the specifications of Annex 3 of the EBM for the affected psychotherapeutic services (cf. Resolution of the Valuation Committee in its 439th session on 19 June 2019 to amend the EBM effective 1 July 2019).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software uses the calculation time (in minutes) per Annex 3 of the EBM as the calculation basis (not the examination time) when creating a daily profile for GOPs 30931[G-alpha], 30932[G-alpha], 35140[G-alpha], 35141[G-alpha], 35150[G-alpha], 35151[G-alpha], 35152[G-alpha], 35401[G-alpha], 35402[G-alpha], 35405[G-alpha], 35411[G-alpha], 35412[G-alpha], 35415[G-alpha], 35421[G-alpha], 35422[G-alpha], 35425[G-alpha], 35431[G-alpha], 35432[G-alpha], and 35435[G-alpha].

### Notes

For the creation of a quarterly profile, the examination time for the above-mentioned GOPs must be used as the calculation basis in accordance with regulations. In the EBM master file, both the examination time in element `//pruefzeit/@V` and the calculation time in element `//zeitbedarf_liste/zeit/@V` are available.
