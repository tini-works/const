## P2-70 — Uniqueness of LANR per (N)BSNR

### Requirement

The software must ensure that a LANR per (N)BSNR in the ADT, SADT, and KADT billing is transmitted only once.

### Rationale

If a LANR per (N)BSNR is transmitted multiple times in the billing, e.g. with different physician names, this can lead to rejections of the billing data by the KVen.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must ensure that in the ADT, SADT, and KADT billing, in the field pairs 0201/0212 of the besa data record, the LANR (FK 0212) occurs only once per BSNR (FK 0201).

### Notes

This requirement only governs the transmission of LANRs in the besa data record of the ADT, SADT, and KADT billing and does not specify any requirements for master data maintenance in the software.

It is therefore still permissible for e.g. training assistants to be assigned to a responsible physician in master data maintenance, so that services provided by the training assistant can be attributed to the responsible physician in the billing.
