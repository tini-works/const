## KP20-031 — Recording of Device Type and Manufacturer for pnSD/uu

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | KP20-031 |
| **Type** | Conditional Mandatory |
| **Source** | KVDT v6.06 |
| **Section** | 2.5.6 Abfrage zur Patientennahen Sofortdiagnostik (pnSD) mittels Verwendung von unit-use-Reagenzien (uu) |
| **Status** | TBC |
| **Goals** | — |
| **Verification Method** | — |
| **Matched by** | — |
| **Refer** | [KP20-030](KP20-030.md) |

### Requirement

If the use of pnSD/uu is confirmed under [KP20-030](KP20-030.md), the recording of at least one device type and manufacturer must be enforced. In principle, the specification of multiple devices must also be supported.

The data must be permanently stored and editable.
