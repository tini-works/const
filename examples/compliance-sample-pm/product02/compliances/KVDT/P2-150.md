## P2-150 — Legitimation of Services in the Current Quarter via Read-in Date

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **ID** | P2-150 |
| **Type** | Mandatory |
| **Source** | KVDT v6.06 |
| **Section** | 2.2.1.6 Einlesedatum |
| **Status** | TBC |
| **Goals** | — |
| **Verification Method** | — |
| **Matched by** | — |
| **Refer** | [P2-535](P2-535.md), [P2-530](P2-530.md), [P2-540](P2-540.md) |

### Requirement

If multiple physician-patient contacts occur in a quarter and no change of the Besondere Personengruppe according to [P2-535](P2-535.md) or health insurer change according to [P2-530](P2-530.md) or insurance type change according to [P2-540](P2-540.md) is present, the software must automatically update the last read-in day of the insurance card in the quarter (FK 4109).

### Rationale

To identify whether an electronic Gesundheitskarte was presented and read during treatment as proof of entitlement pursuant to § 13 BMV-Ä, the read-in date must be transmitted within the scope of billing.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If in the current quarter services for the same insured person are provided by the same physician, no change of Besondere Personengruppe according to [P2-535](P2-535.md) or health insurer change according to [P2-530](P2-530.md) or insurance type change according to [P2-540](P2-540.md) exists, and **no** read-in date for record types 010x is present, then the software must fill the read-in date from reading the insurance card in the current quarter into FK 4109 of all record types of the ADT data package "010x" and transmit it.
2. If in the current quarter services for the same insured person are provided by the same physician, no change of Besondere Personengruppe according to [P2-535](P2-535.md) or health insurer change according to [P2-530](P2-530.md) or insurance type change according to [P2-540](P2-540.md) exists, and **a** read-in date for record types 010x is already present, then the software must overwrite FK 4109 for all record types of the ADT data package "010x" with the new read-in date from reading the insurance card in the current quarter and transmit it.
3. The read-in date (FK 4109) of ADT data packages "010x" from previous quarters must not be adjusted by the software.

### Notes

1. Please also note the specifications regarding Besondere Personengruppe ([P2-535](P2-535.md)), health insurer change ([P2-530](P2-530.md)), and insurance type change ([P2-540](P2-540.md)), as well as the specifications for modification of official insurance card data under Section 2.3.5.
2. The proof of insurance is provided under the above conditions with a read-in date generated in the current quarter. The "last read-in day of the insurance card in the quarter" thereby automatically legitimizes all services in the current quarter.
