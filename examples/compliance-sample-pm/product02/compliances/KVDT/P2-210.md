### Requirement

Preconditions:
- IK_v is an IK from the insurance card and present under element `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/@V` in the KT-Stammdatei
- LQ is the service quarter
- The KV-Bereich of the respective user is not listed as content of `/kostentraeger/unz_kv_geltungsbereich_liste/unz_kv_geltungsbereich/@V` (not a disallowed KV-Geltungsbereich)
- KTAB_y is a KT-Abrechnungsbereich assignable to the patient

**If** LQ lies within the validity period of `/kostentraeger/gueltigkeit/@V` **and** `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/gueltigkeit/@V` **and** `/kostentraeger/kt_abrechnungsbereich_liste/kt_abrechnungsbereich/gueltigkeit/@V`, **then**:

1. The cost unit identified via IK is billable with KTAB_y.
2. IK_v must be used as the unique key for identifying the cost unit.
3. For ADT billing, the corresponding **Abrechnungs-VKNR** (`kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/@V`) must be derived from the IK of the insurance card via the KBV KT-Stammdatei, under which the ADT billing (FK 4104 of the ADT data package) is performed.
4. IK_v must be stored **patient-related**.
5. The "Kassenname zur Bedruckung" (`/kostentraeger/kt_abrechnungsbereich_liste/kt_abrechnungsbereich/bedruckungsname/@V`) derived from the KT-Stammdatei must be used for the issuance of contractual physician forms.

### Notes

The Kostenträgername from the insurance card, transmitted under FK 4134 in the ADT billing, **must not** be used for issuance of contractual physician forms!
See also function [P2-136](P2-136.md) "Name des Kostenträgers von der Versichertenkarte" **and** function [P2-220](P2-220.md) (CASE 2 - Aufnehmender Kostenträger, Fusion), item (4).
