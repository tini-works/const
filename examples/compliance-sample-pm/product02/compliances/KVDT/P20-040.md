### Requirement

Based on the materials used, a tailored selection of potentially relevant RV analytes must be made available, which are individually performed at the facility.

The selection of analyte-GOP combinations must be permanently stored and editable.

### Notes

If a table view is chosen for the analyte selection, the requirement for the query of RV materials per [P20-021](P20-021.md) can, for example, be implemented by offering a material filter and directing the user's attention to this filter via the user interface.
