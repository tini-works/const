### Requirement

For simultaneous procedures, according to EBM specifications, only the highest-valued service is to be billed. Additional procedures are accounted for through the GSNZ (Gesamt-Schnitt-Naht-Zeit / Total Suture Time) (FK 5037) and through billing of time surcharges.

The PVS may offer the physician **as support** the highest-valued service for billing. The time surcharges are then calculated from the difference between the GSNZ and the actual SNZ of the main procedure.

For billing, **both pieces of information** must be transmitted: the GSNZ and the surcharge service(s).
