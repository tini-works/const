### Requirement

Billing software that can be used in both outpatient and inpatient areas must ensure that patient master data in the inpatient and outpatient areas are processed separately from one another.

### Rationale

When billing in the inpatient area is performed, the Hauptkassen-IK (main insurance IK) of the health insurer must always be used for direct billing with the health insurer, not the IK read from the insurance card. For KV billing in the outpatient area, the IK interpreted according to "Mappingtabelle KVK" [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Mapping_KVK] from the insurance card must be used.

The background of this requirement is the distinction in billing domains with the health insurer (e.g. for inpatient services and outpatient services at hospitals pursuant to § 115b SGB V etc.) and the KV billing.

### Example

When a patient is treated in the inpatient area and their insurance card is read there, and the data is processed in the billing software, but the patient is later treated in the outpatient area, the insurance card must be re-read and the data must be processed separately.
