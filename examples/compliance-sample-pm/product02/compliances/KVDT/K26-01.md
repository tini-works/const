### Requirement

To implement the electronic referral using digital Form 6 in doctor-to-doctor communication, all requirements concerning digital Form 6 in doctor-to-doctor communication are to be realized by the software, provided the software manufacturer decides on voluntary implementation.

### Rationale

The implementation of digital Form 6 in doctor-to-doctor communication results from the combination of multiple requirements. Requirement K26-01 bundles these.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must fulfill the following requirements:
