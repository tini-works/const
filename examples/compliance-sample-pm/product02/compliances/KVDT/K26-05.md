### Requirement

The software may only use the secure transmission channel KIM for the transmission of digital Form 6 in doctor-to-doctor communication.

### Rationale

In the BMV-Ae, for the transmission of digital forms, only transmission channels that fulfill the requirements of section 3 number 1 of Annex 2b of the BMV-Ae are permissible. Therefore, the software may only integrate and offer such secure transmission channels to the contract physician for use.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software system must use the specialized service KIM of the Telematikinfrastruktur for sending and receiving digital Form 6 in PDF/A standard.

### Notes

It is recommended to implement the specification of the KIM application "DiMus" [DiMus] by kv.digital.
