### Requirement

The software must enable the user to mark GOPs with an ASV team number within the framework of service documentation.

### Rationale

The contractual basis is section 6, para. 3 of the ASV-AV in conjunction with section 116b, para. 6, sentence 12 SGB V.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must enable the user to mark GOPs recorded within the framework of service documentation with a defined ASV team number.
2. The software transfers the ASV team number assigned to the GOP with the billing in field FK 5100 (ASV team number of the contract physician).
