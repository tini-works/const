### Requirement

The billing of services for pregnancy terminations is performed exclusively with the following record types:

1. Record type "sad1" is used to bill: outpatient services of the operator.
2. Record type "sad2" is used to bill:
   a) Services of the anesthesiologist who works on referral of the operator.
   b) Services of the gynecologist to whom the operator has referred for follow-up examination.
3. Record type "sad3" is used to bill: inpatient physician services.
