### Requirement

The software must enable the recording and transmission of "Pseudo treatment cases" with GOP 88194 within the ADT billing for selective contract / mine-physician treatment cases.

### Rationale

A prerequisite for the billing of fee schedule positions 03060, 03062, and 03063 by physicians who participate in a selective contract per section 73b SGB V (HzV contracts) and/or a selective contract per section 140a SGB V, is the verification of all selective contract/mine-physician treatment cases per Preamble No. 11 section 3.1 in the quarter to the Kassenaerztliche Vereinigung using GOP 88194; cf. Einheitlicher Bewertungsmassstab (EBM), Chapter 3.2.1.2, Care area-specific provision, physician-ordered auxiliary services, Point 2.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must enable the user, for each selective contract / mine-physician treatment case that is billed directly with the cost carrier, to record and transfer an additional "Pseudo treatment case" as a further data record 010x within the ADT billing of the current quarter.
