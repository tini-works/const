### Requirement

The software correctly implements the requirements regarding ICD-10-GM application.

### Rationale

Panel physicians and panel psychotherapists are obligated to code according to ICD-10-GM pursuant to § 295, Abs. 1 SGB V, Satz 1, 2, 3.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software implements all required requirements of the requirements catalog "Anforderungskatalog zur Anwendung der ICD-10-GM", cf. [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Anforderungskatalog_ICD-10].
