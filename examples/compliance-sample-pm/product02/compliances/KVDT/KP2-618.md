### Requirement

The software must enable the specification of the "Type of disease" coded per "ICD-10-GM" for GOPen 11302[G-alpha], 11303[G-alpha], 19402[G-alpha], 19421[G-alpha], 19424[G-alpha], 19451[G-alpha], 19452[G-alpha], 19453[G-alpha], 19456[G-alpha], 32901[G-alpha], 32902[G-alpha], 32904[G-alpha], 32906[G-alpha], 32908[G-alpha], 32910[G-alpha], 32911[G-alpha], 32915[G-alpha], 32916[G-alpha], 32917[G-alpha], and 32918[G-alpha].

### Rationale

According to EBM billing provisions for GOPen 11302[G-alpha], 11303[G-alpha], 19402[G-alpha], 19421[G-alpha], 19424[G-alpha], 19451[G-alpha], 19452[G-alpha], 19453[G-alpha], 19456[G-alpha], 32901[G-alpha], 32902[G-alpha], 32904[G-alpha], 32906[G-alpha], 32908[G-alpha], 32910[G-alpha], 32911[G-alpha], 32915[G-alpha], 32916[G-alpha], 32917[G-alpha], and 32918[G-alpha], the "Type of disease coded per ICD-10-GM" is to be specified.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software system must require the user, when capturing the above-mentioned GOPen, to provide a valid "ICD-10-GM code" (distinct from the ICD-10-GM code "Z01.7") with "Diagnosis certainty" within the scope of service documentation.
2. The software transmits with the billing for the above-mentioned services the "ICD-10-GM code" specified by the user in field 6001 and the "Diagnosis certainty" in field 6003.
