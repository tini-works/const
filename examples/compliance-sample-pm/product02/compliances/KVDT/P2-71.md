## P2-71 — Uniqueness of (N)BSNR

| **Refer** | — |

### Requirement

The software must ensure that a (N)BSNR in the ADT, SADT, and KADT billing is transmitted only once in the besa data record.

### Rationale

Since multiple physicians practicing under a (N)BSNR can be listed in the besa data record, the multiple transmission of the (N)BSNR in the besa data record is not necessary.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must ensure that in the ADT, SADT, and KADT billing, a BSNR occurs only once in field code 0201 of the besa data record.
