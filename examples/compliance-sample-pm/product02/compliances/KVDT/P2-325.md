### Requirement

The software must inform the user about the restricted benefit entitlement of recipients of health care services according to §§ 4 and 6 AsylbLG.

### Rationale

§§ 4 and 6 of the Asylbewerberleistungsgesetz (AsylbLG) regulate the scope of health care services for asylum seekers. The scope of health care services for asylum seekers (eligible persons with less than 15 months of residence) and statutory health insurance (GKV) patients differs and should be observed by the user when providing services.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. If an eGK with Besondere Personengruppe "09" is read (after mapping according to "Technische Anlage zur Anlage 4a" [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Mapping_KVK]), the software must immediately alert the user to observe the restricted benefit entitlement of recipients of health care services according to §§ 4 and 6 AsylbLG.
2. This also applies when the data is transferred from a mobile card terminal into a PVS.
3. The user should also be informed of corresponding cases during manual entry analogous to (1).
4. The user must be able to deactivate the function; by default this function should be activated.
5. The notice must not interrupt the physician's workflow.
