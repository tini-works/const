### Requirement

The software must provide the user with manual population of the TSS fields in the billing.

### Rationale

Due to the separate remuneration for treatments resulting from Terminvermittlungen, the billed services are to be marked by Schein and the relevant information is to be transmitted per the defined requirements.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software ensures that the user can manually populate KVDT-Feldkennungen 4103, 4114 and 4115.
