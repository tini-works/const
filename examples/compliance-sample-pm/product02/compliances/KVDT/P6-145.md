### Requirement

1. Only the pseudo subgroups listed under field identifier 4239 of the record type "kvx2" may be used in the context of ADT billing.
2. Only the billing areas listed under field identifier 4122 of the record type "kvx2" may be used with the corresponding pseudo subgroup in the context of ADT billing.
