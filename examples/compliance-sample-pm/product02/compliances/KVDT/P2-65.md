### Requirement

The software must provide the user with the ability to use a pseudo physician number.

### Rationale

The contractual basis of this requirement is the KBV guideline pursuant to § 75 Absatz 7 SGB V on the assignment of physician, practice, and practice network numbers in conjunction with Anlage 6 BMV-Ä (Vertrag über den Datenaustausch), § 4 "Art und Inhalt der Daten der Weiteren Leistungserbringer".

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software must provide the user with the ability to capture and store the pseudo physician number "999999900".
2. In the context of ADT billing, the software must be capable of transmitting the pseudo physician number in the fields 4241, 4242, and 5099.
3. The software must be able to use the pseudo physician number when issuing panel physician forms in these cases.

### Notes

Additionally, requirement [P2-40](P2-40.md) must be observed.
