## KP2-405 — Billing of Services with Electronic Ersatzbescheinigung (eEB) as Proof of Insurance

| **Verification Method** | — |
| **Matched by** | — |
| **Refer** | — |

### Requirement

When adopting insured person data from an eEB, the software must transfer the value 1 into the field "eEB vorhanden" (FK 4112).

### Rationale

The Feldkennung 4112 in the billing serves to verify the traceability of the eEB as the source of insured person data.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. When insured person data are adopted from an eEB, the software marks the respective billing data record with the value 1 in FK 4112.
2. When insured person data are not adopted from an eEB, the software must ensure that FK 4112 is not transferred in the respective billing data record.
