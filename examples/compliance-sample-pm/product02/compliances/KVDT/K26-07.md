### Requirement

The software must be able to read the data from the digital Form 6 received per [K26-06](K26-06.md) in doctor-to-doctor communication according to the provisions of the technical handbook for digital preprints (see: [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Technisches_Handbuch_DiMus]).

### Rationale

To support the physician in their work, the system must be able to correctly read the contents from the received form for further use.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The system reads the contents from the received digital referral according to the provisions of the technical handbook for digital preprints [KBV_ITA_VGEX_Technisches_Handbuch_DiMus]. This includes in particular:
   a) The qualified electronic signature of the form is verified according to Chapter "2.7 Qualifizierte elektronische Signatur."
   b) All required PDF form fields are read according to the provisions of Chapter "2.10 Formularfelder" and Chapter "3.1.2 Formularfelder digitales Muster 6" of the document.
   c) All required metadata are read according to the provisions of Chapter "2.9 Metadaten" and Chapter "3.1.1 Metadaten digitales Muster 6."
   d) The notes on file naming are observed according to Chapter "2.5 Dateinamen" of the document.
