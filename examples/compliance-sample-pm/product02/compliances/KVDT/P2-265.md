### Requirement

The software must verify the validity of a cost unit in the KV-Bereich relevant to the medical practice.

### Rationale

This requirement results from regulations through regional contracts between a cost unit and the Kassenaerztliche Vereinigung(en).

Due to regional contracts, a cost unit may only be billed in one (or several) KV-Bereich(en), and not generally billed nationwide.

For these cost units, the "not permitted" KV-Geltungsbereich(e) are explicitly specified in the element `////unz_kv_geltungsbereich` of the Kostentraeger-Stammdatei. This occurs primarily in the area of Sonstige Kostentraeger (SKT).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The software checks the following conditions:
   a) IK_v is an IK from the insurance card and present under element `/kostentraeger/ik_liste/ik/@V` in the KT-Stammdatei.
   - **If** the KV-Bereich relevant to the medical practice is listed under `/kostentraeger/unz_kv_geltungsbereich_liste/unz_kv_geltungsbereich/@V`, **then**:
2. A system-generated **error message** is issued that billing with the cost unit listed in the disallowed KV-Bereich under `kostentraeger/unz_kv_geltungsbereich_liste/ unz_kv_geltungsbereich/@V` is not permitted.
3. The software does not support direct further processing for the purpose of ADT billing at the expense of this cost unit.
4. The software does not support the issuance of contractual physician forms or BFB forms at the expense of this cost unit.
