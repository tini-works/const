### Requirement

The guideline volumes (Richtgroessen) are stored in the prescribing software. Differences in guideline volumes between KV regions must be observed and updated upon new software activation -- depending on the contract -- by the manufacturer/vendor or by the physician.

The corresponding information is provided by the contractual partners at the state level. The PVS vendor maintains an interface for this purpose.
