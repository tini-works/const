### Requirement

For a medicinal product for which one or more G-BA resolutions pursuant to § 35a SGB V exist, the contents must be retrievable.

### Rationale

If one or more resolutions pursuant to § 35a SGB V exist or are available, the contents of the resolutions must be able to be called up immediately for information purposes.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must enable product-specific retrieval of the contents of all resolutions (contents of one or multiple resolutions (Merkmal 061)), provided that at least one G-BA resolution pursuant to § 35a SGB V (Merkmal 103) exists for the medicinal product.
2. The prescribing software must make the resolution overview pursuant to [**P3-260**](P3-260.md) available with one action at the latest after selecting the medicinal product.
