### Requirement

The system must ensure that free-text prescriptions can be printed on a paper-based prescription.

### Rationale

The prescribing of medicinal products and other products included in the pharmaceutical supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V is carried out in accordance with the legal requirements. When printing, all prescription-relevant information must be printed on the paper form.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must ensure that the contents of the free-text prescriptions are printed.
2. The prescribing software must print the following information when the corresponding fields have been filled in or the indicators have been set by the contract physician:
   - Replacement prescription (per [**P3-630**](P3-630.md)) in the first line
   - "Verordnung nach § 27a SGB V" (prescription in the context of artificial fertilization)
   - Dosage ([**P3-625**](P3-625.md))
   - Dispensing instructions
