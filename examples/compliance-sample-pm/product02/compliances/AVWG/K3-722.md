### Requirement

The system must ensure that active ingredient prescriptions can be printed on a paper-based prescription when the function is offered.

### Rationale

The prescribing of medicinal products and other products included in the pharmaceutical supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V is carried out in accordance with the legal requirements. When printing a pharmaceutical prescription form, all prescription-relevant information must be printed.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must print the following information for an active ingredient prescription in the specified order:
   - Active ingredient
   - Strength with unit
   - Dosage form
   - Pack size quantity and unit or standard size (if both the pack size and the standard size have been entered, the pack size (quantity and unit) is to be printed)
2. The prescribing software must print the following information when the corresponding fields have been filled in or the indicators have been set by the contract physician:
   - Replacement prescription (per [**P3-630**](P3-630.md)) in the first line
   - "Verordnung nach § 27a SGB V" (prescription in the context of artificial fertilization)
   - Dosage ([**P3-625**](P3-625.md))
   - Dispensing instructions

**Condition:** Implementation of function [**O3-622**](O3-622.md) Wirkstoffverordnung (active ingredient prescription).
