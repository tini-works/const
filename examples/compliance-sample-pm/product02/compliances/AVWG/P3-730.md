### Requirement

The form selection must be possible both before and after the selection of the medicinal products and other products included in the pharmaceutical supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V to be prescribed.

Form selection for paper-based prescriptions refers in particular to the pharmaceutical prescription form (Arzneiverordnungsblatt), the BtM prescription, the T-prescription, or the private prescription. For electronic prescriptions, the underlying form selection per [**P3-731**](P3-731.md) is to be mapped.

### Rationale

The form selection is intended to assist the user in selecting the correct prescription form.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. For a statutorily insured patient, the default setting must be the prescription of medicinal products at the expense of the statutory health insurance. This means:
   - For electronic prescriptions per [**P3-710**](P3-710.md), the value "GKV" must be specified in element type in the profile KBV_PR_FOR_COVERAGE.
   - For paper-based prescriptions per [P3-720](P3-720.md), the pharmaceutical prescription form Muster 16 must be used.
2. When prescribing narcotics (Merkmal 012), the BtM prescription form must be used by default.
3. When prescribing teratogenic medicines (Merkmal 013), the T-prescription form must be used.
4. Deviating from acceptance criterion 1, the default for statutorily insured patients must be a private prescription for the following Merkmal values:
   - Merkmale:
     - Negative list (Merkmal 050)
     - Lifestyle medicines (Merkmal 051)
     - Medicines with prescribing exclusion per AM-RL Annex III (Merkmal 056)
     - Pharmacy-only OTC medicines (OTC-Arzneimittel) for adolescents over 12 years without developmental disorders and adults
     - OTX medicines (Merkmal 053)
       1. The prescribing software may offer the configuration option that when displaying the full advisory text of Annex I of the AM-RL, the pre-selection defaults to acceptance criterion 1.
     - Non-prescribable medical devices per requirement [P2-100](P2-100.md) Chapter 2.2.2.2 Mindestangaben fuer sonstige Medizinprodukte
   - For electronic prescriptions per [P3-710](P3-710.md), the value "SEL" must be specified in element type in the profile KBV_PR_FOR_COVERAGE.
   - For paper-based prescriptions per [P3-720](P3-720.md), a private prescription must be used.
5. The prescribing software must allow at any time a change of the "prescription form" per acceptance criteria 1, 2, and 4 without losing the selected products.
6. The prescribing software must, when prescribing from the patient history, adopt the "prescription selection" of the prescription to be repeated.
   - The contract physician may adjust the "prescription selection" setting in compliance with acceptance criteria 1 to 4.
7. Additionally, the system may offer configuration options deviating from the above-mentioned default settings for the prescription form (for example related to a product or ATC code).
