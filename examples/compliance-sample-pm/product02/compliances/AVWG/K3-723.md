### Requirement

The system must ensure that a compound prescription can be printed on a prescription form when the function is offered.

### Rationale

The prescribing of medicinal products and other products included in the pharmaceutical supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V is carried out in accordance with the legal requirements. When printing a pharmaceutical prescription form, all prescription-relevant information must be printed.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must ensure that only exactly one compound prescription is contained on a paper form.
2. The prescribing software must print the following information for a compound prescription in the specified order:
   - Compound name, if specified
   - Total quantity and unit
   - Dosage form
   - Components, each with quantity, unit, and dosage form
   - Container/primary packaging material, if specified
   - Preparation instructions
   - Usage instructions

**Condition:** Implementation of function [**O3-623**](O3-623.md) Rezepturverordnung (compound prescription).
