### Requirement

The contents of the machine-readable version of the resolutions pursuant to § 35a Absatz 3a SGB V described below are to be integrated into the prescribing software.

### Rationale

The contents described below of the machine-readable version published by the G-BA pursuant to the legal regulation referenced in § 73 Absatz 9 SGB V (Elektronische Arzneimittelinformationen-Verordnung -- EAMIV) regarding the resolutions on the assessment of the additional benefit of medicinal products pursuant to § 35a Absatz 3 SGB V are to be integrated into the prescribing software.

Further details on the content, structural design, scope, and data format are set out in the procedural rules of the G-BA and in the specification of the files for a machine-readable version of the resolutions pursuant to § 35a Absatz 3 SGB V (source: G-BA internet pages).

### Acceptance Criteria

1. The prescribing software must fully integrate the Merkmale 101 to 170 specified in [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) for the implementation of the machine-readable version of the G-BA resolutions pursuant to § 35a SGB V.
2. The contents of these Merkmale must be adopted completely and unchanged from the machine-readable version of the resolutions pursuant to § 35a Absatz 3a SGB V.
3. The assignment of the contents of the machine-readable version to the contents of the drug master data must be performed in accordance with the specifications from Chapter 2.1 of the specification of the file for a machine-readable version of the resolutions pursuant to § 35a SGB V of the G-BA.
