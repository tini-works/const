### Requirement

The prescribing software must integrate the information of the regional collective-contractual pharmaceutical agreements (Arzneimittelvereinbarungen) pursuant to § 84 Paragraph 1 SGB V into the application via the interface defined in the following.

The information, regulations, and markings ([**P3-120**](P3-120.md), [**P3-121**](P3-121.md), [**P3-130**](P3-130.md), and [**P3-610**](P3-610.md)) are to be represented in the manner described in the interface specification.

### Rationale

It must be ensured that information on pharmaceutical agreements (ARV) (framework agreements, target agreements) is available.

These are made available in a suitable form by the contractual partners at the federal/state level (Kassenaerztliche Bundesvereinigung (KBV), GKV-Spitzenverband (GKV-SV), Kassenaerztliche Vereinigungen, health insurance fund associations, health insurance funds).

The interface serves to transmit the content of the regional, collective-contractual pharmaceutical agreements in the respectively current version.

The required interface is defined in the data set description ARV (ftp://ftp.kbv.de/ita-update/Stammdateien/).

If adjustments or additions to the ARV interface become necessary due to regional agreements that cannot be mapped with the existing interface specification, these are to be made available by the regional contractual partners in the form of technically specified extensions. These will be incorporated into the ARV interface by the KBV after content and technical review.
