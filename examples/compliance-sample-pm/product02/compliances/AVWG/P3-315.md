### Requirement

Search results by trade name or manufacturer/provider are displayed as lists sorted alphabetically by trade name. They can also be displayed sorted according to the criteria of [**P3-320**](P3-320.md).

When the search result is displayed as a list of products, upon selecting a product all associated packages with the attributes according [**P3-120**](P3-120.md) must be shown.
