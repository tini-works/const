### Requirement

The following substitution suggestions for a selected medicinal product are to be presented upon individual request with only one action through the prescription software in the form of selection lists in accordance with mandatory function [**P3-320**](P3-320.md) or [**P3-325**](P3-325.md):

- Active-substance-equivalent, lower-priced medicinal products suitable for substitution (price comparison): Selection and display follow the criteria of [**P3-325**](P3-325.md), with the proviso that only those medicinal products are shown that are lower-priced than the selected medicinal product. Simultaneous display of identically priced and higher-priced products is also permissible.
- Substitution suggestions from medicinal product agreements pursuant to § 84 Absatz 1 SGB V: Selection follows the criteria of the respective medicinal product agreement (cf. [**P3-610**](P3-610.md)). Display follows [**P3-320**](P3-320.md) unless the respective medicinal product agreement provides otherwise.
- Discount medicinal products pursuant to § 130a Absatz 8 SGB V that are suitable for substitution of the original medicinal product: Selection and display follow the criteria of [**P3-325**](P3-325.md), with the proviso that only those medicinal products are shown for which a discount agreement designation (090) exists.

Displaying substitution suggestions without prior individual request or suggestions other than the listed substitution suggestions is impermissible.
