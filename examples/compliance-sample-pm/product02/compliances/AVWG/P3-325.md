### Requirement

For every medicinal product and other product included in the drug supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V, a price comparison search must be offered. The price comparison must include medicinal products with:

- The same active substance, or for combination medicinal products the same active substance combination
- The same potency (taking into account equivalences)
- The same or interchangeable dosage form according to AM-RL Annex VII Part A
- The same N-designation, or where no N-designation exists the same package size

The result of the comparison search must be displayed sorted in ascending order by AVP -- for medicinal products according to AMPreisV or for non-prescription medicinal products pursuant to § 129 Absatz 5a SGB V (i.e. the lowest-priced item at the top).

The list must be positioned at the beginning. Only the starting position of the selection list may be visualised. If multiple products are identically priced, the entire range should be marked. Highlighting (e.g. colour changes, underlines, etc.) that goes beyond the markings regulated in the requirements catalogue, as well as the focus or cursor position, must not suggest a pre-selection. The original medicinal product underlying the price comparison may be visually marked. The selection must be made without influence.
