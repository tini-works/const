### Requirement

The pharmaceutical master data consist of price and product information pursuant to § 131 SGB V, covering all products included in the pharmaceutical care of statutory health insurance pursuant to § 31 SGB V (hereinafter referred to as the Price and Product Directory).

The pharmaceutical master data must contain the Merkmale of mandatory function [**P2-110**](P2-110.md) and conditional mandatory function [**K2-120**](K2-120.md). They must correspond to the information published by the pharmaceutical entrepreneur pursuant to § 131 Paragraph 4 SGB V in connection with the framework agreement concluded for this purpose pursuant to § 131 SGB V.

The regulations of the directive pursuant to § 92 Paragraph 1 Sentence 2 Number 6 SGB V -- Arzneimittel-Richtlinie (AM-RL) of the G-BA are to be represented. The texts of the AM-RL and Annexes I through VIIa are to be provided as PDF files.

The contents of the AM-RL:

- Anlage I (OTC overview),
- Anlage II (Lifestyle pharmaceuticals),
- Anlage III (Overview of prescribing restrictions and exclusions),
- Anlage IV (Therapy guidance),
- Anlage V (Overview of prescribable medical devices),
- Anlage VII (Aut idem), and
- the pharmaceutical overview for the so-called negative list

are to be represented in a context-dependent and pharmaceutical- or package-related manner, as described in the following mandatory functions. This also applies to imported pharmaceuticals.

The prescribing software always represents, on a reference-date basis, the price and product data corresponding to the respectively published Price and Product Directory for all products. The data status at the time of prescribing must be identifiable.

The contents of the machine-readable version of the resolutions pursuant to § 35a Paragraph 3a SGB V are an integral part of the pharmaceutical master data. The corresponding machine-readable version is to be obtained from the internet pages of the G-BA. The prescribing software must represent the respectively current status of the machine-readable version.

### Notes

The term "Arzneimittelstammdaten" (pharmaceutical master data) also includes the master data for other products included in pharmaceutical care pursuant to § 31 SGB V.
