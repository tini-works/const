### Requirement

Subsequent editing of master data pursuant to [**P2-100**](P2-100.md) by the contract physician is not permitted. However, the physician may be given the option to assign additional short names, designations, comments, and keyboard shortcuts for in-house pharmacy entries that facilitate searching in the in-house pharmacy or prescribing from the in-house pharmacy. When printing the prescription form, however, the original data from the drug database must be used.
