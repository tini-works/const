### Requirement

Medicinal products that were searched for by neither trade name nor manufacturer name are to be sorted hierarchically for each active substance (the authoritative classification is the ATC classification according to BfArM) and displayed according to the following criteria:

1. If the search result comprises medicinal products with different active substances/active substance combinations: Grouping by active substance/active substance combination (For active substance combinations: first by combination partner A, then by combination partner B, then by combination partner C, etc.)
2. Grouping by dosage forms (taking into account interchangeable dosage forms according to AM-RL Annex VII Part A)
3. Within each grouping, sorted in ascending order by potency (taking into account equivalences) (For active substance combinations: first by potency of combination partner A, then by potency of combination partner B, then by potency of combination partner C, etc.)
4. Within each potency, sorted in ascending order by unit count of the package (Merkmale 006 and 007)
5. Within identical package sizes, sorted in ascending order by AVP according to AMPreisV or § 129 Absatz 5a SGB V for non-prescription medicinal products

If a focus or cursor is automatically placed in the selection list, it must be set to the beginning. Only the starting position of the selection list may be highlighted. If multiple products are identically priced, the entire range is to be highlighted. Highlighting (e.g. colour changes, underlines, etc.) that goes beyond the markings regulated in the requirements catalogue, as well as the focus or cursor position, must not suggest a pre-selection. The selection must be made by the physician without influence.
