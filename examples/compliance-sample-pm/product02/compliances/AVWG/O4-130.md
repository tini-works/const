### Requirement

Additional controlling programs (regarding target fields of target agreements, costs incurred, etc.) are offered. Regional specifics are to be taken into account where required.

The corresponding information is provided by the contractual partners at the state level via the interface designated in [**P1-050**](P1-050.md).

The interface and its contents are to be made available without activation or enabling measures and without delay.
