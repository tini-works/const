### Requirement

It must be possible to display the changes to the Hausapotheke following an automatic update.

### Rationale

An overview of changed product Merkmale after each update of the pharmaceutical master data makes these changes comprehensible at a glance.

### Acceptance Criteria

1. During the update of the pharmaceutical master data or other updates of the prescribing software, no controlled population in the Hausapotheke area may occur, i.e., in particular no products may be added to the Hausapotheke.
2. During the update of the pharmaceutical master data, the data stored in the Hausapotheke per [K2-155](K2-155.md) acceptance criterion 1 must be updated corresponding to the PZN reference. This also applies to structurally stored information per [O3-623](O3-623.md) (compounded prescription).
3. After an update per [**P2-130**](P2-130.md), a change list must be generated. This contains a listing of all PZN-related products that are no longer available after the update (Merkmal 09 = "Not marketable" or "Withdrawn" or the PZN is no longer contained in the pharmaceutical master data). In connection with the change list, the user is shown the following notice text: "The following products are no longer in circulation and must therefore be removed." The user must confirm or initiate the removal of the displayed PZNs. Automatic deletion of PZNs without user confirmation is not permitted. If the user does not confirm the deletion of PZNs, the system must prevent the user from prescribing these PZNs from the Hausapotheke. Note: The system may support the physician during the deletion process by including an alternative product to the product being deleted in the Hausapotheke.
4. The change list additionally contains the changes to the following data of PZN-related products: Merkmal 008 Package size regulation, Merkmal 009 Article status, Merkmal 030 Price, Merkmal 031 Reimbursement amount, Merkmal 034 AVP above/below/equal to fixed amount, Merkmal 036 Additional costs, Merkmal 038 Determination pursuant to § 130b Paragraph 1c SGB V (Confidential reimbursement amount), Merkmal 051 Lifestyle pharmaceutical, Merkmal 052 Conditional lifestyle pharmaceutical, Merkmal 053 OTC pharmaceutical together with 054 -- For OTX pharmaceutical: Texts of the AM-RL Anlage I (One labeling encompasses both changes to OTX Merkmal (053) and changes to AM-RL text (054)), Merkmal 055 Prescribing restrictions of Anlage III of the AM-RL, Merkmal 056 Prescribing exclusions of Anlage III of the AM-RL, Merkmal 057 For pharmaceuticals subject to prescribing restrictions/exclusions of AM-RL Anlage III: Texts of the AM-RL, Merkmal 058 Therapy guidance of the G-BA, Merkmal 059 Date of therapy guidance (date) and reference to therapy guidance, Merkmal 060 Pharmaceutical of AM-RL Anlage VII Part B, Merkmal 061 Resolution on benefit assessment pursuant to § 35a SGB V, Merkmal 093 Practice special feature pursuant to § 130b SGB V, Merkmal 094 Link to practice special feature, Merkmal 212 Price as specified by manufacturer/supplier (for medical devices), Merkmal 411 Price as specified by manufacturer/supplier (for bandages), Merkmal 510 Price as specified by manufacturer/supplier (for test strips), Merkmal 611 Price as specified by manufacturer/supplier (for balanced diets for enteral nutrition).
5. In case a compounded prescription is structurally stored and at least one component of this compounded prescription is no longer available in the current master data after the update per [P2-130](P2-130.md), acceptance criterion 2 applies.
6. Changes to the Merkmale price (030), AVP above/below/equal to fixed amount (034), and article status (009) must be particularly highlighted.
7. The respectively current list must be accessible at any time with a maximum of two additional actions.
8. The respective fields per acceptance criterion 4 are to be displayed only if changes have occurred.

### Notes

Condition: Implementation of requirement [**O2-145**](O2-145.md).
