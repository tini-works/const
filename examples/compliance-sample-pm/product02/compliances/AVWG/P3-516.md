### Requirement

When displaying search results or substitution suggestions at the level of finished medicinal product packages (PZN level), the panel physician must be given the ability through a single action to limit the displayed results to the discounted medicinal products for the respective patient. If no discounted medicinal products are available in the search results, a corresponding notice must be provided and the search results must remain unchanged.

In the user settings of the prescription software, an option must be provided that allows the panel physician to set the default restriction of displayed search results to discounted medicinal products. If no discounted medicinal product is available for the respective search parameters, the panel physician must be given a corresponding notice and the search results must be displayed without the restriction to discounted medicinal products.
