### Requirement

Selection lists for prescribing finished medicinal products and other products included in the drug supply pursuant to § 31 SGB V have a uniform layout without highlighting.

If deviating arrangements have been agreed through regional contracts between health insurance funds and Associations of Statutory Health Insurance Physicians, highlighting may be applied when the contract physician is informed in the prescribing context about the reasons for the highlighting and the legal basis, and the prescribing software provides corresponding information.

Marking of products in selection lists/search results that are part of an in-house pharmacy is also permissible through highlighting.
