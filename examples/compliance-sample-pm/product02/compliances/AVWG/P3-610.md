### Requirement

Measures for implementing supply and cost-effectiveness objectives as well as pharmaceutical classifications (DDD, medication catalogues, etc.) agreed upon by the contractual partners at the state level in agreements pursuant to § 84 Absatz 1 SGB V must be mapped in the prescribing software.

Where specific regulations relating to individual active substances etc. are found, the affected pharmaceuticals must be annotated with corresponding information. The display is KV-region-specific and, where applicable, specialist-group-specific, so that the physician only receives the information relevant to them.

The corresponding information is provided by the contractual partners at the state level via the interface referred to in [**P1-050**](P1-050.md).

The interface and its content are to be made available without activation or unlocking. Measures for activation are to be provided without delay.
