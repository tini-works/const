### Requirement

An alternative sorting or filtering of the selection list according to the following criteria (also combined) must be supported:

- Price (AVP according to AMPreisV or § 129 Absatz 5a SGB V for non-prescription medicinal products)
- N-designation
- Potency (taking into account equivalences)
- Co-payment
- Products with the designation discount agreement (090)
